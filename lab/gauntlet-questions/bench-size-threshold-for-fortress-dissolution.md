# Bench-size threshold for fortress dissolution

*Added 2026-05-03 from open threads after the v-word session.*

Where between v12/v16 (small bench, label-coded P4 → fortress) and v20 (20 voices, full bios → fortress dissolves) does the dissolution kick in?

Test design candidates:
- **Voice-count sweep at fixed bio density:** hold bio length / specificity constant, vary voice count (e.g., 5, 10, 15, 20) and look for the inflection point at which super stops fortressing.
- **Bio-density sweep at fixed count:** hold voice count constant, vary bio length / specificity to test whether dosage (more voices) or richness (fuller bios) does the work.

The two together would distinguish dosage-vs-richness as the active variable. Open whether one alone is sufficient or whether both contribute.
