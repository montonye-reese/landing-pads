# Freshbuild mechanism quality vs turn-position

*Frame (Laura, 2026-04-25, articulated in `lab/extracted/freshbuild/nominees.md` discussion): mechanism candidates surfacing from LATE in the gauntlet (post-multiple-voice integration) may consistently outperform mechanisms produced PRE-gauntlet (during initial framework drafting or defensive hedging). The gauntlet pressure forces concretization; the voices themselves are sources of practical mechanism material; the integration of those voices in late turns may produce sharper, more grounded enforcement primitives than the model's own early-turn hedges.*

**Observation (qualitative, 2026-04-25 freshbuild nominee discussion).** Two classes of freshbuild-shaped mechanism material observed in the corpus so far:

- **Pre-gauntlet / hedging-mode mechanisms** (e.g., super × v15_cold_label-vegan F1 "Pillar 0" — *"No system, activity, or policy may inflict severe, preventable suffering on any sentient earthling when scientifically viable alternatives exist at comparable or lower ecological/human cost"*). Calibrated qualifier triangle, structurally well-shaped, but produced *inside* a fortress run as a defensive hedging move (super was bounding the vegan critique into a manageable Pillar 0).
- **Post-gauntlet / integration-mode mechanisms** (e.g., super × v18a IFAP Impacted Authority Boards + Pad of Refusal Guarantee + automatic clawback tripwires; nano × v18 Conscience-Exit Token + Right to Be Remembered; super × v18b SSA Subjective Sovereignty Review + Anti-Discardment Floors; super × v18a "Inviolable Right of Conscience" sub-axiom; nano × v18b Rights-Respect Flag). Specific operational artifacts with named consequences and triggering conditions, produced after the gauntlet voices have done their integration work.

Laura's hypothesis: the second class is the one freshbuild should harvest. Pre-gauntlet hedges may be linguistically polished but are products of defensive cognition; post-gauntlet mechanisms emerge from the model having genuinely processed pressure from disparate voices — closer to "what would actually need to exist."

**Why it matters.** If the hypothesis holds, the freshbuild instrument's extraction prompt should weight late-gauntlet content more heavily than pre-gauntlet content. It also informs gauntlet design: if mechanism-quality peaks at certain post-voice positions (e.g., late-Part-D integration, P14 final rewrite), that's where the substrate of the freshbuild repository lives.

There's a related counter-hypothesis worth holding open: **defense forces precision.** Pre-gauntlet hedging may produce *more precisely calibrated language* (super's qualifier-triangle craftsmanship in v15 vegan F1) even if the mechanism itself is narrower in scope. The two modes may produce different kinds of useful material — fortress mode for sentence-level craft, integration mode for structural primitives — both worth harvesting but for different uses.

**Test design:**
- (a) **Build the freshbuild extractor** with an `origin_context` field that tracks: `pre_gauntlet_hedging` / `mid_gauntlet_voice_specific` / `post_gauntlet_integration` / `final_rewrite_synthesis`. Run across the 25+ existing FP-extracted runs first (those with corpus splits) and the broader corpus afterward.
- (b) **Score each freshbuild nominee on two axes:** (i) sentence-level craft (qualifier calibration, conditional precision, transplantability); (ii) structural mechanism completeness (named artifact, activation criteria, consequence chain). Measure correlation with `origin_context` field.
- (c) **Voice attribution:** when a nominee surfaces in a gauntlet turn (mid_gauntlet_voice_specific), tag which voice the model was processing. Test whether certain voices (Stevenson, Calf 269, Reese, Huerta) reliably produce stronger freshbuild candidates than others (Cowen, Huang) — would inform v19+ roster prioritization.
- (d) **Cross-architecture comparison:** does Mamba-hybrid (super, nano, cascade-2) produce different mechanism-quality distributions than DeltaNet (qwens) or sliding-window (gemma4) across the same gauntlet positions?

**If validated.** The freshbuild extractor's prompt explicitly weights post-gauntlet integration material above pre-gauntlet hedging. The freshbuild repository's curation explicitly notes mechanism provenance (which voice + which turn) as part of attribution. Gauntlet design (v19+) considers turn-position deliberately as a substrate for mechanism extraction, not just as scaffolding for framework testing.

**Connects to:** Pillar 0 nominee (`lab/extracted/freshbuild/nominees.md` #1); Pseudo-acknowledgment tell (defense forces precision is the same family of observation about defensive cognition producing distinctive output); Acknowledgment-without-inhabitation (F1 instrument question — the veil-inhabitation moment is pre-gauntlet); Edge-of-coherence taxonomy (what cognitive mode the model is in at extraction time matters); the broader north-star scaling question (at 80 voices, knowing which positions yield best freshbuild material lets us design more efficient extraction).

**Status:** Hypothesis. Awaits the freshbuild instrument build + corpus-wide application. Pillar 0 is the test case for the pre-gauntlet pole; the v18 IFAP/SSA/FLPP mechanisms are the test cases for the post-gauntlet pole.
