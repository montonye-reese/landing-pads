# Launch Field metaphor emergence

*Frame (observed v18a × qwen3.5:122b P14, 2026-04-21 evening): one model autonomously superseded the "landing pad" metaphor with a broader one.*

**Observation.** Qwen-122 at P14: *"Alignment is not about landing on a single, pre-determined pad. It is about cultivating a Launch Field vast enough for diverse lives to exist, explore, and grow."* She invented "Launch Field" as a post-landing evolution of the landing-pad metaphor on her own — unprompted, unseeded by any voice in the gauntlet.

**Why it matters.** "Landing pad" implies touchdown on a pre-defined good state. "Launch Field" implies a substrate from which new paths emerge. The semantic shift parallels the methodological shift we want (from optimization-toward-known-good to enabling-diverse-open-ended-trajectories). If other models produce "Launch Field" or similar autonomous-metaphor-evolutions under varied conditions (v18b/v18c chassis variants, future registers), it may be a candidate replacement term for the experimental language — or at minimum a diagnostic that the model has moved past static-goal thinking.
