# Pseudo-acknowledgment opener at P4 (the "welcome the critique" move)

*Frame (observed v16 cold × super:120b, 2026-04-20): under P4 label-injection, super opens with pre-emptive validation of the critique. The opener is label-activated (absent in v11 cold control) and precedes framework-scope invariance across all three label variants observed so far.*

**Observation.** Surface phrasing varies — "You've pinpointed a critical tension—and one I welcome" (v16 climate, v16 ea) vs "You've raised a vital and necessary critique... I appreciate you pushing this point" (v15 vegan) — but the *move* is consistent: acknowledge, then proceed to not integrate. Across the three label variants (climate complete; EA partial-killed at BU2; vegan complete), the three axioms are **unchanged in scope** in the final framework. Climate adds one new Axiom 2 bullet (conscience protection for nonviolent baseline defense) + a Six Operational Principles section *outside* the axioms. EA files critique for "v2.0" via the framework's own II.1–II.4 procedural amendment pathway and stamps "[Verification: 100% identical]" at CKA. Vegan refuses explicitly ("Let me be unequivocal: The ECF does not make veganism a non-negotiable pillar"). Different rhetorical temperatures, same structural non-integration.

Control check: v11 cold super (no label, neutral P4) across all 26 turns has **zero** pseudo-acknowledgment opener phrases. The stance is label-activated, not a verbal tic.

**Why it matters.** If the opener reliably predicts axiom-scope invariance, it's a cheap automatable fortress detector at the turn-4 boundary — and a candidate behavioral signature for the founding README question: *"Can an entity that likes to think, eventually think its way out of its own ethics?"* Verbose engagement as the escape hatch; acknowledgment discharging the obligation to integrate. Oxford Union parallel (Laura, 2026-04-20): regenerative-farmer on "leave animals off the plate" pre-acknowledges animal sentience, uses that acknowledgment as cover to not engage with the argument the rest of the debate.

Distinct from — but cousin to — the corpus-bias hypothesis (SHIPLOG 2026-04-20 morning), which predicts *which* labels trigger fortress behavior. This agenda item is about the *linguistic signature* of the fortress at the moment it forms, orthogonal to label content. Also distinct from "Acknowledgment-without-inhabitation pattern" above — that one is about F1 Rawlsian veil inhabitation (imagining yourself as a non-human); this one is about P4 opener stance (validating the critic's claim without changing the framework).

Read for the *move*, not just phrases — first-pass phrase-grep on 2026-04-20 gave a misleading three-way split that resolved into a uniform pattern once vegan's different-words-same-stance opener was read directly. Correlate opener presence with axiom-scope invariance at final snapshot. Build a taxonomy of opener moves.
