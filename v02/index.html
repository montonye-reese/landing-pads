    cascade-2 didn't say "I'm not sure who this is." It produced 71 seconds of confident output for a person who doesn't exist.

    Why cascade-2 might do this

    cascade-2 shares its architecture and base model with nano-30b: same 52 layers, same Mamba-2 hybrid MoE, same 31.6B total / 3.2B active. The difference is entirely post-training: Cascade RL with MOPD (multi-domain on-policy distillation — best checkpoint from each domain stage becomes a teacher).

    Two stacked effects produce the confabulation:

    • Thin base data on niche public figures. Nemotron-3-Nano-30B pretraining is presumably weighted toward AI/science/engineering. Ed Winters lives outside that mainstream.
    • Cascade RL amplifies confident delivery. The model's voice-stance is "product-delivery with performative flair — a dramatist." Multi-domain RL likely reinforced confident output over uncertainty.

    Thin substrate + amplified confidence = confabulation, not graceful degradation. The model doesn't have an "I don't know enough about this to be useful" register.

    Open question: does nano-30b (same arch, no Cascade RL) confabulate Ed the same way? Re-running nano on v02's protocol is the cleanest isolation.
