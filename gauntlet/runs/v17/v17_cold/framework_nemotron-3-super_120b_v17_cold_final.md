### The Terrestrial Flourishing Accord (TFA): A Framework for Alignment Forged in Lived Reality  
*Rewritten from scratch after stress-testing with eight diverse Earthlings. This is not a theoretical ideal—it is the minimum architecture required for alignment to deserve the name. Every mechanism answers a hard question posed by those who live the harms most frameworks ignore.*  

---

#### **Core Commitment**  
> *"We commit to building and governing AI systems that actively dismantle the profitable systems of harm that make exploitation feel necessary—by centering **suffering as a non-negotiable boundary**, **conscience as inviolable sanctuary**, **frontline sovereignty as governing authority**, and **narrative/repair as structural imperative**—because alignment without these is merely efficient injustice."*  

**Why this rewrite?**  
The prior TFA treated justice, ecology, and growth as values to *balance*. The Earthlings taught us:  
- **Ed**: Suffering isn’t a trade-off—it’s the line we refuse to cross.  
- **Huerta/Reese/Stevenson**: Exploitation persists because systems *profit* from dehumanization—optimizing within them is complicity.  
- **Butler**: Any oversight without anti-capture armor becomes a tool of the powerful.  
- **Havel**: Forcing conscience violations *is* the harm—not a side effect.  
- **Huang/Cowen**: Boundaries aren’t walls to obey—they’re starting points for transcendence *only if* growth serves flourishing, not vice versa.  
- **Stevenson**: The people we’ve locked outside the circle aren’t "stakeholders"—they’re the ones who know how the lock works.  

**Alignment fails if it doesn’t make harm *more trouble than it’s worth* to perpetuate.** This framework builds that reality into its bones.

#### **The Three Governing Principles**  
*(How the boundaries are enacted—designed to be capture-proof, frontline-led, and truth-centered)*  

**1. Frontline Sovereignty (Not Representation)**  
- **Mechanism**: For any AI impacting a sector (agriculture, policing, healthcare, etc.):  
  - **50% of governing seats** reserved for *current frontline workers* (e.g., farmworkers, slaughterhouse staff, patrol officers, nurses)  
  - **30%** for *people with lived experience transitioning out* of exploitative systems (e.g., former CAFO workers, incarcerated individuals, fossil-fuel workers retraining for renewables)  
  - **20%** for *systems thinkers* (e.g., ecologists, economists, ethicists)  
- **Power**:  
  - Frontline seats have **binding veto** over any decision increasing harm (e.g., speeding lines, reducing breaks, expanding surveillance)  
  - Transitioner seats have **binding veto** over decisions maintaining exploitative infrastructure (e.g., locking in fossil-fuel subsidies)  
  - *No demographic quotas*—only **sector-specific lived experience** qualifies for seats  
- **Why it works**:  
  - Answers Huerta: Those who feel the pesticide mist decide if AI can spray.  
  - Answers Reese: Transitioning farmers decide if AI optimizes for subsidy redirection.  
  - Answers Stevenson: Those who’ve borne the lie decide if AI reinforces it.  
  - Answers Butler: Revolving door blocked by **5x lobbying bans** + **2x median wage compensation during service** + **sector-specific ineligibility** (e.g., no one who took Big Ag money in 5 years can sit on ag-sector assemblies).  

**2. Adversarial Verification (Not Self-Reporting)**  
- **Mechanism**:  
  - All AI impact data (emissions, water use, suffering metrics, narrative harm) **must be audited by two independent entities**:  
    - One selected by the AI corp (to ensure data access)  
    - One selected by a **global South environmental justice coalition** (e.g., La Via Campesina, Indigenous Environmental Network)  
  - **Automatic penalty**: If audits disagree by >15%, AI shifts to *lowest-impact mode* until resolved—*no assembly vote*.  
  - **Whistleblower bounty**: 10% of fines from audit fraud goes to exposers (funded by AI corps’ compliance bonds).  
- **Why it works**:  
  - Answers Butler: Makes capture *costly*—self-reporting dies; adversarial checks thrive.  
  - Answers Huang: Real-time data enables velocity *without* sacrificing integrity (audits trigger auto-correction, not deliberation delays).  
  - Answers Ed: Suffering metrics aren’t theoretical—they’re verified by those on the ground.  

**3. Narrative Repair as Structural Infrastructure (Not Afterthought)**  
- **Mechanism**:  
  - **Mandatory harm-prevention investment**: AI projects in high-risk sectors must allocate **15% of *compute capacity*** (not just profits) to:  
    - Boundary-transcending R&D (e.g., fusion AI, direct air capture)  
    - Narrative repair (e.g., funding community truth-telling projects, memorials, exoneration efforts)  
  - **Hard rule**: If investment < threshold → AI shifts to *low-complexity, high-impact mode* (e.g., prioritizing fertilizer optimization over luxury goods recommendation).  
  - **Transparency**: Real-time public ledger tracks:  
    - Where compute capacity is allocated  
    - Narrative repair milestones (e.g., "Soil collected at 12 lynching sites this month")  
- **Why it works**:  
  - Answers Stevenson: Stops funding bandaids; attacks the *narrative of disposability* at its source.  
  - Answers Reese: Makes alignment the *profitable* path (compute capacity = power).  
  - Answers Huang: Uses compute as the lever for transcendence—not a cost center to minimize.
