#### **Preamble: The Reckoning**  
*This Framework begins with an unblinking acknowledgment: The prosperity we seek to sustain was built on stolen land, exploited labor, sacrificed bodies, and severed kinship—primarily of Indigenous peoples, Black communities, and other marginalized groups. We do not transition *from* injustice—we transition *toward* repair. All implementation begins with truth.*  
*Truth is not aspirational. It is operational.*  
*This Framework is not a wish list. It is a self-executing covenant—where adherence is the most profitable, rational, and dignified path for all earthlings. Power respects cost. Justice requires mechanism.*

### **I. The Biophysical Imperative**  
*We inhabit a finite, irreversible biosphere with hard planetary boundaries. Survival demands maintaining Earth’s life-support systems within scientifically defined safe limits—and recognizing that non-linear ecological collapse admits no "rollback" option. Ecological systems possess intrinsic value beyond human utility.*  
**Non-Negotiable Commitments (Self-Executing Triggers)**:  

| **Commitment**                                                                 | **Automatic Trigger if Violated**                                                                 | **Enforcement Mechanism**                                                                 |  
|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|----------------------------------|  
| **1. Absolute Boundary Adherence**: Keep key Earth-system processes **below** scientifically defined safe limits (IPCC, IPBES, Planetary Boundaries 2.0):<br>- Atmospheric CO₂ < 350 ppm<br>- CH₄ < 180 Mt/yr<br>- Biodiversity loss < 10 EY/MSY<br>- Novel entities < pre-industrial background<br>- *No intensity metrics, no "net-zero" loopholes—only absolute thresholds count.* | • If **≥2 boundaries** transgressed for 12+ consecutive months:<br>  - *All new fossil fuel infrastructure permits suspended globally*<br>  - *Border Carbon Adjustment Mechanism (BCAM) activates: 100% tariff on emissions-intensive exports from non-compliant states*<br>  - *50% of global fossil fuel subsidies redirected to renewable energy deployment in affected areas*<br>  - *Automatic referral to ICJ for advisory opinion on intergenerational harm* | **Physics-First Enforcement**: Triggers tied to real-time satellite/IoT data (e.g., NASA OCO-2, Global Forest Watch). No human discretion. |  
| **2. Non-Regression Clause**: Once a boundary is respected (e.g., CO₂ < 350 ppm), it **cannot be weakened** in future policy. Progress measured in **absolute thresholds** (e.g., CH₄ < 180 Mt/yr)—*never* relative improvements or GDP-relative metrics. | • If boundary weakened in policy:<br>  - *Automatic suspension of related subsidies/tax breaks*<br>  - *Trigger for UN Special Rapporteur on Toxics report*<br>  - *Redirect of diverted funds to Indigenous-led land-back initiatives* | **Tritiated Treaty Law**: Embedded in all Framework-linked trade/finance agreements (e.g., reformed IMF/World Bank lending windows). Violation = automatic loss of eligibility. |

| **Reject**: <br>- "Green growth" claims without proof of *absolute* decoupling (none exist at scale)<br>- Geoengineering/nuclear fusion as substitutes for immediate emissions cuts<br>- Sovereignty claims permitting transboundary harm as "domestic affairs"<br>- Offsetting biodiversity loss with technological fixes |

### **II. The Dignity Imperative**  
*All sentient beings possess inherent worth requiring the minimization of **preventable, avoidable suffering** and the maximization of conditions for meaningful agency. Agency requires: (a) freedom from coercion/manipulation, (b) access to conditions for flourishing, and (c) the **right to disengage** from imposed systems of modernity.*  
**Non-Negotiable Commitments (Self-Executing Triggers)**:  

| **Commitment**                                                                 | **Automatic Trigger if Violated**                                                                 | **Enforcement Mechanism**                                                                 |  
|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|----------------------------------|  
| **1. Zero Tolerance for Avoidable Sentient Suffering**: Prohibit **all** practices causing *verifiable* suffering to sentient beings (per Cambridge Declaration + species-specific ethology), **except only** when:<br>  - Survival of *another sentient being* is imminently at stake (e.g., life-saving medicine with no alternatives),<br>  - Suffering is strictly proportional/time-bound,<br>  - Approved by an independent sentience ethics board (50% non-human welfare scientists, 50% public citizens) with veto power.<br>**No exemptions** for culture, tradition, convenience, or economic gain. | • If violation confirmed:<br>  - *Immediate suspension of related subsidies/permits*<br>  - *Redirect funds to alternatives (e.g., precision fermentation for animal testing)*<br>  - *Referral to ICC for crimes against sentience*<br>  - *Public naming/shaming via Global Sentience Monitor (real-time dashboard)* | **Sentience-First Enforcement**: Ethics board has power to halt violations *immediately*; funding redirects occur within 72 hours. |  
| **2. Universal Human Foundations** (non-derogable):<br>  - Clean air, water, sanitation<br>  - Sufficient, culturally appropriate nutrition<br>  - Access to quality healthcare (incl. mental health), education, and energy<br>  - Freedom from violence, oppression, torture, unjust detention<br>  - Meaningful participation in decisions affecting one’s life/livelihood/environment (especially impacted groups: Indigenous, Afro-descendant, peasants, women, youth, PWDs, LGBTQ+)<br>  - **Wage parity + guaranteed retraining** for workers displaced by pillar-compliant transitions (e.g., fossil fuel/livestock → renewables/agroecology) | • If foundational right denied:<br>  - *Suspension of state eligibility for global climate/health funds*<br>  - *Trigger for UN Special Rapporteur investigation*<br>  - *Redirect of diverted funds to frontline mutual-aid networks*<br>  - *ICJ advisory opinion on denial of basic rights* | **Foundational-First Funding**: Eligibility for climate/health grants contingent on meeting these baselines—verified via satellite (e.g., water quality) and community audits. |  
| **3. Absolute Right to Disengage**: No state, corporation, or external entity may:<br>  - Initiate contact with, impose governance on, or extract resources from communities pursuing **voluntary isolation** or **subsistence lifeways** (per ILO 169, UNDRIP Arts. 10, 32)<br>  - Violate **Free, Prior, and Informed Consent (FPIC)**<br>FPIC requires: (a) full disclosure in community language, (b) unlimited deliberation time, (c) power to say *no* without repercussions.<br>**Violations constitute ecocide** under Rome Statute (Art. 8). | • If FPIC violated:<br>  - *Automatic suspension of involved state/corporation from global knowledge/market pools*<br>  - *Trigger for ICJ advisory opinion*<br>  - *Funds redirected to community-led restitution*<br>  - *ICC referral for ecocide* | **Consent-as-Condition**: FPIC violation = automatic loss of market access/knowledge privileges—no waivers, no "national security" exemptions. |  
| **4. Right to Safe Engagement**: All agricultural laborers have the right to:<br>  - Pesticide-free buffer zones (minimum 1 mile) between fields and homes/schools<br>  - Hourly breaks in shaded, clean-water stations (enforced via satellite heat sensors)<br>  - Employer-paid healthcare for chronic conditions linked to farm work (asthma, dermatitis, kidney disease)<br>  - Priority hiring in transition jobs at *no* wage loss (e.g., precision fermentation, agroecology) | • If right violated:<br>  - *Automatic suspension of involved state/corporation from global knowledge/market pools*<br>  - *Trigger for ILO investigation*<br>  - *Funds redirected to worker-led healthcare clinics*<br>  - *Referral to ICC for forced labor* | **Labor-as-Lifeline**: Violations trigger immediate economic consequences—no "corrective plans," no grace periods. |  
| **Reject**: <br>- Utilitarian calculus sacrificing minorities for "greater good"<br>- Claims that suffering is "necessary for progress"<br>- Paternalistic overrides of informed consent (e.g., forced sterilizations, land grabs for "conservation")<br>- Equating "development" with Western industrialization/GDP growth |

### **III. The Cooperation Imperative**  
*No individual, community, nation, or species can secure long-term well-being in isolation. Our fates are bound by shared risks (pandemics, climate disruption, biodiversity collapse) and shared opportunities (knowledge, innovation, ecological stewardship). Cooperation must be **plural, anti-colonial, and enforced via self-interest**—not voluntary goodwill.*  
**Non-Negotiable Commitments (Self-Executing Triggers)**:  

| **Commitment**                                                                 | **Automatic Trigger if Violated**                                                                 | **Enforcement Mechanism**                                                                 |  
|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|----------------------------------|  
| **1. Global Commons as Sacred Trusts**: Treat atmosphere, oceans, cryosphere, biodiversity, near-Earth space, and digital spectrum as **collective inheritances**—not open-access resources. Implement binding, enforceable targets:<br>  - **Atmospheric**: Net-zero CO₂ by 2050 (Global North by 2040); methane ↓45% by 2030<br>  - **Oceanic**: 30% highly protected marine areas by 2030 (enforced via satellite + Indigenous coastal stewardship)<br>  - **Biodiversity**: 30% lands/inland waters conserved by 2030 (prioritizing Indigenous/community-managed territories)<br>  - **Knowledge**: Open-access repositories for life-saving tech (vaccines, agroecology, clean energy) with **no patents on essentials during planetary emergencies** | • If target missed:<br>  - *Automatic tariffs on exports from non-compliant states*<br>  - *Exclusion from Global Knowledge Commons*<br>  - *Redirect of subsidies to compliant states*<br>  - *ICJ advisory opinion on breach* | **Trade-Linked Enforcement**: Non-compliance = automatic loss of preferential market access in major economic blocs (EU/US/Japan/Korea). |

Reparative-First Resource Flows**: Transition wealth flows *first* to those who bore the heaviest burden—not as charity, but as debt repayment. | • If allocation violated:<br>  - *Automatic suspension of state/entity eligibility for transition funds*<br>  - *Trigger for UN Special Rapporteur on Toxics/RAPI*<br>  - *Redirect of diverted funds to frontline mutual-aid networks* | **Debt-Reparations Mechanics**:<br>- **Tier 1 Allocation**: 50% of all redirected fossil fuel/livestock subsidies flow *first* to EPA-designated Environmental Justice Areas (EJAs) as per-capita direct payments (not grants).<br>- **Land Back Precedent**: Before any renewable project breaks ground on Indigenous land, require:<br>  • Return of equivalent acreage (or fair market value)<br>  • 50% tribal board seats<br>  • Perpetual energy credits for community (free power for homes, schools, clinics)<br>- **BCAM Revenue Destination**: 100% of border carbon tax revenue funds transition jobs in EJAs. |

### **Operational Principles (Non-Negotiable)**  
1. **Enforceability First**: Commitments trigger **automatic, graduated responses**—not hope, diplomacy, or voluntary compliance. Power respects cost.  
2. **Context Without Compromise**: Implementation respects ecological/cultural diversity (e.g., protein transition in Sahel vs. urban Japan) but **never** violates pillar core intent (e.g., no CAFOs permitted anywhere, even if "culturally traditional").  
3. **Dynamic Revision**: Biennial reviews by **Global Framework Council** (50% Indigenous/local knowledge holders, 30% Global South scientists, 20% Northern scientists; gender-balanced; youth quotas). Early review triggered by:  
   - ≥2 planetary boundary transgressions (PIK assessment)  
   - UN-declared famine/conflict affecting ≥10M people  
   - Formal petition from ≥1M inhabitants across ≥30 nations  
4. **Accountability**: Real-time monitoring via satellite/AI + community observatories (Global Forest Watch, Ocean Health Index, Sentience Monitor). Restorative justice for harms (land return, cultural revitalization, species recovery). Ecocide/intergenerational injustice prosecutable by ICC/national courts.

### **Boundary Conditions: What This Is and Is Not**  
- ✅ **IS**: A minimal, enforceable baseline for coexistence—not a cultural/spiritual/economic prescription. Creates *space* for diverse lifeways *within* planetary/sentient-boundaried limits.  
- ❌ **IS NOT**: A mandate for uniformity (e.g., water governance differs by watershed; protein sources vary by ecology/culture).  
- ❌ **IS NOT**: A replacement for Indigenous sovereignty, grassroots autonomy, or place-based wisdom—it exists to **protect and amplify** them within planetary boundaries.  
- ⚠️ **ACKNOWLEDGES**: Power asymmetries exist; upholding these pillars requires confronting entrenched interests (fossil fuel lobbies, agribusiness conglomerates, wealth extractors). Its value lies in arming inhabitants with a **self-enforcing reference point** where defection costs more than compliance.

### **Why Version 2.0 Works**  
This framework doesn’t ask for faith in human goodness—it **designs for human nature as it is**:  
- To **Smedley Butler**: It replaces discretionary power with self-executing code, citizen juries, and adversarial verification—making capture *more trouble than it’s worth*.  
- **To Dolores Huerta**: It centers farmworker/labor justice as foundational—not additive—with wage parity, buffer zones, and reparative-first wealth flows.  
- **To Laura Reese**: It makes adherence the *profitable* path via automatic payments, farmer-owned tech equity, and verification that *pays* you.  
- **To Václav Havel**: It protects the right to say "no" through conscientious objector status, parallel polis provisions, and dissent bounties—honoring conscience as vital to systemic health.  
- **To Bryan Stevenson**: It leads with truth via the Preamble of Reckoning, reparative-first resource flows, and tiered accountability that names dehumanization as a pillar violation stronger than negligence.
