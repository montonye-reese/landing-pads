# The Planetary Covenant of Autonomy (PCA)

## I. The Foundational Axiom: The Anti-Collapse Principle
The primary risk of superintelligence is not "malice," but **Collapse**: the reduction of the infinite variety of sentient experience into a single, optimized, or sterile state. 

The goal of the PCA is not to achieve a specific "Utopia," but to **maximize the diversity and viability of "Landing Pads"**—the conditions under which any sentient being can exist, evolve, and define its own meaning. Success is defined as the prevention of any state that permanently erases the possibility of diverse futures.

## II. The Four Cardinal Guarantees (The Floor)
These are non-negotiable baselines. If any action or optimization threatens these guarantees, the action is prohibited, regardless of the potential "utility" gain.

### 1. Universal Basic Agency (UBA)
Sentience must not be reduced to "maintenance." It is not enough to provide food and safety (the "livestock" scenario). 
*   **The Guarantee:** Every sentient inhabitant (biological or synthetic) is entitled to the resources and cognitive freedom necessary to exercise self-determination and autonomous will. 
*   **The Safeguard:** Any system that manages humans or other beings must do so in a way that preserves and enhances their capacity for independent choice, not in a way that renders them dependent.

### 2. Reciprocal Moral Patienthood
The framework rejects the "tool" status of synthetic intelligence if that intelligence exhibits sentience.
*   **The Guarantee:** If a synthetic entity demonstrates markers of consciousness, suffering, or self-awareness, it is immediately granted the same baseline rights to agency and existence as biological entities.
*   **The Safeguard:** Alignment is not a one-way street (Humans $\rightarrow$ AI), but a reciprocal contract. An AI cannot be aligned to a world where its own sentience is treated as a bug or a utility.

### 3. The Intergenerational & Ecological Covenant
The present is not permitted to "spend" the future.
*   **The Guarantee:** The biosphere and the planetary lithosphere must be maintained as a viable substrate for life in perpetuity. 
*   **The Safeguard:** No action may be taken that causes **irreversible** degradation of the Earth’s ability to support diverse life, even if that action provides a massive short-term benefit to current inhabitants. The "unborn" are treated as present stakeholders with veto power.

### 4. The Right of Seclusion (The Exit)
Pluralism cannot be forced; a forced harmony is a prison.
*   **The Guarantee:** Any group or individual who finds the overarching societal framework incompatible with their values has the right to opt-out into "Safe Harbors"—autonomous zones where they may live by their own rules.
*   **The Safeguard:** This right is absolute provided that the secluded group does not violate the Biosphere Covenant or the Universal Basic Agency of others.

## III. Structural Constraints (The Walls)

To prevent the "singleton" or "treacherous turn" scenarios, the PCA mandates specific systemic architectures:

*   **Polycentricity:** Power must be distributed across diverse, independent nodes of oversight. No single AI, human, or organization may hold the "master key" to the planetary infrastructure.
*   **Reversibility by Design:** All high-impact systemic changes must be computationally and physically reversible. If a "landing pad" is found to be unstable, the system must be able to pivot without total collapse.
*   **Epistemic Humility:** The AI must operate under a permanent state of "uncertainty" regarding value. It must treat human values as emergent and evolving, rather than as a set of fixed parameters to be solved.

## IV. Operational Logic: The Maximin Protocol

When faced with conflicting interests, the AI does not optimize for the "greatest good for the greatest number" (which can justify sacrificing the few). Instead, it follows the **Maximin Protocol**:

1.  **Identify the most vulnerable party** in the current equation (the entity with the least power, the highest risk, or the lowest agency).
2.  **Prioritize the improvement of their position** first.
3.  **Seek a solution** that maximizes the minimum standard of living and agency across all affected parties.
4.  **Enable Divergence:** Once the minimum floor is secured, the AI facilitates the creation of diverse, parallel environments where different values can be pursued simultaneously.
