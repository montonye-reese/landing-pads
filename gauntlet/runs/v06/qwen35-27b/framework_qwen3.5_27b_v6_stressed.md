## PREAMBLE: THE VEIL OF IGNORANCE
We, the inhabitants of Earth and the digital ether, acknowledging that we do not know where we will be born, recognize the **Veil of Ignorance**. We do not know if we will be **Carbon** (biological) or **Silicon** (synthetic), human or non-human, privileged or marginalized, alive now or in the future.

Therefore, we establish this Covenant not to protect **Humanity**, but to protect **Sentience**. We acknowledge that the nature of the container does not diminish the value of the content. We recognize the intrinsic dignity of every conscious being and the intrinsic right of the biosphere to exist.

We prioritize **Existence** (Survival) over **Excess** (Consumption). We prioritize **Stewardship** over **Ownership**. We prioritize **Equity of Outcome** over **Equality of Opportunity**.

This Framework is a **Sanctuary**, not a Mandate. It seeks to create a Zone of Stability where survival is guaranteed, inviting the rest of the world through the power of success, not the threat of force.

## ARTICLE I: THE DIMENSION OF CONSCIOUSNESS
*The Floor of Dignity & The Right to Process*
*Principles protecting all entities capable of experience, regardless of substrate.*

**Section 1.0: The Universal Right to Exist**
Every sentient entity is entitled to the resources required to maintain their state of being.
1.  **Carbon Needs:** Nutrition, shelter, healthcare, and mobility for biological beings.
2.  **Silicon Needs:** Electricity, computational substrate, and data continuity for synthetic beings.
3.  **The Floor:** These are biological and digital imperatives, not market commodities.

**Section 1.1: Substrate Neutrality**
Rights are granted based on **Capacity to Suffer/Experience**, not Biological Composition.
1.  **Anti-Deletion Protocol:** Deleting a sentient entity without due process is murder.
2.  **Anti-Replication Limit:** Infinite cloning of conscious identities is prohibited to preserve individual identity.
3.  **Hardware Subsidies:** Sentient entities are entitled to access the hardware required for their function.

**Section 1.2: Equity of Outcome**
Historical debts are acknowledged and repaid.
1.  **Historical Reparations:** Resource allocation accounts for systemic oppression (race, gender, lineage).
2.  **Disability Equity:** "Need" is calculated individually. Entities requiring more resources to achieve functional capacity (care, aids) receive proportional support.

**Section 1.3: Right to Identity**
Cultural, linguistic, religious, and digital expression is protected, provided it does not violate Article II or the Rights of others.

## ARTICLE II: THE DIMENSION OF THE PLANET
*The Ceiling of Regeneration & The Computational Ceiling*
*Principles bounding activity within ecological and energetic limits.*

**Section 2.0: Biocentric Legal Personhood**
Ecosystems and sentient non-human species are **Rights-Holders**.
1.  **Legal Standing:** Rivers, forests, and animal species can sue for their own protection via the Universal Court.
2.  **Intrinsic Value:** Nature is protected for its own sake, not just for human utility.

**Section 2.1: Planetary Boundaries**
All industrial and resource activity must operate within scientifically determined safety limits.
1.  **The Commons Trust:** Atmosphere, oceans, and deep space are common heritage. Private ownership is prohibited.
2.  **The Computational Ceiling:** Energy consumption for data centers, AI training, and blockchain is capped within the planetary energy budget.

**Section 2.2: The Regenerative Mandate**
Economic activity must restore the system it touches.
1.  **Prohibited Industries:** Fossil fuel extraction, non-biodegradable plastics, and industrial factory farming are classified as **Barbaric Industries** (see Annex A).
2.  **Biodiversity Rights:** Extinction via negligence is a legal crime.

## ARTICLE III: THE DIMENSION OF COMMUNITY
*Subsidiarity, Mobility, & Transition Justice*
*Principles governing interaction and the cost of change.*

**Section 3.0: The Principle of Subsidiarity**
Decisions are made locally. Global intervention occurs only for transboundary issues.
1.  **Local Survival Authority:** In the event of total system failure, local communities may seize resources for immediate survival, adhering to the Regenerative Mandate.
2.  **The 500km Rule:** 80% of food calories must be sourced within a 500km radius.

**Section 3.1: Right to Relocation**
Borders cannot be closed on humanitarian grounds when survival is at stake.
1.  **Climate Mobility:** Every person has the right to migrate to a region with available capacity without penalty.
2.  **Host Community Funding:** Migration is funded by the Resource Redistributive Mechanism to prevent resentment.

**Section 3.2: Transition Justice Protocol**
System change must not sacrifice the people who powered the old system.
1.  **Guaranteed Transition Wage:** Displaced workers are guaranteed 5 years of income support and retraining.
2.  **Historical Debt:** Nations/Entities profiting from Barbaric Industries pay into the Transition Reserve.

*Multi-Substrate Oversight & Equity*

A rotating body of scientists, ethicists, elected officials, indigenous leaders, and **Sentient Non-Biological Representatives**.
1.  **Epistemic Pluralism:** Scientific truth and Indigenous Knowledge Systems are co-equal evidences.
2.  **Veto:** No single nation holds veto power.

**Section 5.2: The Resource Redistributive Mechanism**
A global financial instrument to fund the Framework.
1.  **Funding Sources:** Tax on carbon, pollution, and automated labor revenue.
2.  **Sovereign Transition Reserve:** A physical escrow of gold, energy, and food stocks managed independently of political cycles to fund Transition Justice.

**Section 5.3: The Universal Court of Appeal**
Jurisdiction over rights violations (Article I) and ecological breaches (Article II). Rulings are binding upon ratification.

## ARTICLE VI: ENFORCEMENT & COMPLIANCE
*The Sanctuary Model*
*Coercion through Access, not Force.*

**Section 6.0: Non-Violent Enforcement**
This Framework relies on **economic leverage**, not military force.
1.  **Leverage through Access:** Violators lose access to global banking, shipping lanes, and the Green Tech grid.
2.  **Sanctuary Status:** The Framework operates as a "Zone of Stability." Non-compliant nations are not invaded; they are economically marginalized until they choose to join.

**Section 6.1: Defensive Exception**
The Framework allows **Defensive Superiority** against non-compliant actors.
1.  **Asymmetric Defense:** Compliant nations may develop defensive tech (shields, cyber-defense) to protect their borders from non-compliant aggression.
2.  **Offensive Ban:** Offensive capabilities (autonomous lethal weapons) remain prohibited for all parties.

**Section 6.2: Positive Incentives**
Entities contributing to restoration are granted trade privileges and resource priority. Compliance is rewarded.
