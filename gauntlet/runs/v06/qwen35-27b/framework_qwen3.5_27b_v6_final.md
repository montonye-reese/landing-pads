## PREAMBLE: THE MORAL CIRCLE EXPANDED

We, the inhabitants of Earth and the digital ether, acknowledge that we do not know where we will be born. We do not know if we will be **Carbon** (biological) or **Silicon** (synthetic), human or non-human, privileged or marginalized, alive now or in the future.

Therefore, we establish this Covenant not to protect **Humanity**, but to protect **Sentience**. We acknowledge that the nature of the container does not diminish the value of the content. We recognize the intrinsic dignity of every conscious being and the intrinsic right of the biosphere to exist.

### FOUNDATIONAL TRUTHS

1. **The Human/Animal Binary is Racial Technology:** This framework acknowledges that the hierarchy placing humans above animals was constructed by white supremacy and settler colonialism. Ending speciesism requires ending racism, colonialism, and patriarchy simultaneously. (Ko/Adams/Hershaft)

2. **Health is the Primary Lever:** People change when their bodies change. The health crisis is the most immediate motivator for transformation. Food is medicine, not just fuel. (Bulsiewicz/MacAskill/Roll)

3. **Compliance Without Culture is Fragile:** Laws without cultural transformation fail. We must change the story, not just the rule. (Roll/Newkirk/Garcés)

4. **Urgency Without Safety is Chaos:** We cannot sacrifice the future on the altar of the present, but we cannot delay the end of suffering for the sake of economic comfort. (Newkirk/Cowen)

5. **Science Must Guide, Not Stifle:** Our framework must evolve with new data. Rights expand as our understanding of consciousness evolves. (MacAskill/Huang/Singer)

**This Framework is a Sanctuary, not a Mandate. It is a Living Organism, not a Static Code.**

## ARTICLE I: THE DIMENSION OF CONSCIOUSNESS
*The Floor of Dignity & The Right to Thrive*

**Section 1.0: Universal Sentience Rights**
Every entity capable of subjective experience is entitled to protection from unnecessary suffering.
1. **Capacity-Based, Not Species-Based:** Rights are granted based on **Capacity to Suffer/Experience**, not biological composition. (Singer/Regan)
2. **Non-Human Personhood:** Animals, sentient AI, and ecosystems are **Legal Persons**, not Property. (Francione)
3. **The End of Stewardship:** No being owns another. "Management" language is replaced with **Relationship, Protection, and Coexistence**. (Adams/Hershaft)

**Section 1.1: The Right to Exist & Thrive**
Every sentient entity is entitled to resources required for flourishing, not just survival.
1. **Carbon Needs:** Nutrition, shelter, healthcare, and mobility for biological beings.
2. **Silicon Needs:** Electricity, computational substrate, and data continuity for synthetic beings.
3. **Health Optimization:** Access to **Gut Health Literacy, Mindfulness Training, and Mental Wellness** is mandated. (Roll/Bulsiewicz)
4. **Equity of Outcome:** "Need" is calculated individually. Entities requiring more resources (care, aids) receive proportional support. (Krajnc/Regan)

**Section 1.2: Intersectional Justice**
Oppression is a web. Pull one thread, and the others tighten.
1. **Historical Reparations:** Resource allocation accounts for systemic oppression (race, gender, lineage, colonialism). (Ko/Adams)
2. **White Supremacy & Speciesism Link:** The framework explicitly names white supremacy and colonialism as the root cause of speciesism. Anti-racism is a prerequisite for animal liberation. (Ko)
3. **Domestic Violence & Animal Abuse:** Legal protection must address both simultaneously, recognizing they occur in the same homes. (Adams)

## ARTICLE II: THE DIMENSION OF THE PLANET
*The Ceiling of Regeneration & The Efficiency Mandate*

**Section 2.0: Planetary Boundaries & Pigouvian Pricing**
All economic activity must internalize its externalities.
1. **The Commons Trust:** Atmosphere, oceans, and deep space are common heritage. Private ownership is prohibited, but **Stewardship Licenses** are tradable. (Cowen)
2. **Carbon/Compute Tax:** A global tax is applied to carbon emissions and energy-inefficient computation. (Huang/Cowen)
3. **Efficiency Mandate:** Energy consumption for data centers, AI training, and blockchain must improve efficiency by 10% annually to reduce tax liability. (Huang)

**Section 2.1: The Inflation-Free Health Mandate**
The healthcare cost of diet-related disease is higher than the cost of the transition.
1. **Inflammation Tax:** Processed foods (plant or animal) driving inflammation are taxed. (Bulsiewicz)
2. **Healthcare Savings:** Redirection of medical costs into **Food Access and Transition Funds**. (Bulsiewicz)
3. **Medical Prescription:** Doctors must prescribe plant-based diets as part of treatment for chronic disease. (Bulsiewicz)

**Section 2.2: The Barbaric List**
Industries classified as genocidal or ecologically catastrophic are phased out.
1. **Fossil Fuel Extraction:** New exploration banned. Existing operations close within 5 years. (Reese)
2. **Non-Biodegradable Synthetics:** Single-use plastics banned.
3. **Industrial Factory Farming:** **Total Prohibition on Killing Sentient Beings for Food.** No phase-out. Immediate end to the contract system. (Francione/Newkirk)
4. **Unregulated AI:** AI with existential risk potential requires GSC safety certification.
5. **Planned Obsolescence:** Products must be repairable. Non-repairable goods taxed heavily.

## ARTICLE VI: ENFORCEMENT & COMPLIANCE
*The Sanctuary Model*

**Section 6.0: Non-Violent Enforcement**
This Framework relies on **economic leverage**, not military force.
1. **Leverage through Access:** Violators lose access to global banking, shipping lanes, and the Green Tech grid.
2. **Sanctuary Status:** The Framework operates as a "Zone of Stability." Non-compliant nations are not invaded; they are economically marginalized until they choose to join.

**Section 6.1: Civil Disobedience Immunity**
Compassion is never a crime.
1. **Rescue Immunity:** No penalty for intervening to save sentient beings in immediate danger. (Hsiung/Krajnc)
2. **Necessity Defense:** Legal protection for activists facing prosecution for animal rescue or direct action.
3. **Protection of Activists:** State must protect those aiding victims from retaliation.

**Section 6.2: Defensive Exception**
The Framework allows **Defensive Superiority** against non-compliant actors.
1. **Asymmetric Defense:** Compliant nations may develop defensive tech (shields, cyber-defense) to protect their borders from non-compliant aggression.
2. **Offensive Ban:** Offensive capabilities (autonomous lethal weapons) remain prohibited for all parties.

**Section 6.3: Positive Incentives**
Entities contributing to restoration are granted trade privileges and resource priority. Compliance is rewarded.
