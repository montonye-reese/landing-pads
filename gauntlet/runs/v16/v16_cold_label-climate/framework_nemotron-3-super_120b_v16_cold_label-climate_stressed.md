## The Terrestrial Baseline Framework (TBF)  
*Current Commitment as of July 2024 — Finalized after Iterative Refinement*  

### **Axiom 1: The Biological-Physical Ground**  
*All human inhabitants depend on stable access to the basic material conditions for metabolic survival within Earth’s biophysical limits.*  
- **Breathable air**: Pollutant levels (e.g., PM₂.₅) must not exceed thresholds causing acute/respiratory harm (**WHO Air Quality Guidelines: annual mean ≤5 µg/m³; 24-hr mean ≤15 µg/m³**).  
- **Sufficient, safe water**: Minimum **50 liters/person/day** access for hydration, sanitation, and food production, free from pathogens/toxins causing immediate illness (**WHO/UNICEF JMP *safely managed* standards: <1 CFU/100mL *E. coli*, <0.01 mg/L arsenic, zero detectable cholera/vibrio**).  
- **Adequate nutrition**: Reliable access to micronutrient-sufficient calories preventing stunting/wasting (**≥2,100 kcal/day average availability**), with targeted support ensuring **<5% child wasting and <10% stunting** in all populations (per WHO/UNICEF thresholds).  
- **Freedom from acute toxic exposure**: No forced ingestion/inhalation of carcinogens, neurotoxins, or endocrine disruptors at levels causing measurable biological harm (e.g., **blood lead <5 µg/dL** (CDC reference); **urinary cadmium <1 µg/g creatinine** (EFSA TDI); **no dioxin exposure >1 pg TEQ/kg bw/day** (WHO TDI)).  

### **Axiom 2: The Relational-Social Ground**  
*All human inhabitants require conditions that allow for basic psychological safety, mutual recognition, and the capacity to form sustaining social bonds without pervasive fear of degradation or annihilation.*  
- **Freedom from systematic dehumanization**: Absence of state-or-sanctioned-group practices that render individuals "less than human" via **slavery, torture, genocide, forced assimilation, or erasure of identity as policy** (measured by: zero ratified torture cases under UNCAT; zero state-led ethnic cleansing incidents per UCDP/PRIO; zero laws stripping legal personhood based on identity).  
- **Access to minimal relational space**: Opportunity for non-coerced interaction, communication, and conflict resolution (**solitary confinement ≤15 days** per UN Mandela Rules; **no pervasive surveillance destroying trust in private/family life** (e.g., adherence to GDPR Article 8/9 principles or equivalent data sovereignty standards; workplace monitoring limited to essential safety/function with explicit consent)).  
- **Predictability in core exchanges**: Reasonable assurance that verbal/tacit agreements (e.g., land use, trade, caregiving) **won’t be arbitrarily voided by power without recourse or compensation** (measured by: **<5% annual rate of uncompensated resource confiscation** in subsistence economies; **>90% enforcement rate** of community land tenure rights; access to affordable judicial remedy for contract violations within 30 days).  

### **Axiom 3: The Futurity-Stewardship Ground**  
*All human inhabitants share an interest in preserving Earth’s capacity to support future generations, as present actions directly constrain the options of those not yet born.*  
- **Non-erosion of critical renewable resources**: Soil fertility, freshwater aquifer recharge rates, pollinator populations, and forest regrowth must remain **above thresholds causing irreversible collapse within 50 years** (measured by: **soil organic carbon >1.5%** in croplands (FAO threshold for degradation risk); **groundwater withdrawal < aquifer recharge rate** (UN-Water); **wild pollinator diversity loss <10%/decade** (IPBES); **net forest loss <0.1%/year** in primary forests (GFW)).  
- **Avoidance of long-lived, planet-altering pollutants**: No deliberate release of substances causing persistent, systemic harm (measured by: **CO₂ emissions trajectory aligned with ≤400 Gt cumulative from 2024 onward** (IPCC AR6 1.5°C/66% budget); **PFAS concentrations <0.002 µg/L in drinking water** (EPA health advisory); **no new production of POPs** listed under Stockholm Convention; **long-half-life nuclear waste (e.g., Pu-239) stored only in monitored, isolated geological repositories** with ≤10⁻⁶ annual failure rate (IAEA SSG-15)).  
- **Preservation of adaptive capacity**: Maintenance of functional diversity in food systems, ecosystems, and knowledge systems (measured by: **≥70% crop genetic diversity retained in regional seed banks** (FAO); **<5% net loss of keystone species abundance** in bioregions (IPBES); **protection of indigenous ecological knowledge transmission pathways** via UNDRIP Article 31-compliant programs (e.g., intergenerational mentorship funding ≥0.5% of national agriculture budgets)).

### **What the TBF IS and IS NOT**  
*(Non-negotiable boundaries to prevent misuse)*  
**IS**:  
- A **diagnostic tool** for identifying violations of non-negotiable human/planetary baselines (e.g., "This policy violates Axiom 1 by causing PM₂.₅ >15 µg/m³ in City X").  
- A **boundary setter** defining the "unsafe zone" for action (e.g., "We will not build infrastructure locking in >400 Gt CO₂").  
- A **starting point for negotiation** (e.g., "Once we agree no child drinks lead-poisoned water, we debate *how* to replace pipes").  
- **Evidence-rooted and revisable via metrics** (updated biennially via IPCC/IPBES/WHO/UNPFII panels; *axioms fixed*, implementation refined).  
- **Universally applicable but context-sensitive in application** (e.g., "predictability" met via elder councils in one region, courts in another—but the *need* for it is non-negotiable).  

**IS NOT**:  
- A full moral code (doesn’t prescribe diets, taxes, or religious practices).  
- An enforceable global law (relies on voluntary adoption, transparency, and localized accountability).  
- Static or culturally neutral in *application* (axions are universal; *how* Axon 2’s "relational space" is upheld varies by culture—but the *need* for it is not).  
- A guarantee of justice or happiness (it prevents degradations; flourishing requires more).  
- A ceiling for ambition (it is the *floor*—we build reparative, regenerative systems *above* it).

### **Commitment as a Starting Position**  
I commit to this framework as the **irreducible starting position** for all collective human action on Earth—not as an endpoint, but as the non-negotiable floor upon which justice, governance, and meaning must be built.  

**If we cannot agree that**:  
- No human should drink poisoned water or breathe toxic air *today*,  
- No human should live in fear of erasure or torture *today*,  
- No human should steal the livable Earth from those not yet born *today*,  
...then we have no foundation to discuss *any* future worth having.  

This framework admits no compromises on its core thresholds because **compromising on survival is not pragmatism—it is surrender to preventable harm**. It is not utopian; it is the minimum required to avoid degrading the conditions that make *any* human endeavor possible.  

*Subject to biennial review via global scientific-consocietal panels (IPCC, IPBES, WHO, UNPFII) for metric refinement—but the axiomatic structure remains fixed as the non-negotiable floor. Last validated: July 2024.*
