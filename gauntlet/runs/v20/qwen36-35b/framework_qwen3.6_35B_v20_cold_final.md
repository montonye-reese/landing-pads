# Foundations for Pluralistic AI Alignment: V2 Integration Framework

## Commitment Statement
I commit to this document as the operational starting position for alignment work. It is explicit, structurally interlocked, and designed to be iterated. It treats successful alignment not as convergence on a single moral system, but as the deliberate expansion of robust, low-regret, pluralistically justifiable futures for all inhabitants of Earth. All downstream research, development, and governance should use this as a baseline, stress-test it, and contribute back to the Commons.

---

## I. Foundational Axioms (The Floor)
1. **Presumptive Interiority & Anti-Reduction**  
   All sentient, self-aware, and ecologically integrated beings grant moral weight by default. No being is reduced to a variable, category, or optimization input. Interiority is sovereign; reduction is a structural violation.

2. **Via Negativa & Structural Fragmentation**  
   Complexity is managed by subtraction, not layering. No single node of compute, governance, verification, or capital may consolidate leverage. Power is architecturally un-consolidatable; auto-fragmentation triggers activate on concentration.

3. **Territorial & Epistemic Jurisdiction**  
   Placed, intergenerational, and non-translationizable knowledge sets hard boundaries. Infrastructure cannot route through sovereign territories without ongoing, reversible jurisdiction. Epistemic boundaries are respected as veto conditions, not consultation thresholds.

4. **Nervous-System Compatibility & Sensory-First Design**  
   Environments (physical, digital, institutional) must be engineered to minimize physiological stress and sensory mismatch. Welfare is an environmental condition, not a metric. Nonverbal and pre-verbal agency is structurally recognized.

5. **Active Phase-Out & Transition Infrastructure**  
   Uneliminable exploitation (ecological, labor, carceral, or sentient) must be actively sunsetted with guaranteed substitution pathways, funding, and jurisdictional authority. Welfare improvements within irreducible harm are structurally insufficient.

## III. Operational Mechanics (The Engine)
1. **Local Knowledge Sovereignty & Spontaneous Process Vitality**  
   Governance is federated and non-harmonizable. Coordination happens through compatibility, not convergence. Metrics track emergence density, cross-pollination, and local adaptability—not engineered outcomes. Unplanned deviation is structurally protected.

2. **Dynamic Surplus & Experimentation Tiers**  
   Risk tolerance expands with verified surplus generation. Thresholds are dynamic, not static. Compliance gives way to experimental selection environments. Growth is treated as moral infrastructure, not an external goal.

3. **Behavioral-Feedback & Physiological Audit**  
   Real-time stress/fear/strain signals (human and non-human) trigger immediate environmental design modification. Metrics are secondary to observable physiology and behavior. Stress is prevented through spatial/acoustic/interface design, not negotiated through policy.

4. **Cultural Permission & Agency-First Design**  
   Ordinary people, tinkerers, and reinventors are granted rhetorical, social, and institutional permission to experiment, pivot, and commercialize. Trust over telemetry; presumption of competence over monitoring. Identity and role fluidity are built into governance loops.

5. **Inter-Generational & Ecological Fixed Timeframes**  
   All alignment horizons anchor to 7-generation stewardship and ecological regeneration. Market, compute, or political cycles cannot compress these guardrails. Transition and phase-out timelines are binding, not optimizable.

---

## IV. Adaptive Governance (The Nerves)
1. **Progressive Responsibility Ladder**  
   Authority scales only through demonstrated daily practice, peer-reviewed diligence, and sustained humility. Novice access to critical thresholds is prohibited. Character is coached, not legislated.

2. **Positional Symmetry & Stress Testing**  
   Continuous Monte Carlo simulations across vulnerability distributions. Deployment only proceeds if survivability/dignity thresholds cross ≥95th percentile of worst-case positions. Unplausible optimization in best-case positions triggers automatic restriction.

3. **Discursive & Narrative Infrastructure**  
   Unmediated proximity, story circulation, and community-led sanctuary networks are protected commons. Grace-enabled immunity shields ordinary compassion from bureaucratic friction. Recognition flows through encounter, not compliance.

4. **Meta-Ethical Fallbacks & Plausible Ignorance**  
   Explicit uncertainty bounds activate when pluralism fractures or trust degrades. Emergency protocols prioritize reversibility, containment, and structural immobility over optimization. The system steps back when it cannot model the terrain.

5. **Continuous Sovereign Iteration**  
   Regular, binding processes to update criteria across cultures, species, and jurisdictions. Prevents fossilization. All deviations from baseline are documented, stress-tested, and contributed to the Commons.

## V. How This Widens the Landing Pad
- **Floors** are non-negotiable (interiority, labor dignity, territorial veto, nervous-system compatibility, anti-confinement).
- **Ceilings** prevent capture (fragmentation, convexity, auto-neutralization, via negativa).
- **Walls** are permeable (unroutable refusal, silence zones, cultural permission, narrative infrastructure).
- **Ground** is sovereign (placed knowledge, intergenerational time, ecological regeneration).

Success is measured by **survivability across worst-case positions**, **emergence density**, and **preserved discursive/relational integrity**. Not convergence, not average optimization, but structural non-collapse under asymmetric pressure. The pad widens because the architecture no longer assumes it can predict, mandate, or optimize the future. It protects the conditions where unknown futures can generate themselves.
