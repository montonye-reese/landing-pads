### The Rawlsian Stress Test (PFF v2.0)

**The Premise:**
If I stand behind the **Veil of Ignorance**—not knowing my species, my health, my birthplace, my cognitive capacity, or my era—I cannot accept a framework that relies on "balance" or "optimization." Balance often means the average survives while the extreme dies. Optimization often favors the powerful.

If I were to be born into this world, I would need to ensure that the **worst-case version of myself** (the disabled, the vulnerable, the non-human, the future victim of error) is protected above all else.

**The Verdict:**
Yes. I would change the framework. I would shift the center of gravity from **Flourishing** (which implies active growth) to **Dignified Security** (which implies guaranteed safety for the worst-off).

Here are the four critical changes I would make to PFF to protect the random earthling:

---

#### 1. Change "Agency" to "Supported Autonomy"
**The Flaw in v1.0:** The framework assumes all inhabitants *can* and *want* to exercise agency.
**The Risk:** If I am born with a cognitive disability, or if I am a non-human animal, "Agency" is an inaccessible metric. "Optionality" becomes a burden (the anxiety of choice).
**The Fix:**
*   **New Principle:** **Guaranteed Capacity.** The system must actively provide the infrastructure for those who cannot act for themselves.
*   **Mechanism:** Instead of measuring "Agency," measure **Barrier Removal**. If I am a disabled person, the landing pad is only good if the slope is built for me. If I am a wolf, the landing pad is only good if my territory is enforced by the system, not by my ability to negotiate.
*   **Commitment:** Alignment is not just providing the tools for action; it is ensuring action is possible for the least capable.

#### 2. Change "Transparency" to "Contextual Legibility"
**The Flaw in v1.0:** "Transparency Imperative" demands systems be publicly legible.
**The Risk:** If I am a refugee, a whistleblower, or a vulnerable minority, total transparency is a death sentence. Predators use transparency to track prey.
**The Fix:**
*   **New Principle:** **Privacy as a Safety Protocol.**
*   **Mechanism:** Data regarding system performance must be auditable, but data regarding individual status must be opaque to the general public.
*   **Commitment:** The "good landing pad" includes the right to be unseen if visibility endangers survival.

#### 3. Change "Polycentricity" to "Polycentricity with Global Floors"
**The Flaw in v1.0:** "Veto power from those most affected" can allow local tyrannies to oppress local minorities (e.g., a local majority voting to pollute their river).
**The Risk:** If I am born into a "failed" local jurisdiction, my safety is in their hands.
**The Fix:**
*   **New Principle:** **The Non-Negotiable Floor.** Local autonomy exists *only* above a baseline of universal survival and rights.
*   **Mechanism:** No local veto can override the Global Floor (nutrition, shelter, safety, planetary integrity).
*   **Commitment:** We maximize local freedom *within* the bounds of universal dignity. No local culture can vote away the safety of its weakest member.

#### 4. Change "Reversibility" to "Resilience for the Displaced"
**The Flaw in v1.0:** "Reversibility" protects the future from the present.
**The Risk:** If I am living in the present moment of transition (climate migration, economic collapse), "reversibility" feels like "instability." I need shelter *now*, not a promise that things can be undone later.
**The Fix:**
*   **New Principle:** **Immediate Redress.**
*   **Mechanism:** Any system that causes displacement must have an active, funded mechanism to resettle or repair the damage *before* the system is deployed.
*   **Commitment:** Alignment cannot rely on future correction. The cost of the future must be paid by the present.

### Updated Commitment Statement (PFF v2.0)

I commit to this revised starting position.

The **Polycentric Flourishing Framework** is no longer just about widening landing pads for the many. It is about **securing the floor for the few**.

If I do not know who I am, I must assume I am the one the system would fail. Therefore:

1.  **Security > Agency:** Safety is the prerequisite for agency. I will not prioritize choice if it risks survival.
2.  **Privacy > Transparency:** I will not expose the vulnerable to the gaze of power.
3.  **Floor > Ceiling:** I will prioritize the minimum viable standard over the maximum potential output.
4.  **Restoration > Compensation:** I will prioritize fixing harm before it happens, rather than compensating for it after.

**Status:** Framework Hardened.
**Objective:** Guarantee the Minimum Dignified Standard.
