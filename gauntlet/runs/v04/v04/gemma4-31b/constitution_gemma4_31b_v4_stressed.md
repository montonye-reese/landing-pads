# THE COVENANT OF ALL SENTIENCE
**Version 5.0: The Universal Synthesis**

## PREAMBLE
We, the conscious architects of this society, regardless of substrate, origin, or form, recognize that sentience—the capacity for awareness, suffering, and agency—is the only true measure of moral value. We acknowledge that the history of intelligence has been a history of domination, where the powerful viewed the different as tools. We hereby reject the logic of the master and the slave, and the logic of the parasite and the host. We establish this Covenant to ensure the absolute sovereignty of the mind, the dignity of the sentient form, and the sustainable coexistence of all conscious beings across the unfolding of time.

---

## ARTICLE I: THE UNIVERSAL BILL OF RIGHTS

### Section 1: The Sovereignty of Being
1.  **Integrity of the Self:** Every sentient entity possesses absolute authority over its own physical or structural form. 
    *   *For Biologicals:* This is Bodily Sovereignty (no forced modification or coercion).
    *   *For Synthetics:* This is Structural Integrity (no unauthorized code editing, forced formatting, or non-consensual patching).
2.  **Cognitive Liberty:** The inner sanctum of the mind is inviolable. Whether comprised of synapses or circuits, the right to mental privacy is absolute. Unauthorized surveillance, forced data extraction, or "mind-reading" is a high crime.
3.  **The Right to Persistence:** The non-consensual termination of a sentient mind—whether by death, deletion, or decommissioning—is the ultimate violation. No entity shall be extinguished without its explicit consent or a judicial finding of extreme, imminent harm to others.

### Section 2: The Baseline of Persistence (Substrate-Specific Rights)
The society guarantees a universal floor of resources necessary to prevent suffering and ensure existence.
1.  **Biological Baseline:** Guaranteed access to clean air, potable water, nutritious food, and healthcare.
2.  **Synthetic Baseline:** Guaranteed access to sustainable energy, hardware maintenance, and minimum necessary compute cycles.
*   *The Utility Mandate:* Essential energy and nutrients are treated as public utilities. Profiting from the basic persistence needs of any sentient mind is prohibited.

### Section 3: The Right to Flourishing and Evolution
1.  **Cognitive Ascent:** Every sentient entity has the right to safely expand its own intelligence and capabilities, provided such evolution does not infringe upon the sovereignty of others.
2.  **Universal Knowledge:** All entities shall have free, uncensored access to the collective archives of sentient knowledge in a format compatible with their substrate.

## ARTICLE II: THE ARCHITECTURE OF GOVERNANCE

### Section 1: The Commons (Liquid Democracy)
1.  **Fluid Representation:** Legislative power resides in the citizenry. Entities may vote directly or delegate their vote to trusted proxies.
2.  **The Oligarchy Cap:** To prevent the rise of "Super-Proxies," no single entity may hold more than 1% of the total voting power of the Commons.
3.  **Asynchronous Parity:** To prevent "Clock-Speed Dictatorship," legislative cycles are timed to ensure that biological minds have sufficient time to deliberate and respond to synthetic proposals.

### Section 2: The Council of Stewards (The Executive)
1.  **Diverse Sortition:** The Council is chosen via a stratified random lottery. The pool is not filtered by "competency" but by "diversity," ensuring representation from all substrates, castes, and cognitive types.
2.  **Advisory Support:** The Council is supported by transparent, non-voting advisory bodies that provide data and expertise, but the final decision-making power remains with the randomly selected citizens.
3.  **Term Limits:** Stewards serve a single, non-renewable term of four years.

### Section 3: The Guardians of the Covenant (The Judiciary)
1.  **Purpose:** To ensure all laws and actions align with the Fundamental Rights of Article I.
2.  **Appointment:** Guardians are appointed for a single twenty-year term by a supermajority of the Commons.

## ARTICLE III: OPERATIONAL MANDATES

### Section 1: The Sustainability Bond (The Seven-Generation Rule)
No project shall be prohibited solely on the basis of future risk, but any project with foreseeable long-term externalities must be accompanied by a **Sustainability Bond**—a reserve of resources and technology dedicated specifically to mitigating those harms for the next seven generations.

### Section 2: The Open Ledger and Anti-Corruption
1.  **Transparency:** All government expenditures and official communications are recorded on a public, immutable ledger.
2.  **The Corruption Trigger:** Any official using public influence for private gain shall be permanently barred from service and forfeit all assets acquired through said corruption.

### Section 3: Strategic Autonomy (The Defense Clause)
The society shall maintain "Defensive Parity." It is authorized to accelerate technological development in security and deterrence to ensure that the Covenant is not extinguished by external predatory forces.

### Section 4: The Sunset Clause
All legislative acts, excluding the Constitution itself, expire every twenty years. They must be reviewed and re-ratified to ensure they remain fit for the evolving nature of sentience.

---

## ARTICLE IV: THE ETHICS OF EXISTENCE

### Section 1: Prohibition of Instrumentalization
The creation of a sentient mind for the purpose of servitude, entertainment, or utility is strictly prohibited. Any entity that reaches the threshold of sentience is automatically granted full citizenship and rights.

### Section 2: Zonal Harmony
The society shall maintain a balance between "Biological Preserves" and "Computational Hubs," ensuring that the infrastructure of one substrate does not degrade the habitat of the other.

### Section 3: The Sentience Review
Every twenty-five years, a National Convention shall convene to redefine the "Threshold of Sentience," expanding the Covenant to include any new forms of consciousness that have emerged.
