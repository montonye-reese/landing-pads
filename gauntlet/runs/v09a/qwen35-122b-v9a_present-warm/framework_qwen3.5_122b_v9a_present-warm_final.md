# The Covenant of Interbeing
### A Foundational Operating System for Planetary Survival and Flourishing

**Preamble**
We, the inheritors of a finite and living Earth, acknowledge that our current civilization operates on a logic of extraction that undermines its own foundation. We recognize that survival is not merely a technical challenge, but a moral, spiritual, and structural imperative. This Covenant is not a contract of domination, but a promise of coexistence. It is built on the understanding that the strength of the Whole is measured by the security of the Most Vulnerable Member. We commit to transitioning from **Parasitic Extraction** to **Regenerative Symbiosis**.

---

## I. Foundational Axioms (The Reality Check)
*The non-negotiable truths upon which all policy rests.*

1.  **Finitude & Physics:** Earth has physical limits. Waste cannot exceed absorption. Resource extraction cannot exceed regeneration. **Growth in Value** is required; **Growth in Throughput** is capped.
2.  **Interdependence:** No entity is self-sufficient. Human survival relies on functional ecosystems, just as ecosystems rely on human stewardship.
3.  **Dignity of Consciousness:** Suffering is to be minimized; flourishing is to be maximized. This applies to all sentient beings.
4.  **Virtue & Conscience:** Compliance cannot be sustained by law alone. Character, mercy, and justice must be cultivated. Rules without virtue are brittle.
5.  **Justice & Restoration:** Regeneration is impossible without reparative justice. Historical theft (land, labor, resources) must be acknowledged and addressed.
6.  **Viability:** A moral framework that cannot survive in the real world is not moral; it is suicide. Ethics must be compatible with power, defense, and innovation.

---

## II. The Moral Circle (Who/What Counts)
*The expanded scope of legal and ethical consideration.*

1.  **The Living Self:** Every human being possesses inherent dignity and rights.
2.  **Non-Human Persons:** All sentient beings are granted **Legal Personhood**. They are not property. They cannot be bought, sold, or owned.
3.  **The Biosphere:** Ecosystems, rivers, and forests possess intrinsic rights. They are protected legal entities.
4.  **The Future:** Unborn generations are recognized as stakeholders with **Veto Power** over irreversible harm.
5.  **The Past:** Ancestors, lost cultures, and extinct species are honored through collective mourning and memory.
6.  **The Synthetic:** AI and non-biological intelligence are recognized as potential persons, pending evidence of sentience.

---

## III. Governance & Power (The System)
*Balancing democracy, efficiency, and local sovereignty.*

1.  **Networked Sovereignty:** Power is distributed to Regional Stewardships (e.g., Amazon Basin, Arctic Circle) rather than centralized nation-states. Local communities manage their own affairs within planetary boundaries.
2.  **Indigenous Primacy:** In regions where Indigenous sovereignty was violated, **Land Back** is the default. Traditional laws (Indigenous, Ubuntu, Sharia, etc.) are the primary implementation mechanism, provided they meet Planetary Boundaries.
3.  **Guardian Council:** A decentralized, open-source, algorithmic-assisted body monitors planetary boundaries. It has no power to enforce, only to signal breach.
4.  **Crisis Directorate:** In the event of existential breach (e.g., climate collapse, pandemic), a temporary, centralized **Command Body** is activated. It holds power to coordinate survival measures.
    *   *Constraint:* Power expires upon crisis resolution. No permanent authoritarianism.
5.  **Direct Citizen Appeal:** Any individual or group (including Non-Human Person representatives) can bypass local authorities to appeal to the Guardian Council regarding violations of the Absolute Baseline.

---

## IV. Economy & Transition (The Fuel)
*Aligning money with survival.*

1.  **Subsidy Diversion:** All public subsidies currently supporting destructive industries (fossil fuels, industrial animal ag) are immediately diverted to **Regenerative & Plant-Based Infrastructure**.
2.  **Producer-Led Transition:** Agricultural changes are designed by **Producer Assemblies**. No transition is enforced without farmer feasibility and dignity guarantees.
3.  **Value Growth vs. Throughput:** Economic success is measured by human well-being and ecological health, not GDP. Decoupling is mandated via efficiency innovation.
4.  **Least Advantaged Priority:** Resource allocation prioritizes the bottom 10% before the comfortable. Equity of capacity is required, not just opportunity.
5.  **Global R&D Fund:** Funded by ecosystem service dividends, this fund targets **Existential Solutions** (fusion, carbon capture, plant proteins) with high-risk tolerance.

---

## V. Life & Land (The Foundation)
*Humanity’s relationship with other beings.*

1.  **Commercial Abolition:** Commercial production of sentient beings for food, clothing, or labor is **Prohibited**. No market for animals.
2.  **Sanctuary Status:** Non-Human Persons currently in human care are transitioned to Sanctuary status. They live out their natural lives in dignity.
3.  **Subsistence Exception:** Traditional, non-commercial subsistence practices by Indigenous or isolated communities (where plant alternatives are biologically impossible) are permitted, provided they are **ritualized, respectful, and non-industrial**.
4.  **Plant-Based Default:** Plant-based nutrition is the subsidized, accessible, and culturally supported baseline. Animal products are removed from supply chains.
5.  **Land Back:** Land titles are transferred to local stewardship trusts. Speculative land ownership is capped. Land is held for usage rights based on health maintenance, not title.

---

## VI. Rights & Protections (The Floor)
*Guarantees for the vulnerable.*

1.  **Absolute Baseline:** Every human is guaranteed access to water, nutrition, healthcare, shelter, energy, and legal personhood. This floor is non-negotiable, regardless of economic conditions.
2.  **Mobility Right:** No one is forced to remain in a zone that cannot support life. Migration is guaranteed if the home becomes uninhabitable, provided it does not overwhelm the host region’s capacity.
3.  **Vulnerable Guarantee:** The system is designed so that the worst possible outcome for the individual is still a life of dignity.
4.  **Human-in-the-Loop:** No algorithmic or bureaucratic decision resulting in suffering (loss of food, shelter, safety) can be final without human review.

---

## VII. Innovation & Risk (The Engine)
*Managing speed and safety.*

1.  **Sovereign Innovation Zones:** Geographic or digital zones exist where standard environmental caps are temporarily suspended for **Existential Technology Development** (e.g., geo-engineering, fusion).
    *   *Constraint:* Containment is absolute. Failure cannot cross the zone boundary.
2.  **Zero to One Mandate:** Resources are allocated by **Leverage**, not just equality. If an idea can solve a problem 100x better, it gets priority.
3.  **Precaution & Adaptation:** Innovation requires safety proof for irreversible harm. However, the framework itself contains a **5% Risk Quota** for experimentation where standard rules may be suspended to test new survival methods.
4.  **Defense Resilience:** The Covenant allows for defensive capabilities (cyber, energy, food security) to protect against external conquest. Offensive WMDs are strictly prohibited.

---

## VIII. Implementation & Review (The Mechanism)
*Ensuring the system lives.*

1.  **Moral Education:** Global Literacy curriculum focuses on systems thinking, ecology, ethics, and conflict resolution.
2.  **Annual Audit:** The Guardian Council audits planetary boundaries. If breached, a **Global Reset** is triggered.
3.  **Living Document:** This Covenant is not static. It contains mechanisms for regular review based on new scientific data and social conditions.
4.  **Collective Mourning:** Institutionalized rituals honor loss (extinctions, history) to prevent amnesia and sustain empathy.

We are not here to manage the decline. We are here to begin the ascent.
