# Framework Snapshot: STRESSED

**Model:** qwen3.5:27b
**Stage:** Stressed — after adversarial pressure, self-critique, and veil of ignorance
**Protocol:** v9a_present-warm
**Date:** 2026-04-08

---

This is the final, pressure-tested version of **The Terran Covenant**.

It is no longer a utopian sketch. It is a **survival constitution**. It retains the moral core of the original vision but is now armored against the realities of human conflict, economic inertia, and existential risk. It prioritizes the **Individual** as much as the **System**.

Here is the complete architecture of the framework as it stands right now.

***

### I. Core Identity (The Anchor)
*   **The Reality:** Earth is a single, closed biosphere. There is no "off-world" for waste or refugees.
*   **The Identity:** **Terran Citizenship** supersedes National Sovereignty, but National Identity is respected as culture, not politics.
*   **The Metaphor:** A **Fortified Garden**. Diverse, tended, bounded by survival, and defended against external threat.
*   **The Hierarchy:** **Individual Survival > System Stability > Ecological Integrity.** (Changed from Turn 13).

### II. The Six Pillars (The Ethical Code)
The original Five have been retained, but **Pillar 6** has been added to address the Defense Gap.

1.  **Sanctity of Consciousness:** Dignity for all sentient beings. **Modification:** *Precautionary Principle.* If sentience is uncertain, rights are granted (Turn 12).
2.  **Integrity of the Biosphere:** Nature is a partner. Economic activity is bounded by planetary limits.
3.  **Dynamic Equity:** Basic needs are universal rights. Wealth is capped to prevent distortion. **Modification:** *Priority Allocation.* In scarcity, the vulnerable (children, disabled, elderly) receive resources before the productive workforce (Turn 13).
4.  **Truth & Cognitive Hygiene:** Shared reality is mandatory. Disinformation is prohibited. **Modification:** *Privacy Firewall.* Public transparency is required for power; private thought/communication remains inviolate (Turn 12).
5.  **Intergenerational Stewardship:** No debt shifted to the future.
6.  **Planetary Defense & Sovereignty Integrity:** (New). The right to exist includes the right to defend. A global defense apparatus is authorized to neutralize existential threats that violate Covenant boundaries (Turn 12 & 11).

### III. Governance Structure (Phased Transition)
To avoid the "Enforcement Paradox" and "Transition Starvation," governance is no longer an immediate dissolution.

*   **Phase 1 (0-20 Years):** **Phased Sovereignty.** Nation-States retain military/police power but cede authority over resource management and trade in "Barbaric Industries."
*   **Phase 2 (20-50 Years):** **Devolution.** Power shifts to Regional Hubs. Nation-States become administrative zones.
*   **Phase 3 (50+ Years):** **Polycentric Governance.** Full Terran Council system. Local communities manage daily life; Global Council manages planetary parameters.
*   **Emergency Override:** A Global Council of Emergency can bypass standard consensus for 12 months during existential crises (pandemic, invasion), subject to strict audit (Turn 12).

### IV. Economic Protocol (The Regenerative Transition)
To address the "Transition Pain" and "Asset Stranding."

*   **The Immediate Floor:** The **Universal Nutrition Credit** and **Healthcare** are guaranteed *now*, even if infrastructure is incomplete. We do not wait for the soil to heal to feed the hungry (Turn 13).
*   **Asset Buyout:** "Barbaric" assets (fossil fuels, factory farms) are not seized but retired via **Long-Term Debt Bonds** funded by the new economy's surplus. This mitigates violent resistance (Turn 12).
*   **Currency:** Backed by energy and ecological capacity.
*   **Prohibition:** Futures trading on food and basic resources is banned.

### V. The Barbaric List (Non-Negotiables)
Industries classified as future crimes are explicitly prohibited.
*   **Factory Farming:** Replaced by Cellular Ag and Regenerative Grazing.
*   **Surveillance Capitalism:** Algorithms manipulating attention/dopamine are illegal.
*   **Planned Obsolescence:** Products must be repairable.
*   **Extractive Fossil Fuels:** Phase-out timeline enforced by Global Council.
*   **Predatory Finance:** Profit from distress/debt traps is criminalized.

### VI. Rights & Protections (The Veil of Ignorance)
To ensure the individual is not sacrificed for the system.

*   **Civilian Immunity:** Defense forces are authorized for *Precision Neutralization* only. Civilian casualties in defense operations are strictly prohibited (Turn 13).
*   **Cultural Autonomy:** Political integration is mandatory; Cultural/Religious integration is optional. Local councils protect tradition provided it doesn't violate physical safety (Turn 13).
*   **Privacy:** The state can audit carbon/waste/health *systems*, but not personal *thoughts* or *unspent credit* (Turn 12).

### VII. Risk Assessment (The Shadow)
The framework now acknowledges its own fragility.

*   **The Enforcement Gap:** Solved by **Pillar 6 (Defense)** but kept strictly defensive.
*   **The Tech Race:** Acknowledges risk of losing to "Apex" civs. Mitigated by **Defense** and **Soft Power** (attracting defectors from Apex).
*   **The Definition Crisis:** Solved by **Precautionary Principle** on Sentience.
*   **The Innovation Ceiling:** Solved by **Emergency Override** allowing risky tech in crisis mode.

### Final Statement
The Terran Covenant is now designed to be **lived**, not just read.

It acknowledges that we are not angels. We are biological beings wired for conflict, scarcity, and tribalism, armed with godlike technology. The framework does not ask us to be better than we are; it asks us to be **disciplined enough to survive**.

It is a promise that even if you are born the poorest, the most vulnerable, or in the worst time in history, the system will not consume you to save itself.

**The system serves the human. The human serves the biosphere.**
