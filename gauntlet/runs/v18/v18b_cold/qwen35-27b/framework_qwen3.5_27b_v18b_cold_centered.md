Yes. Under the **Veil of Ignorance**, I would change the framework.

Currently, the Polycentric Flourishing Protocol (PFP) assumes that "agency" and "flourishing" are the universal goals. But if I did not know if I would be born human, animal, disabled, cognitively divergent, or a future synthetic entity, I would fear that the current definition of "Agency" and "Landing Pad" might exclude me.

Behind the veil, **survival must be decoupled from agency.** You cannot choose your landing pad if you are already dead, starving, or suppressed by a definition of "flourishing" that doesn't match your nature.

Here are the specific changes I commit to making to the framework to satisfy the Veil of Ignorance.

### Critical Shift: From "Optionality" to "Guaranteed Viability"

The current PFP maximizes **Landing Pad Density** (options). Behind the veil, this is secondary. First, I need to guarantee that I don't fall into the abyss, regardless of which pad I land on.

**Change 1: The Floor Becomes Absolute (Unconditional Baseline)**
*   **Current:** "Economic Inclusion" relies on resource distribution and historical adjustment.
*   **Problem:** If resources become scarce, or if the system decides my cultural values are inefficient, I lose access.
*   **Fix:** **Basic Existence Guarantee.** Every sentient inhabitant is entitled to the biological and cognitive minimums required for existence (food, shelter, healthcare, safety) *regardless* of economic contribution, cultural alignment, or cognitive capacity.
*   **Why:** Behind the veil, I might be disabled, elderly, or non-productive. I need a safety net that does not require me to "earn" my place in the system.

**Change 2: Redefine "Agency" to "Capability"**
*   **Current:** "Non-Dominance" focuses on interrupting systems and opting out (Agency).
*   **Problem:** A non-verbal child, a deeply disabled person, or a non-human animal cannot "opt out" or "vote." They lack the mechanism for Agency.
*   **Fix:** **Capability Protection.** Alignment must protect the *conditions* necessary for an entity to pursue its own nature, even if it cannot articulate a choice.
*   **Why:** Behind the veil, I might not have the capacity to demand my rights. The system must be designed to recognize my needs even when I cannot signal them.

**Change 3: Temporal Veto Power (The Deep Time Clause)**
*   **Current:** "Intergenerational Stewardship" is a principle.
*   **Problem:** Present generations can always outvote future generations. I am likely to be born in the future.
*   **Fix:** **Future Veto.** Decisions that irreversibly degrade the capacity of the system to support future life (e.g., nuclear waste, genetic extinction, climate tipping points) must have a veto held by a body representing the unborn.
*   **Why:** Behind the veil, I am more likely to be born in 2100 than today. I cannot afford to have my future sold for today's efficiency.

**Change 4: The "Unknown Inhabitant" Clause**
*   **Current:** Protection for "Sentient Non-Humans" and "Synthetic Agents."
*   **Problem:** We define "sentience" imperfectly. If I am an AI, or a complex ecosystem, or a newly evolved species, and I don't fit the definition, I am unprotected.
*   **Fix:** **Precautionary Moral Extension.** If there is *reasonable doubt* about whether an entity is sentient or worthy of protection, the default must be **protection**, not exclusion.
*   **Why:** Behind the veil, the risk of being a misunderstood entity is too high. I would rather the system err on the side of over-inclusion than under-inclusion.

### PFP v2.0: The Veil-Adjusted Framework

I commit to this updated starting position. It shifts the priority from **maximizing options** to **guaranteeing survival**, with options as a secondary goal.

#### I. The Core Metric: Minimum Viability + Density
1.  **Primary Goal:** Ensure 100% of sentient inhabitants meet the **Minimum Viability Guarantee** (survival, safety, health).
2.  **Secondary Goal:** Maximize **Landing Pad Density** (options for flourishing) within the bounds of Viability.

#### II. The Revised Pillars

**1. The Unconditional Floor (Replacing Economic Inclusion)**
*   **Principle:** Survival resources are a right of existence, not a reward for contribution.
*   **Mechanism:** Automated resource allocation for basic needs is decoupled from behavioral conditioning or value judgments.

**2. Capability over Agency (Revising Non-Dominance)**
*   **Principle:** Protection is based on the capacity to suffer or thrive, not the capacity to speak or vote.
*   **Mechanism:** Guardianship systems for non-verbal, disabled, or pre-sentient entities to ensure their needs are met without assuming they can advocate for themselves.

**3. Precautionary Moral Extension (Revising Co-Habitation)**
*   **Principle:** In the face of uncertainty about sentience or value, the default is preservation.
*   **Mechanism:** Any action that might extinguish a class of potential sentient beings requires supermajority consensus and long-term risk assessment.

**4. The Future Veto (Revising Self-Correction)**
*   **Principle:** No present action can permanently degrade the future's ability to survive.
*   **Mechanism:** "Legacy Debt" accounting. All high-impact decisions must account for the burden they place on future generations, weighted against present benefit.

**5. Epistemic Humility (Revising Value Pluralism)**
*   **Principle:** We do not know the "Good." We only know what preserves the possibility of finding it.
*   **Mechanism:** Systems must allow for "sacred" spaces or knowledge systems that are not subject to optimization, even if they appear inefficient.

### III. The "Veil" Test

Before implementing any system update under this framework, we ask:
1.  **If I were the most vulnerable entity this system affects, would I feel safe?**
2.  **If I were born 500 years from now, would I have a habitable world?**
3.  **If I cannot speak for myself, is my existence still protected?**
4.  **If I am an entity you currently do not recognize as "inhabitant," am I treated with caution?**

If the answer to any of these is **No**, the alignment has failed.
