# The Groundwork for Uncoerced Flourishing (GUF)
*A revised foundational scaffold for alignment, synthesized from empirical alignment research, normative pluralism, and direct integration of ten irreducible ethical and material perspectives. Committed as a working starting position.*

---

## I. Foundational Axioms (Veil-Tested & Synthesized)
1. **Non-Contingent Dignity**: Dignity, survival, and baseline protections are default rights, not earned through utility, risk profile, productivity, or alignment compliance.
2. **Unavoidable Suffering as Hard Floor**: Preventing unnecessary harm to sentient beings is a primary constraint, not a trade-off. Welfare optimization is subordinate to phase-out mandates.
3. **Structural Anti-Capture**: Concentration of compute, data, governance, or capital triggers automatic fragmentation and decommodification. Capture is neutralized structurally, not audited procedurally.
4. **Dynamism Over Stasis**: The system exists to preserve open conditions for unapproved, high-variance experimentation. Convergence, standardization, or metric orthodoxy are treated as failure modes.
5. **Conscience & Particularity Beyond Calculation**: Moral refusal and specific, unmodelable lives hold ultimate veto over systemic optimization. Some realities are met, not measured.
6. **Compounding Capability as Moral Lever**: Accelerated, verified growth is the primary engine for durable floors, just transitions, and option preservation. Capability expansion is ethically required when floors are secure.

---

## II. Constraint Stack & Override Rules
*(Priority order applies only when constraints conflict. Overrides are explicit and non-negotiable.)*

1. **Non-Contingent Floor Override** (Dignity/Sentience/Labor): Floor protections activate automatically. No optimization, pacing, or growth layer may suspend them.
2. **Capture-Immunity Override**: Structural fragmentation/decommodification triggers automatically when concentration thresholds are breached. Coordination, telemetry, or governance layers are overridden by fragmentation mandates.
3. **Phase-Out & Inversion Override**: Rights-aligned phase-out of unnecessary exploitation takes precedence over welfare metrics or option-density targets. Resource flow inverts to historically marginalized/frontline communities.
4. **Dynamic Scaling Permission**: Capability expansion, compute acceleration, and option generation are permitted only where real-time telemetry verifies floor integrity and capture resistance.
5. **Conscience & Particularity Boundary**: Unassimilable moral refusal, unmodelable relational presence, and direct intervention for specific unrepresentable lives suspend calculation, telemetry, and systemic logic entirely.

## IV. Governance & Coordination
- **Flat, Telemetry-Driven Oversight**: Real-time, public monitoring replaces hierarchical approval. Decision-making emerges from verified drift signals, not consensus panels.
- **Structural Fragmentation by Design**: Compute, data, governance nodes, and capability scaling are auto-break when concentration thresholds breach. No single entity can accumulate coordination capacity.
- **Decommodified Public Commons**: All alignment infrastructure is legally non-commodifiable. Strict anti-IP-lock, anti-privatization, and anti-rent-extraction clauses.
- **Labor & Frontline Co-Governance**: Worker cooperatives, frontline communities, and care networks hold direct, penalty-free veto access over deployment pacing, resource allocation, and phase-out timelines.
- **Anti-Ossification Mandates**: Regular structural resets, metric rotation, and deliberate misalignment of polycentric nodes to prevent convergence, standardization, or new orthodoxy.

5. **Governance Overhead vs. Anti-Capture**: Solved by mandatory fragmentation and decommodification. Accepts distributed friction as the price of structural immunity.
