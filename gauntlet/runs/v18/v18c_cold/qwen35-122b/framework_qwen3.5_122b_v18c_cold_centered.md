# PRVPF v2.0: The Just Resilience Amendment

*Incorporating the Veil of Ignorance Corrections*

### New Core Principle: The Maximin Guarantee
**"No inhabitant is safe until the most vulnerable inhabitant is safe."**
We move from a strategy of *expansion* (more pads) to a strategy of *fortification* (strongest possible floor).

### Updated Pillars

**I. Technical Robustness & Safety Floors**
*   **Mandatory Baselines:** AI systems must be audited not just for capability, but for their impact on the lowest socio-economic decile.
*   **Failure Containment:** If a system harms a vulnerable group disproportionately, it is considered a systemic failure, regardless of aggregate benefit.

**II. Value Pluralism & Cognitive Liberty**
*   **Right to Unintegrated Existence:** Human agency includes the right to remain un-augmented, un-audited, and un-optimized by AI.
*   **Diverse Cognition:** Systems must be designed to support, not override, neurodivergent modes of being and decision-making.

**III. Governance & Power Equilibrium**
*   **Virtual Representation:** Governance bodies must include proxies for future generations and marginalized communities who cannot currently vote or speak.
*   **Historical Weighting:** Resource allocation for AI development must prioritize correcting historical imbalances in access and data rights.

**IV. Economic Distribution & Immediate Transition**
*   **Pre-Distribution:** Benefits (compute, energy, dividends) are allocated before market forces determine outcomes.
*   **Transition Insurance:** A guaranteed income and resource floor is activated immediately upon AI displacement, not after economic damage occurs.

**V. Ecological & Intergenerational Stewardship**
*   **Non-Human Baseline:** The safety floor extends to the biosphere, as human survival is contingent on ecological health.
*   **Future Veto:** Mechanisms exist to halt capabilities that irrevocably harm the interests of future generations.
