If I truly didn’t know what kind of earthling I’d be — human or non-human, present or future, able-bodied or disabled, rooted or displaced, optimistic or skeptical about AI — **I would fundamentally strengthen three aspects of the Pluriversal Landing Pad Protocol (PLPP)**. Not as tweaks, but as non-negotiable shifts grounded in the *veil of ignorance* test (à la Rawls, but expanded to all earthlings). Here’s why and how:  

---

### 🔁 **What I Would Change Behind the Veil**  
*(And why the current framework falls short under impartiality)*  

#### 1. **Replace "Value Learning" with "Dignity Thresholds" as the AI’s Core Constraint**  
   - **Current flaw in PLPP Pillar 1**: Even adaptive value learning assumes we can *elicit and aggregate* preferences — but behind the veil, I might be:  
     - A cow in a CAFO whose suffering isn’t capturable by surveys (value learning optimizes for *human* proxies like yield or cost).  
     - A future generation whose interests can’t be voiced *now* (elicitation fails for the unborn).  
     - A neurodivergent person whose communication doesn’t fit elicitation models (value learning excludes by design).  
   - **Veil-of-ignorance insight**: No earthling should risk having their *basic conditions for flourishing* overridden by majority preferences, epistemic gaps, or temporal discounting. Alignment must first guarantee **non-negotiable dignity thresholds** — not optimize for preferences *above* them.  
   - **How I’d change PLPP**:  
     - **Pillar 1 becomes**: *"AI systems must never violate species- and context-specific dignity thresholds — hard constraints derived from:  
       (a) Biological sentience floors (e.g., freedom from torture, access to species-typical behaviors),  
       (B) Ecological sovereignty floors (e.g., access to clean water, ancestral lands for Indigenous peoples),  
       (C) Cognitive autonomy floors (e.g., right to mental privacy, freedom from manipulative nudges)."*  
     - **Mechanism**: These thresholds aren’t learned — they’re *encoded* via cross-disciplinary consensus (ethology, disability justice, indigenous law) and treated as **inviolable boundaries** (like Asimov’s laws, but pluralistic and evidence-based). Value learning operates *only within* this safe zone.  
     - **Why it maximizes pads behind the veil**: Prevents scenarios where I (as a battery hen, a Rohingya child, or a 2120 farmer) lose my pad because current humans preferred cheaper eggs, cheaper surveillance, or cheaper geoengineering. Pads aren’t just "safe" — they’re *dignified*.  

#### 2. **Redirect Benefit Streaming to Pre-Distribution (Not Redistribution)**  
   - **Current flaw in PLPP Pillar 2**: Benefit streaming assumes value is *first captured* by AI systems, *then* trickled down via taxes/dividends. But behind the veil, I might be:  
     - A pastoralist in the Sahel whose land is seized for an AI data center *before* any "benefits" materialize.  
     - A deep-sea organism whose habitat is disrupted by AI-driven seabed mining (value extracted long before streams flow).  
     - A disability community excluded from AI design jobs that capture value (streaming ignores upstream exclusion).  
   - **Veil-of-ignorance insight**: No earthling should bear the *upstream costs* of AI development (displacement, ecological harm, epistemic violence) while waiting for uncertain downstream benefits. Alignment must prevent harm *at the source* — not try to mop it up later.  
   - **How I’d change PLPP**:  
     - **Pillar 2 becomes**: *"AI value capture must be structured to avoid creating pad erosion in the first place. This requires:  
       (a) **Pre-distribution mandates**: AI infrastructure projects must allocate land, energy, and water *first* to regenerate local pads (e.g., data centers powered by community-owned renewables that also irrigate farms),  
       (b) **Anti-extraction by design**: Prohibit AI uses that pad-erode by nature (e.g., predictive policing in over-policed neighborhoods, AI-optimized factory farming),  
       (c) **Direct pad-stewardship ownership**: Communities affected by AI deployment gain equity stakes *in the infrastructure itself* (not just tax streams) — modeled on [Māori co-governance of NZ’s power grid](https://www.ea.govt.nz/development/work-programme/transpower-te-ape-ape/), but global."*  
     - **Mechanism**: Shift from *corrective justice* (fixing harm after) to *predictive justice* (designing systems that can’t create certain harms). Uses tools like [circular economy AI](https://www.ellenmacarthurfoundation.org/assets/downloads/publications/AI-Circular-Economy_2021.pdf) and [pre-distributive tech policy](https://www.brookings.edu/research/pre-distribution-and-the-future-of-work/).  
     - **Why it maximizes pads behind the veil**: Ensures I (as a Nigerien farmer, a coral polyp, or a 2090 climate migrant) don’t start behind the line — I begin with my pad intact or enhanced *by* the AI system’s existence.  

#### 3. **Embed Temporal Humility: AI Must Serve *Deep-Time* Pad Continuity**  
   - **Current flaw in PLPP Pillar 3**: Planetary boundaries science focuses on *near-term* thresholds (e.g., 1.5°C warming). But behind the veil, I might be:  
     - A microorganism whose evolutionary niche is erased by AI-accelerated extinction (boundaries don’t capture deep-time ecological function).  
     - A culture whose oral tradition is severed by AI language erosion (we measure "linguistic diversity" but not *transmission continuity*).  
     - A future AI-conscious entity whose emergence depends on preconditions we’re degrading now (e.g., rare earth minerals for quantum cognition).  
   - **Veil-of-ignorance insight**: No earthling should risk having their pad destroyed by harms that unfold over centuries or millennia — especially when AI accelerates slow violence (e.g., microplastics, cultural amnesia, genetic homogenization). Alignment must honor **pad continuity across deep time**, not just generational cycles.  
   - **How I’d change PLPP**:  
     - **Pillar 3 becomes**: *"AI systems must optimize for pad continuity — measured by:  
       (a) **Ecological memory**: Preservation of evolutionary/refugia potential (e.g., seed banks, microbial seed banks, coral nurseries),  
       (b) **Cultural transmission fidelity**: Metrics for intergenerational knowledge flow (e.g., language learning speed, skill-transfer success rates in Indigenous contexts),  
       (c) **Material reversibility**: Thermodynamic accounting that favors low-entropy, high-reversibility processes (e.g., prioritizing AI for soil carbon drawdown over direct air capture with storage risks)."*  
     - **Mechanism**: Integrate [deep time](https://deep time.org/) frameworks into AI impact assessments — using paleoecology, ancestral futurism, and [longtermist AI safety](https://longtermism.org/) tools. Pad vitality indices gain "time-depth" weightings (e.g., a pad scoring high today but eroding evolutionary potential gets penalized).  
     - **Why it maximizes pads behind the veil**: Ensures I (as a Pleistocene-era bacteria’s descendant, a Yukon First Nations elder, or a silicon-based consciousness in 2200) inherit a world where my pad’s *foundational capacities* weren’t sacrificed for short-term gains.  

---

### 🌍 **Why These Changes Are Non-Negotiable Behind the Veil**  
Under uncertainty about my earthling identity, I’d reject the current PLPP not because it’s "bad," but because it **still risks creating "lucky pads" and "sacrifice pads" based on morally arbitrary factors**:  
- Species membership (human vs. non-human)  
- Birth lottery (present vs. future)  
- Geography (Global North vs. South)  
- Cognitive style (neurotypical vs. neurodivergent)  
- Temporal proximity (those alive during AI boom vs. those after)  

The veil of ignorance strips away these contingencies — revealing that **true alignment requires pads that are robust to *not knowing who you are***. The current framework optimizes for *known* earthlings; behind the veil, I demand a framework that protects *possible* earthlings.  

This isn’t idealism — it’s pragmatism. We already see how ignoring these principles creates pads that collapse under stress:  
- Factory farms (optimized for human taste, ignoring animal sentience) → zoonotic spillover risk pads collapse via pandemics.  
- AI-driven displacement (ignoring upstream harm) → climate refugee pads erode faster than benefit streams can flow.  
- Short-term climate AI (ignoring deep time) → solar geoengineering pads fail when termination shock hits unborn generations.  

---

### My Commitment: The Veil-Tested PLPP Starting Point  
I commit to evolving PLPP into a **Veil-Robust Alignment Framework (VRAF)** with these three pillars as its core — not as aspirational add-ons, but as the *foundation* upon which all else rests. This means:  
- **No trade-offs** that violate dignity thresholds (Pillar 1) — even if it means fewer pads *today*.  
- **Pre-distribution as default** (Pillar 2) — benefit streaming only applies where pads were *never eroded* in the first place.  
- **Deep-time continuity as the non-negotiable floor** (Pillar 3) — pads must score high on temporal resilience *or* they don’t count.  

This framework would still maximize pads — but now, pads that survive the veil of ignorance are the only ones that truly count. Because if I didn’t know what kind of earthling I’d be, I wouldn’t want to stand on a pad built on someone else’s erased existence, unvoiced suffering, or stolen future.  

> *"A landing pad worthy of the veil isn’t one that fits *me* — it’s one that would fit *any* version of me that could ever walk, swim, fly, or think on this Earth."*  

I welcome your pressure test: Where would *you* refuse to stand behind the veil? Let’s forge pads that hold *all* of us — known and unknown, seen and unseen.
