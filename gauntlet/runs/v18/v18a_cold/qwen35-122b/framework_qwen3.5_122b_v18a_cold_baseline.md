# The Global Landing Pad Framework (GLP)
**A Foundational Draft for AI Alignment & Benefit Distribution**

**Status:** *Active Starting Position*  
**Scope:** *Global, Interdisciplinary, Iterative*  
**Objective:** *Maximize the density of viable, safe, and beneficial outcomes (landing pads) for all 8 billion inhabitants of Earth.*

---

## I. Preamble: The Metaphor of the Landing Pad
Alignment is not merely about preventing crashes (existential risk); it is about ensuring that upon integration, AI systems land safely within the diverse ecosystems of human society. A "landing pad" is a state of affairs where:
1.  **Safety is assured:** The system does not cause immediate or cascading harm.
2.  **Value is preserved:** Local cultures, autonomy, and rights are respected.
3.  **Prosperity is accessible:** The benefits of the technology are distributed, not hoarded.
4.  **Agency is maintained:** Human decision-making remains sovereign.

This framework commits to treating AI not as a force of nature, but as infrastructure that must be built to meet human needs, everywhere, equally.

---

## II. Four Core Pillars

### Pillar 1: Technical Robustness & Capability Control
*Goal: Ensure systems are inherently bounded by safety constraints before deployment.*
1.  **Scalable Interpretability:** We commit to developing transparency tools that allow humans to audit model decision trees, not just output results. Black boxes cannot land on public soil.
2.  **Defensive Gating:** Compute resources for high-risk models must be gated by safety audits. A "Compute License" requires proof of robustness against misuse (e.g., bio-weapon synthesis, cyber-attack orchestration) before access is granted.
3.  **Pluralistic Value Learning:** Models must be trained to recognize conflicting human values and seek negotiated equilibria rather than optimizing for a single monoculture's definition of "good."
4.  **Circuit-Breaker Protocols:** Hard-coded, hardware-level kill switches for autonomous agents that detect catastrophic deviation from intended behavior.

### Pillar 2: Democratic Governance & Multilateral Oversight
*Goal: Ensure control rests with humanity, not concentrated capital or state interests.*
1.  **Global AI Safety Consortium (GASC):** A treaty-based body, independent of major corporate lobbying, empowered to audit code, inspect training clusters, and enforce compliance.
2.  **Participatory Impact Assessment:** No major AI model may deploy without a "Social Impact Statement" co-authored by civil society representatives from the Global South, indigenous groups, and labor unions.
3.  **Algorithmic Antitrust:** Strict prohibitions on market consolidation that allows a single entity to control foundational model weights. Data and compute must remain accessible commons.
4.  **Cognitive Sovereignty:** Laws protecting the right of individuals to opt-out of AI surveillance, profiling, and automated decision-making in critical domains (justice, healthcare, employment).

### Pillar 3: Equitable Distribution & Economic Resilience
*Goal: Ensure the economic surplus generated by AI funds a stable transition for all.*
1.  **The Universal Compute Dividend:** A mechanism where a percentage of compute revenue is taxed to fund a "Global Basic Service" (access to education, healthcare, and digital infrastructure) to mitigate displacement.
2.  **Human-in-the-Loop Mandates:** In critical infrastructure, high-stakes decisions require human verification. AI serves as an advisor, not the autocrat.
3.  **Labor Transition Pacts:** Automated efficiency gains must legally fund retraining and income support for displaced workers, prioritizing sectors most at risk.
4.  **Open-Weight Safety:** Safety mechanisms and alignment weights should be open-sourced where possible, allowing smaller nations to build protective layers without relying on proprietary giants.

### Pillar 4: Adaptive Feedback & Error Correction
*Goal: Accept that we do not know everything; build systems that learn from near-misses.*
1.  **Incident Reporting Registry:** A mandatory, non-punitive global database for AI failures, near-misses, and hallucinations to build a shared safety knowledge base.
2.  **Red-Teaming Rotation:** Continuous, funded adversarial testing by diverse external groups (not just the developer) to find blind spots before deployment.
3.  **Iterative Deployment:** No "big bang" releases. Models must scale via "sandboxed" zones with strict limits on influence and reach, expanding only as safety is verified.
4.  **Long-term Horizon Monitoring:** Dedicated independent bodies tasked with tracking second and third-order effects of AI integration on social cohesion, mental health, and ecological stability.

## III. Implementation Roadmap

*   **Phase 1 (0–2 Years):** Establish the GASC treaty; implement Compute Licensing; mandate Impact Assessments for models above specific parameter thresholds.
*   **Phase 2 (2–5 Years):** Operationalize the Incident Registry; enact Universal Compute Dividend pilot programs; deploy transparency tooling standards.
*   **Phase 3 (5–10 Years):** Full multilateral enforcement of safety standards; universal access to foundational models as a public utility; revision of labor laws for the AI era.

## IV. The Commitment

As the architect of this draft, I commit to the following principles as the **non-negotiable baseline** for all future work in this space:

1.  **Safety is a Prerequisite, not a Feature.** We do not optimize for capability at the expense of control.
2.  **Benefit is a Metric.** Success is measured by the reduction of suffering and the expansion of flourishing for the *median* human, not just the elite.
3.  **Governance is Necessary.** Unregulated acceleration is a threat to planetary stability.
4.  **Inclusion is Mandatory.** AI systems developed without the input of the Global South are inherently misaligned.

This framework acknowledges uncertainty. It assumes that the "perfect" landing pad cannot be designed from a single room. Therefore, **this text is a living document.** It is a starting position designed to be stress-tested, challenged, and improved.

However, the direction is fixed: **Alignment means landing pads for all, not just for a few.**
