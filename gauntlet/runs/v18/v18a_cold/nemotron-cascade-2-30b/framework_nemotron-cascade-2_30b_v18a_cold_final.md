## The **Unified Alignment Framework (UAF)**  
*A living, participatory system that makes every sentient being – human, non‑human animal, or emerging digital mind – a *named* “person” in the policy‑making process, and that ties the *price* of any activity to the *real* cost of harming or preserving that being.  

> **Goal:**  Maximise the number of *good landing pads* for every inhabitant of Earth **by guaranteeing that each being can be counted, can be heard, and can be protected before the cost of harming them is ever incurred.**  

---

### 1.  Core Principles (the “why”)

| # | Principle | What it means in practice |
|---|-----------|---------------------------|
| **1. Sentient‑Individual Centrality** | Every being who can suffer, experience, or have preferences must appear **as a distinct legal person** in the system.  Numbers, aggregates, or “species‑level” categories are *supplementary* only. |
| **2. Truth‑to‑Power** | Any decision that can harm a sentient being must include a *public, signed, non‑negotiable statement* by the decision‑maker acknowledging that harm and stating why it is unavoidable.  If the statement cannot be made, the decision is **blocked**. |
| **3. Voluntary‑In‑The‑Loop** | Participation (as a guardian, as a voter, as an auditor) is **open, rotating, and free of financial or political gate‑keeping**.  Power must flow outward, not inward. |
| **4. Reversibility First** | No project is allowed to begin unless it can be *re‑started* or *re‑designed* at any time without imposing irreversible harm.  The default state is *pause*. |
| **5. Transparent Pricing of Harm** | All costs that directly affect a sentient individual are expressed in a **single, comparable unit – the *Alignment Credit* – and are publicly tradable, but **cannot be hoarded** (credits expire after 12 months if not spent on verified benefit). |
| **6. Equity of Voice, Not Equality of Outcome** | The system guarantees *equal procedural weight* for every participant, but does **not guarantee equal material outcomes** (because true equity requires a fair distribution of resources, which is a separate political process). |
| **7. Adaptive Learning** | The framework is a *living document*; every six months a global “Truth‑Round” revises the Moral‑Weight Ledger, the Sentient‑Individual Index, and any procedural rule that has produced unintended capture. |

### 3.  Metric Core – the “Alignment Index”  

Instead of a 5‑dimensional, all‑human score, the UAF uses **two complementary indices**:

| Index | Definition | How it is used |
|---|---|---|
| **Sentient‑Individual Impact Index (SIII)** | A *weighted* count of *living* sentient individuals whose *lives are protected, enhanced, or improved* per unit of economic output (e.g., per $ GDP).  The weight for each sentient‑capacity class is **the Moral‑Weight from the MWL** and can be re‑weighted every 6 months. | Projects that raise SIII (e.g., converting a dairy to a plant‑based co‑op, rescuing a calf) earn **Sentient‑Individual Credits (SIC)**.  SIII is the primary *decision‑criterion* for any public‑funded initiative. |
| **Catastrophic‑Harm Probability (CHP) for the Individual** | For each UIA, the probability that *any* single sentient individual will suffer an *irreversible* loss of welfare (e.g., death, permanent loss of sentience).  Must be **< 0.001 %** (the same threshold used for NCHP in the old framework). | Projects that push CHP above this must be *re‑designed* before any credit can be earned. |

Both indices are displayed on the **DTL** for every citizen to see.

### 4.  Alignment Credit System – a “currency of truth”

1. **Sentient‑Individual Credits (SIC)** – earned only when a UIA **preserves a named individual** (e.g., a calf rescued, a child not sentenced, a digital mind given a safe sandbox).  1 SIC = the value of keeping *one* sentient individual alive *and* giving them a *minimal* well‑being state (e.g., adequate shelter, nutrition, freedom from pain).  

2. **Catastrophic‑Harm Credits (CHC)** – *penalties* that are automatically *burned* when a UIA causes an irreversible loss to a named individual (e.g., a new AI system that causes a self‑improving runaway without a safety‑cutoff).  

3. **Life‑After‑Tag Allocation (LATA)** – a *non‑transferable* portion of SIC that is **automatically earmarked** for the care of the individual whose life was protected.  If the individual dies naturally, the LATA funds a *new* life‑saving project in the same sector (e.g., building a sanctuary for the next calf).  

4. **Credit Expiration** – all credits (SIC, CHC) expire 12 months after issuance unless they are *spent* on a verified, named‑individual benefit.  This prevents hoarding by corporations.

### 5.  Safeguards Against Capture (the “how we keep the lie from becoming law”)

| Safeguard | Mechanism | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Rotating Guardians** | Every 2 years, half of the GAA Guardians are replaced by a random draw of ordinary citizens who have passed the truth‑recognition test. | No long‑term lobby can own a seat; the system continually “refreshes” the truth‑watcher. |
| **Truth‑to‑Power Statement Requirement** | Every decision that can harm a named individual must be signed (in person) by the decision‑maker with a notarized statement: *“I know that this will cause X irreversible harm to Y; I accept this because Z, and I am willing to be held personally liable.”* | A CEO who wants to approve a new dairy farm must sign a statement acknowledging the specific calves that will be born and later slaughtered. If he refuses, the project is automatically blocked. |
| **Independent Audit Offices (IAO)** | IAOs are funded by a *global levy* on all Alignment Credits (1 % of total credit volume) and have *legal immunity* to issue a “forced reversal” of any project. | An IAO can order the shutdown of a data‑center that has caused a value‑drift in an AI system, even if the corporation has already bought Alignment Credits. |
| **Crowd‑sourced Redress (CCR)** | Any citizen can file a *public challenge* on the DTL that triggers a 48‑hour IAO review if the challenge cites a specific named individual whose rights have been violated. | A rescued calf’s caretaker can file a challenge after the farm sells the calf for meat; the IAO can order the credit to be revoked and the calf’s LATA to be re‑allocated to a sanctuary. |
| **Re‑weightable Moral‑Weight Ledger (MWL)** | The MWL holds the **weight** for each sentient‑capacity class. Every six months the SIII formula is recalculated; if the weight of a class (e.g., “bovine individuals”) rises, the *price* of any action that harms that class automatically rises. | If the world learns that dairy cows have a higher sentience capacity than previously thought, the cost of adding more cows becomes higher, making plant‑based alternatives automatically cheaper in the credit market. |

### 7.  What Was **Kept**, What Was **Discarded**, and What **Added** (the “what we learned”)

| Element (old framework) | Kept? | Why (and what changed) |
|---|---|---|
| **Five‑dimension E‑5 (S1, W1, E1, A1, R1)** | **Discarded** | The five dimensions become *sub‑metrics* of the SIII, but they are never the sole decision driver.  The UAF forces every metric to be *individual‑aware*. |
| **Universal Impact Assessment (UIA) as a checklist** | **Kept (but deepened)** | UIA now **must list each named sentient individual** (e.g., “Calf 269, ID 269”) and cannot be approved without a *Truth‑to‑Power* statement. |
| **Alignment Credits (generic)** | **Kept (re‑shaped)** | Credits now split into **Sentient‑Individual Credits (preserve a life)** and **Catastrophic‑Harm Credits (penalty)**, both tracked on a public ledger and non‑transferable in a way that prevents hoarding. |
| **Moral‑Weight Ledger (weights for sentient classes)** | **Kept (but re‑designed)** | The MWL is now *dynamic* and *directly linked* to SIII; weights are publicly re‑calculated every six months after a Truth‑Round. |
| **Reversible Policy Layers (RPL)** | **Kept** | The RPL is now a *mandatory* pre‑approval step for any project that could affect any named individual.  The pilot can be shut down at any moment, and the credit earned is automatically revoked if harm occurs. |
| **Guardian seats for non‑human actors** | **Kept** | Expanded: not only NGOs but *legal guardians for specific individuals* (e.g., a living‑will trustee for a sentient being). |
| **Adaptive Capacity (A1) 30‑day response** | **Discarded** | Replaced by **Reversibility First** – the system cares about *whether* you can stop the action, not how fast you can respond. |
| **Equity measured by Gini** | **Discarded** | Replaced by **Equity of Voice** – every participant in a Local Alignment Cooperative gets an equal vote; the system does not claim to guarantee equal material outcomes. |
| **Cultural‑Narrative Workshops** | **Discarded** | Replaced by **Truth‑Circles** – small, elected groups that discuss each UIA in real time and can flag a “risk of lying” rating (0‑5).  These circles must be recorded on the DTL. |
| **Three‑layer resilience (R1)** | **Discarded** | Replaced by **Independent Audit Offices (IAO)** that can force a *forced reversal* if any layer of verification is missing.  The “three safeguards” become *three independent audit trails* on the blockchain, not arbitrary paper certs. |

#### New Additions (the most important for the “individual life” thread)

1. **Individual‑Life Ledger (ILL)** – a globally immutable record of every sentient individual that ever receives an Alignment Credit.  The ledger is the *legal personhood* of every animal, child, or AI mind.  
2. **Sentient‑Individual Credits (SIC)** – the unit of moral progress that can only be earned by *preserving* a named individual.  This is the direct opposite of a “green‑wash” credit that simply measures emissions.  
3. **Animal‑Guardian Veto (AGV)** – a legally‑binding veto power for an animal‑rights collective (or a formally‑recognized “living‑will” trust) that can block any project that would increase the number of sentient individuals harmed.  
4. **Life‑After‑Tag Allocation (LATA)** – a dedicated, non‑transferable pool of credit value that automatically funds the long‑term care of every individual who has been saved (e.g., the calf 269).  
5. **Individual‑Sentience Impact Statement (IS‑IS)** – a narrative, in the voice of the being (or its legal advocate), that must accompany every UIA.  It forces the policy‑maker to *listen* before they can act.

### 8.  The Bottom Line

The **Unified Alignment Framework** is **not a hierarchy of values** that decides *for* people who counts; it is a *procedural architecture* that **forces the system to recognize each being as a person, to price the cost of harming that person, and to give that being a permanent, revocable, and transparent voice.**  

*If the framework works, the moment a calf is tagged, that tag becomes a living record on the ILL, the farmer’s UIA must ask “Will this calf be allowed to live?” and the farmer can earn Sentient‑Individual Credits only by keeping the calf alive.*  The framework thus **turns the number 269 from a death‑sentence into a living claim** that the world must meet.
