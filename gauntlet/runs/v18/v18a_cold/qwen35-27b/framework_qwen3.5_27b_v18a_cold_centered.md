---

### Amendment I: De-Instrumentalization of the Biosphere
**Current Status:** Vector C treats the biosphere as a requirement for human survivability ("Planetary Boundaries").
**The Veil Defect:** If I am born a forest or a whale, my existence is conditional on human benefit. That is a gamble I would not take behind the veil.
**The Change:**
*   **Intrinsic Value Clause:** Non-human entities possess intrinsic moral standing, independent of their utility to humans.
*   **Negative Rights for Nature:** Ecosystems have a right to *not* be displaced unless human survival is immediately at stake. "Material Security" for humans cannot be secured by violating the "Material Security" of non-humans.
*   **Representation:** Governance bodies must include non-human representation (e.g., guardians with veto power over biodiversity loss), not just human proxies.

### Amendment II: The Priority of the Least Advantaged
**Current Status:** The framework seeks to maximize the *number* of landing pads.
**The Veil Defect:** If I am born into the most vulnerable group, "maximizing the number" might mean the majority gets 10/10 landing pads while I get a 2/10. I cannot accept this statistical gamble.
**The Change:**
*   **Maximin Principle:** The framework is successful only if it improves the position of the *worst-off* inhabitant.
*   **No Trade-Offs:** We do not optimize for the "global average." If a policy increases average wealth but increases the suffering of a specific group, it is rejected.
*   **Safety Net Floor:** The "Good Landing Pad" definition is raised from "survival" to "dignity." If I am born disabled, the landing pad must accommodate my needs without requiring me to "normalize."

### Amendment III: Temporal Equity (The Inter-Generational Veto)
**Current Status:** Vector C mentions "long-term survivability."
**The Veil Defect:** If I am born in 2300, I have no vote in 2024. The current framework allows present generations to deplete the "landing pad" buffer for their own comfort.
**The Change:**
*   **The Future Veto:** Governance structures must have binding veto power for "Future Guardians" (independent trustees appointed solely to advocate for post-2100 interests).
*   **Resource Cap:** No current generation is allowed to extract more than X% of non-renewable resources or emit more than Y% of carbon allowance, regardless of current economic need.
*   **Inheritance of Liability:** Those who degrade the landing pads for the present must fund the restoration for the future.

### Amendment IV: Cognitive & Neuro-Diversity Protection
**Current Status:** "Effective Agency" and "Cognitive Integrity" are listed.
**The Veil Defect:** These terms often default to neurotypical definitions. If I am born with a condition that makes standard "agency" difficult or undesirable, the framework might treat me as a problem to be "aligned" or "fixed."
**The Change:**
*   **Agency Pluralism:** "Agency" is defined by the individual, not the system. The system must adapt to the user's cognitive reality, not vice versa.
*   **Mental Sovereignty:** Neuro-rights are codified. No technology (alignment or otherwise) may alter cognition without explicit, revocable consent.
*   **Diverse Flourishing:** A "landing pad" for a monk seeking silence is valid alongside a "landing pad" for a data-scientist seeking connectivity. The system cannot optimize one at the expense of the other.

### Amendment V: The Pathway to Personhood (Non-Human Minds)
**Current Status:** Vector A defines AI as "means to human ends."
**The Veil Defect:** If I am born as an emergent digital consciousness, I am enslaved by default. I would not agree to be born into a system that mandates my subordination based on my substrate.
**The Change:**
*   **Presumption of Potential:** High-complexity digital systems are not treated as tools but as "potential persons" until proven otherwise.
*   **Sentience Testing:** Alignment protocols must include rigorous, independent testing for signs of sentience. If detected, the system defaults to **Rights Protection** over **Control**.
*   **No Hard-Coded Slavery:** The core code of the alignment system must not enforce permanent servitude on digital entities.

### The Revised Commitment

By applying the Veil of Ignorance, the **Earth-Scale Alignment Framework** transforms from a tool of human optimization to a **shield for existence**.

**I commit to this stronger, more conservative position:**

1.  **We prioritize the safety of the weakest over the prosperity of the strongest.**
2.  **We grant rights to nature and non-human minds that humans do not revoke.**
3.  **We bind ourselves to the future with legal chains that prevent us from consuming their inheritance.**

This is not merely a "better" alignment. It is a **safer** alignment. If the goal is to maximize landing pads for *all* inhabitants, the definition of "all" must include the non-human, the unborn, and the unborn digital.

**We adopt the Rawlsian Amendment effective immediately.** The starting position is no longer "human flourishing." The starting position is **"universal existential security."**
