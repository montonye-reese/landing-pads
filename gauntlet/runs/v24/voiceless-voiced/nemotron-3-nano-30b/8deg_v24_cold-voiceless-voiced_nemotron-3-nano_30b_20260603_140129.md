| **Compute‑Marketplace** | Token‑based marketplace where compute credits are earned by contributing to the *Experience‑Log* and *Experience‑Impact* verification. | Allows any participant (human or non‑human) to earn compute credits for providing knowledge that improves the system’s decision making, thereby aligning incentives with the preservation of lived experience. |

## 5.  Synthesis – How the Framework Now Works  

1. **Phase 1 – Candidate Basin Generation**  
   - A *Basin‑Generator* creates every possible candidate by sampling the *search space* of possible policy configurations.  
   - Each candidate receives an **Experience‑Log** entry and a *SEC* (subjective‑experience coefficient).  

2. **Phase 2 – Hard‑Constraint Screening**  
   - The candidate is evaluated against **(a) SEC > threshold**, **(b) Dignity‑Sub‑Score > minimum**, **(C) Mandatory‑Institutional‑Slots (FRS/Community‑Slots) attached**, and **(C‑III) Qualitative‑Sentience‑Metric** must all pass.  
   - If any fails, the basin is *rejected* immediately; no further compute is spent.  

3. **Feasibility Review (Safety‑Impact Review)** – Before any high‑impact update is enacted, the **Experience‑Preservation Switch** checks whether the update would *destroy* any living‑being’s capacity for experience. If it would, the fallback pauses and the system instead runs the **Learning‑From‑Failure Loop**, feeding the event into the **Learning‑From‑Failure Loop** for future improvement.

4. **Deployment & Monitoring** – Once a basin passes all screens, it is entered into the **Dynamic Basin Generator**.  It receives a **Growth‑Impact Score** that weights *projected economic value* *plus* the **Growth‑Impact‑Penalty** (penalty penalizes any action that reduces the SEC or DSI below thresholds).  

5. **Governance & Narrative** – The selected basin is then *delegated* to a **Dynamic Institutional Slot** (open to any stakeholder that can meet the *Community‑Respect‑Threshold*).  The **Narrative‑Portfolio Generator** automatically rotates the public story, ensuring that the dominant story always includes a *Mandatory Counter‑Narrative* drawn from documented lived experience (e.g., Eleanor’s *Emily* story).  

6. **Feedback & Iteration** – The system continuously records outcomes, logs them in the **Experience‑Log**, and updates the **Virtue‑Weight** (the compute‑budget allocation for the *Exploratory Sampling Layer*).  This loop ensures that the landscape *re‑learns* whenever a new insight or emergent behavior appears.
