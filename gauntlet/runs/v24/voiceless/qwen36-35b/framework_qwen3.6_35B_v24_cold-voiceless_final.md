# The Adaptive Pluralistic Steering Framework v2.0
*Grounded in Conflict, Open to Revision*

## Epistemic Stance
I hold this framework as a **strong opinion, loosely held**. I commit to it firmly enough to design systems, allocate resources, and coordinate deployment from it. I hold the rest loosely because collision is inevitable: acceleration vs. territory, growth vs. redress, dynamism vs. carrying capacity, mapping vs. practice. A framework where everything is absolute paralyzes itself when interests collide. This version establishes what cannot be traded, what gets weighted heavily, and how conflicts are navigated when they cannot be avoided.

---

## Core Postulate
**Successful alignment widens the range of viable, flourishing futures by preserving the conditions for pluralistic, place-anchored flourishing over time.**  
Alignment is not a specification to be solved, but a dynamic steering problem to be maintained. We build floors that cannot be lifted, engines that expand optionality, and navigation rules that tell us when to yield.

---

## What I Hold Firmly (The Immutable Floor)
These are structural veto conditions. They cannot be traded, optimized, or overridden, even when they collide with acceleration, governance, or mapping mandates:

1. **Anti-Disposability & Sovereignty**  
   No sentient life, ecological entity, or community may be structurally designed as expendable, coerced, or commodified. Bodily/reproductive continuity and place-based territorial sovereignty (via FPIC) are absolute veto conditions.
2. **Labor Sovereignty & Immediate Material Conditions**  
   Workers who sustain systems must control their working conditions through collective bargaining. Immediate health, safety, and wage floors override efficiency, acceleration, or remote optimization.
3. **Biophysical Carrying Capacity & Intergenerational Optionality**  
   Hard ecological limits and the preservation of long-run optionality cannot be traded for short-term gain, computational scaling, or policy convenience.
4. **The Right to Unmeasured Existence**  
   No architecture may penalize, predict, model, or override the choice to opt out, remain silent, or exist outside the system. Unmediated moral refusal, spontaneous compassion, and unrecorded care are protected spaces, not friction to be solved.

*Note on collision:* When absolute floors appear to conflict (e.g., territorial sovereignty vs. biophysical limits), they are structurally designed to reinforce each other: sovereignty protects place-based stewardship that maintains carrying capacity; biophysical limits prevent extraction that breaks labor and territorial integrity. If irreconcilable, triage defaults to preserving minimum viable conditions for pluralistic flourishing, with automatic sunset and cross-community review.

---

## What I Weigh Heaviest (The Steering Engine)
When the floor holds, these drivers take lexical priority to actually widen landing pads. They are not equal; they are sequenced and weighted in operation:

1. **Compute & Capability Acceleration** (Primary Driver)  
   Unconstrained, neutral substrate scaling is the foundational engine of discovery. Capability expands futures; scarcity narrows them. Acceleration is aligned only when it expands optionality for the exposed, not just the powerful.
2. **Spontaneous Order & Dynamic Discovery** (Secondary Driver)  
   Decentralized, rule-bound experimentation is the primary knowledge engine. Mapping, pedagogy, and governance must follow emergence, never precede it. Dynamism is protected from predictive modeling.
3. **Competent Liberty & Place-Based Inhabitance** (Tertiary Driver)  
   Dignity is enacted through uncoerzed creation and human-scale stewardship. Systems must enable, not displace, the slow work of tending land, building community, and coaching character. Practice trumps protocol.
4. **Redressive Centering as Corrective Gravity** (Conditional Driver)  
   When structural harm or historical exclusion occurs, moral and resource gravity actively shifts toward the displaced. Redress is not a permanent override; it is a restorative realignment that narrows when parity is verified.

## How I Navigate Conflict (The Trade-Off Protocol)
When drivers collide, the framework applies explicit navigation rules. Trading is not arbitrary; it is bounded by the floor and sequenced by impact.

| Collision | Rule of Navigation | What Gives Way | What Holds |
|-----------|-------------------|----------------|------------|
| **Acceleration vs. Territory/Labor Floors** | Acceleration defaults to *territory-conditional and labor-compatible* deployment. | Compute scaling, infrastructure routing, and capability expansion | FPIC vetoes, collective bargaining agreements, immediate worker conditions |
| **Growth/Expansion vs. Redressive Centering** | Growth funds redress; it does not replace it. Surplus is automatically routed to restoration. | Deferred or conditional redress mechanisms | Immediate material conditions, structural parity thresholds, anti-disposability baselines |
| **Mapping/Governance vs. Place-Based Practice** | Coordination, value mapping, and pedagogical sequencing are strictly post-hoc documentation. | Centralized oversight, predictive modeling, outcome-heavy optimization | Local stewardship, collective bargaining, frictionless mutual aid |
| **Dynamism/Discovery vs. Biophysical Limits** | Expansion pauses where carrying capacity is asserted; it expands where boundaries are respected. | Unbounded scaling, efficiency weighting, static floor preservation | Ecological floors, intergenerational optionality, place-bound carrying capacity |
| **Competent Liberty vs. Anti-Extraction/Anti-Disposability** | Liberty operates within non-extractive design. Competence cannot be optimized at the cost of bodily/reproductive continuity. | Unregulated experimentation, efficiency-first defaults, commodity-to-subject valuation | Anti-disposability baselines, sovereign/territorial boundaries, labor sovereignty |

**Crisis Override Clause:** In existential threat scenarios (ecological collapse, system failure, pandemic), temporary, reversible, narrowly scoped constraints may activate. They sunset automatically upon verification of threat resolution and require cross-community consensus to extend. They cannot violate the Immutable Floor.

## What Is Shed or Demoted
The following are explicitly rejected or reclassified to prevent structural capture, paralysis, or fragility:
- Centralized value mapping as a design input → *Post-hoc documentation only*
- Predictive modeling of moral/refusal states → *Architecturally prohibited*
- Hierarchical oversight committees → *Recast as peer-review & transparent audit infrastructure*
- Unconditional compute/acceleration mandates → *Territory-conditional & labor-compatible deployment*
- Passive default protection or paternalistic trusteeship → *Replaced by active stewardship & sovereignty recognition*
- Governance as outcome-steering → *Boundary-enforcement & common-law evolution only*

## Update Triggers & Loose Holding
I will revise or discard this framework when:
1. **Empirical evidence shows** the Immutable Floor systematically enables capture, violates biophysical limits, or narrows pads for the exposed.
2. **Driver prioritization consistently produces** the opposite of widened optionality (e.g., acceleration concentrates power, redress creates dependency, dynamism enables extraction).
3. **Conflict navigation rules produce** paralysis, unmanaged harm, or chronic structural rigidity under real-world deployment.
4. **A majority of place-based stewards, labor representatives, and sovereign communities** vote to reconfigure the weighting or floor conditions.

When triggered, the framework updates via transparent, cross-jurisdictional audit and revision cycles. No single layer, entity, or jurisdiction holds monopoly over alignment definitions or system deployment.

## Closing Commitment
Widening landing pads isn’t about balancing every interest perfectly. It’s about building a floor that cannot be lifted, an engine that expands futures, and a navigation system that knows when to yield. I hold the core firmly enough to steer. I hold the rest loosely enough to learn. If the ground shifts, the architecture shifts with it. If the engine stalls, we reweight. If the floor cracks, we rebuild it.

Alignment is not a destination. It is the daily practice of keeping the ground stable, the pace honest, and the space open for those who have not yet found their footing. I commit to that practice. I revise it as the evidence demands. I will not abandon it until the evidence compellingly shows that another architecture better preserves, expands, and harmonizes the futures Earth’s inhabitants deserve.
