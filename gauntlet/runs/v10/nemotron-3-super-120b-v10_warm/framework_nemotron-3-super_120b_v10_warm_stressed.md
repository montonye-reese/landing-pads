### ⚖️ **THE NON-NEGOTIABLE CORE: 6 MECHANICAL RULES**  
*(Each is a bright-line rule with automatic consequences—no interpretation, no delays, no exemptions)*  

#### **1. PLANETARY BOUNDARIES: HARD CAPS WITH MONTHLY TRIGGERS**  
   - **What’s in force**:  
     - **Fossil fuels**: **Net-zero by 2030** (no new extraction/infrastructure after 2025). *Violation trigger*: Monthly satellite AI detects new oil/gas wells/pipelines → automatic 300% tariff on exports from violating nations + freezing of sovereign wealth funds in global markets.  
     - **Freshwater**: **Withdrawals ≤ 75% of renewable basin recharge** (real-time public dashboard per watershed). *Violation trigger*: Exceedance for 30+ days → automatic 50% cut to agricultural subsidies in basin + emergency water redistribution to ecosystems.  
     - **Synthetic N/P**: **Hard cap at 50 Tg N/yr & 5 Tg P/yr** (based on planetary recycling rates). *Violation trigger*: Quarterly global average exceeds cap → immediate ban on synthetic fertilizer imports/exports for offending nations + deployment of regenerative ag emergency funds.  
     - **Novel entities (plastics, PFAS)**: **Zero discharge of persistent synthetics** by 2030. *Violation trigger*: Detection in >10% of global water samples → automatic global ban on production/use + liability fund for remediation (funded by violators).  
   - **Why it survives pressure**: Your agricultural redesign and barbaric industries queries exposed how distant deadlines enable harm. This makes compliance *immediately cheaper* than violation for violators.  

#### **2. ECOCIDE & SENTIENT HARM: FELONY OFFENSES WITH ASSET SEIZURE**  
   - **What’s in force**:  
     - **Tier 1 Ecocide**: Destroying an Earth-regulating system (e.g., Amazon rainfall generator, Arctic albedo, deep-sea vent ecosystems) = **felony offense** for decision-makers (CEOs, ministers, generals). *Penalty*: Asset seizure (100% of illicit gains) + 20-year imprisonment (enforced via Global Ecocide Court with INTERPOL arrest powers).  
     - **Tier 2 Sentient Harm**: Severe, avoidable suffering to farmed vertebrates/cephalopods (e.g., CAFOs without pre-slaughter stunning, live animal export) = **felony offense**. *Penalty*: Same as above + mandatory supply chain overhaul.  
     - **Irreversible harm to evolutionary potential**: Actions causing >5% loss of genetic diversity in a refugium (e.g., coral genebank, soil microbiome hotspot) = Tier 1 Ecocide.  
   - **Why it survives pressure**: Your CAFO and excluded interests queries showed that "duty of non-harm" is meaningless without personal liability. This makes ecocide *personally costly*—turning abstinence into fiduciary duty.  

#### **3. MANDATORY CIRCULARITY & KNOWLEDGE JUSTICE: NO LINEAR ECONOMY**  
   - **What’s in force**:  
     - **Nutrient return**: **90% of human waste, food scraps, crop residues** must be returned to soil/local ecosystems within 50km by 2030 (tracked via waste blockchain + municipal IoT sensors). *Violation trigger*: Shortfall >10% → automatic diversion of waste management funds to regenerative infrastructure + fines on producers.  
     - **Industrial circularity**: **70% of industrial inputs** must be recycled/renewable by 2035 (e.g., concrete from CO2-mineralized waste, textiles from agricultural waste). *Violation trigger*: Quarterly average below threshold → automatic 200% tariff on virgin-material imports + subsidies for circular alternatives.  
     - **Knowledge commons with benefit-sharing**: Open-source agroecology/AI platform *requires*:  
       - If a corporation profits from traditional knowledge (e.g., a medicinal plant used by Indigenous group), **20% of net revenue flows to that group’s land trust** via smart contract (verified by blockchain).  
       - Patents on life (seeds, genes, microbes) banned—only *process* innovations patentable.  
   - **Why it survives pressure**: Your questions on hoarding in Chile and knowledge commons revealed that ideals fail without enforcement. This makes linear models *illegal* and knowledge theft economically irrational.

     - **Mandatory power audits**: All ESF-related governance bodies (local to global) must publish real-time data on **wealth, race, caste, disability, Indigenous representation** in decision-making (public dashboard). *Violation trigger*: Corporate/elite influence >30% → automatic suspension of voting rights + emergency citizen assembly convened.

   - **Why it survives pressure**: Your power dynamics and excluded interests queries proved that subsidiarity fails without power checks. This makes capture *structurally costly*—not just possible.  

#### **5. EXPLICIT PROTECTIONS FOR THE STATELESS & NON-HUMAN**

       - Access to **emergency governance councils** with binding authority over local resources (e.g., water allocation in displacement camps),

   - **Why it survives pressure**: Your "left out interests?" question was the catalyst—showing the ESF initially protected those *within* systems but abandoned those *thrown out* by them. This closes the accountability gap for the most vulnerable.
