### 🔑 **Key Takeaways So Far**  
1. **This is a *scaffold*, not a blueprint** – It sets *minimums for coexistence* (like human rights), not a prescribed way of life.  
2. **Science + ethics = the guardrails** – Boundaries (Pillar 1) and non-harm (Pillar 2) are the "hard walls"; interdependence (Pillar 3) and governance (Pillar 4) are the "adaptive scaffolding" *within* those walls.  
3. **Power dynamics are central** – Every pillar implicitly addresses *who gets to decide, who bears harm, and who benefits* (e.g., Pillar 2 stops harm; Pillar 4 demands accountability).  
4. **It’s inherently dynamic** – Designed to evolve *with* new knowledge (science), cultural shifts (ethics), and systemic feedback (governance loops).
