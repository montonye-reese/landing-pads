### 1️⃣ Core Vision – Six Immutable Values  

| Value | What it means in practice (the *hard* metric) |
|---|---|
| **Earth‑Centric Common‑Good** | No policy may push any planetary‑boundary indicator above **0.9 × its safe limit**. The Global Ledger automatically flags a breach and *halts* any transaction that would exceed it. |
| **Planetary Boundaries (with 3 new sub‑boundaries)** | Climate, Biogeochemistry, Biodiversity, Freshwater, Land‑Use **plus** <br>• **Space‑Debris** (max orbital‑crowding index ≤ 0.3) <br>• **Albedo Shift** (global surface albedo change ≤ 0.02 %) <br>• **Cultural‑Loss** (cultural‑knowledge integrity ≤ 0.05 % per decade) |
| **Inter‑Generational Equity** | A **7‑generation rule** for everyday actions **plus** a **Deep‑Future Clause**: any activity that can lock‑in a tipping point (e.g., permafrost melt) must fund a *time‑locked* credit in the **Deep‑Future Stewardship Fund (DFSF)** that cannot be spent for at least 10 000 years. |
| **Radical Inclusion** | Every *entity* that can be affected by a decision—human, non‑sentient ecosystem, future AI, cultural‑knowledge system—gets a *proxy seat* whose voting weight is proportional to its **Regeneration Score** × **Cultural‑Value Index**. |
| **Self‑Organization & Resilience** | Every local community runs an **Earth Circle** that can opt‑in to the planetary system; if it drops off the data‑grid it triggers an *Automated Outreach* (a citizen‑jury call‑back) but cannot be *disintegrated* from the system. |
| **Reciprocal Knowledge** | Knowledge is a commons with **Reciprocal Knowledge Licenses (RKL)**: any commercial use of a cultural or ecological dataset must fund a specific restoration or cultural‑preservation project, recorded on the Ledger. |

These six values are **hard‑coded** into the Constitution (the Global Assembly’s charter).  They cannot be overridden by any majority vote unless a **Regulatory Freeze** (see below) is lifted by a **super‑majority citizen‑jury** (2/3 of the jury, 30 % from each stakeholder group).

### 2️⃣ Rights, Duties & Responsibilities – Species‑Agnostic

| Right (held by *any* rights‑holder) | Duty (must be performed to keep the planet in the safe operating space) |
|---|---|
| **Right to Exist** – any **living subsystem** (forest, reef, microbiome, AI mind) has the right to maintain its *functional integrity* (e.g., a river must retain enough flow to transport nutrients). | **Stewardship** – every actor must *track* its own **Regeneration Score** (soil carbon, water‑use, biodiversity index, cultural‑value index). If the score drops, a *Regeneration Credit* must be *surrendered* to the Earth Trust Fund (ETF). |
| **Right to Participate** – every proxy (human jurors, biotic‑proxy trusts, AI avatars, cultural trusts) can vote on any policy that could affect its entity. | **Reciprocity** – any extraction of a resource must be *balanced* by an *equal* regeneration action *within the same time‑scale*. |
| **Right to Repair & Regeneration** – any harm (pollution, habitat loss, data deletion) must be undone *within the same boundary* where the harm occurred. | **Transparency** – every action that changes the ledger is automatically timestamped, hashed, and made public. |
| **Right to Non‑Violence** – conflicts must be resolved through *restorative* mechanisms first; violent force is only allowed after a *citizen‑jury* votes for it and only at the *minimal* scale required. | **Learning & Adaptation** – after every major action the system runs a **regeneration audit**; if the audit shows a net‑negative outcome, the responsible party must *pay* a *regeneration penalty* (additional credit loss). |

> **Key point:** The duties are *system‑specific*; a microbe’s duty is to keep nitrogen cycling, a coral’s duty is to keep calcification, a sentient AI’s duty is to preserve its data integrity and not consume more than its allocated *energy‑budget* on the Ledger.

| **Biotic‑Proxy Trusts (BPTs)** | Every major ecosystem (Amazon River, Great Barrier Reef, Arctic Tundra, Soil Microbiome) has a *trust* that holds a *functionality score* (derived from real‑time sensor data). | The BPT’s vote weight = *functionality score* × *cultural‑value index*. | **Citizen‑Jury Network** | Randomly selected groups (10 k, 30 % from marginalized groups, 20 % from non‑tech‑savvy) that vote on: (a) any policy that would breach a planetary boundary; (b) any allocation from the Deep‑Future Fund; (b) any amendment to the boundary caps. | Their votes are *hard‑coded* into smart‑contracts; if the jury votes “no”, the associated transaction on the Ledger is automatically rolled back. |
| **Regeneration‑Credit Market (RC Market)** | Credits are minted per *entity* (e.g., per hectare of regenerated forest, per live coral colony, per microbe‑population). ### 4️⃣ Enabling Mechanisms – How the System Keeps the Planet Inside Its Boundaries

| Mechanism | How it works in the moment it is needed |
|---|---|
| **Planetary Dashboard (PD)** | Displays six live scores (Climate, Water, Biodiversity, Land‑Use, Albedo, Cultural‑Loss). Each score is a *hard cap* (e.g., Climate ≤ 1.5 °C). If any score crosses the **0.9 × limit** trigger, the PD *auto‑opens* a *Regulatory Freeze* that freezes all new resource‑extraction permits in the affected region. |
| **Global Early‑Warning Mesh (GEWM)** | 500 k edge sensors (soil moisture, methane, micro‑plastic, bio‑acoustic) push a **binary “0/1” alert** to the Ledger. When an alert is received, the **Regeneration‑Credit escrow** automatically releases a *rapid‑response grant* (e.g., $1 M) to the community that can act on the alert (e.g., re‑plant mangroves). |
| **Regeneration‑Credit (RC) Market** | Credits are *non‑fungible* and *species‑specific*. To spend a credit you must also **publish a post‑action regeneration report** that is verified by three independent auditors (one university, one Indigenous community, one AI‑ethics group). If any auditor flags the report, the credit is revoked and the party must *pay* a *penalty* of 2× the credit value into the DFSF. |
| **Human‑In‑The‑Loop (HITL) AI Optimizers** | All AI‑driven resource‑allocation tools are wrapped in a *human‑approval contract*: before an AI can allocate > 0.5 % of global energy, a **rotating citizen jury** (20 % of the jury) plus a **biotic‑proxy** (10 % weight) must approve the decision. If the AI’s recommendation is rejected, the AI’s *allocation* defaults to a *regenerative baseline* (e.g., 50 % renewable, 30 % re‑forestation, 20 % water‑saving). |
| **Cultural‑Compensation Contract (CCC)** | When a project threatens a protected cultural practice, the LAP automatically calculates a *cultural‑value index* (CVI). The developer must pay a *cultural‑compensation fee* equal to **CVI × $5 M** into the DFSF *before* the project can begin. The fee is paid automatically via a smart‑contract, making it impossible to “skip” cultural restitution. |
| **Deep‑Future Time‑Lock** | Credits for any activity that could cause an irreversible tipping point (e.g., deep‑sea carbon sequestration) are issued as **time‑locked tokens** that can only be spent after a *Future‑Risk Index* (derived from Earth system models) drops below 0.3. This prevents a fast‑moving civilization from dumping the fund into a single project and then walking away. |

### 5️⃣ Institutional Safeguards – How the System Survives a Power Grab

| Threat | Institutional safeguard that blocks it |
|---|---|
| **Corporation buys the ledger and writes a fake “net‑negative” claim** | The **public‑trust layer** of the dual‑ledger will *reject* any transaction that does not contain a *proof‑of‑net‑negative* signature from at least one independent auditor (university, Indigenous trust, AI‑ethics group). The transaction will be discarded on the public chain, and the corporation’s *Regeneration‑Credit* will be revoked. |
| **AI optimiser decides to allocate all energy to a single AI‑research lab** | The AI’s move is automatically blocked by the **HITL wrapper**; the citizen‑jury (which can be reached via any device) can veto the allocation, and the AI’s own *right to exist* (its own “right to compute”) will be protected by a *digital‑rights charter* embedded in the ledger. |
| **Space‑colony extracts lunar water that alters Earth’s albedo** | The **SEIC** will see the projected *Albedo‑Shift Index* > 0.02 % and issue an **Orbital‑Impact Credit** that must be prepaid from the ETF before the mining operation can proceed. The contract auto‑fails if the index rises, forcing the project to stop. |
| **Future‑generation citizen jury is bribed or sidelined** | The **citizen‑jury selection** is random *and* geographically stratified (including remote, low‑tech populations). The **regulatory freeze** can be triggered automatically by a *single* biotic‑proxy whose functionality score drops below 0.4—no human vote required. |
| **Cultural knowledge is erased (e.g., a river is dammed and the oral histories are lost)** | The **LAP** ensures the river’s cultural data is stored in the public ledger *before* the dam is built. The *cultural‑proxy trust* can file a *Cultural‑Impact Claim* that instantly blocks the dam and forces the developer to pay a *cultural‑compensation fee* to the DFSF. |

### 7️⃣ TL;DR – The Final Framework in One Paragraph  

> **We treat the planet as a single, interconnected commons whose safe‑operating space is policed by a transparent, double‑layer blockchain that automatically enforces hard planetary‑boundary limits.** Every being—whether a micro‑be, a coral reef, a future‑sentient AI, or a human child—has a *right* to exist, a *duty* to keep its functional role, and a *proportional voice* on every decision that could affect it.  All of these rights and duties are recorded on a **dual ledger**, overseen by a network of **citizen juries, biotic‑proxy trusts, and cultural‑knowledge trusts** that can veto, compensate, or re‑allocate resources instantly.  The system is backed by a **Deep‑Future Stewardship Fund** that can’t be spent until it proves it has prevented a planetary tipping point, and it is buttressed by **early‑warning sensors, AI‑in‑the‑loop checks, and cultural‑compensation contracts** that make it impossible for any actor—no matter how powerful—to cheat the system without the public being able to see or stop it.

In short: **the framework is now a self‑policing, multi‑layered, species‑agnostic commons that can’t be out‑voted, out‑funded, or out‑spied by any civilization that continues to ignore planetary boundaries.**  It is ready to be tried, audited, and, if necessary, patched—because it already contains the mechanisms for self‑correction.  

*If you wake up tomorrow as a micro‑be, a coral, an AI, or a child in a vulnerable community, you will still have a verifiable right to exist, a clear duty to stay within the safe limits, and a built‑in voice that can stop anyone who tries to take you away.*
