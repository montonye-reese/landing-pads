This is **Terran Covenant Version 2.2: The Resilient Fortress**.

It is no longer a manifesto. It is a **survival architecture** that has been stress-tested against economics, politics, security, and human psychology. It retains the soul of the original vision but wears the armor required to protect it.

Here is exactly what is in the framework now:

### I. The Core Constitution (Immutable)
*   **Name:** The Terran Covenant.
*   **Vision:** Shift from Nation-State Sovereignty to Planetary Stewardship.
*   **The Six Pillars:**
    1.  **Dignified Existence:** Guaranteed food, shelter, health, and **Universal Design** (accessibility by default).
    2.  **Planetary Stewardship:** Rights of Nature, Carbon Caps, Regenerative Land Leases.
    3.  **Cognitive Sovereignty:** Protection from algorithmic manipulation, Truth mandates.
    4.  **Equitable Justice:** Restorative justice, **Right to Movement** (no capacity caps on climate refugees).
    5.  **Cultural Pluralism:** Protection of local traditions within planetary bounds.
    6.  **The Transition Shield:** Absolute Income/Caloric Guarantee during the 10–20 year transition phase.

### II. The Physical Infrastructure (Symbiotic Food Grid)
*   **Tier A (Rural):** Regenerative Commons (Agroforestry, Indigenous Stewardship).
*   **Tier B (Urban):** Bio-Hubs (Vertical Farming, Closed-Loop Waste).
*   **Tier C (Industrial):** Synthetic Labs (Cellular Meat, Precision Fermentation).
*   **Rule:** 100-mile consumption radius. Zero-waste mandate.
*   **Energy:** Dependent on decentralized renewable grids.

### III. The Economic Engine (True Cost Accounting)
*   **Currency:** Decoupled from extraction. Measured by Human Flourishing.
*   **Prohibitions:** No commodities speculation. No single-use plastics. No industrial slaughter.
*   **Funding:** **Asset Appropriation**. Transition costs funded by seizing assets of "Barbaric Industries" (Fossil Fuels, Ad-Tech, Factory Farming), not by debt or taxes.
*   **Pricing:** Prices include environmental and social externalities.

### IV. The Governance Model (Federated Mesh)
*   **Structure:** Local Nodes (Daily Life) + Global Commons Council (Air, Oceans, Space, Pandemics).
*   **Selection:** Sortition (Random Selection) for Council members to prevent elite capture.
*   **Adaptability:** **Evolutionary Clause** (Decadal reviews).
*   **Trigger:** **Ignition Clause**. Automatic activation upon climate/economic crisis thresholds to bypass sovereignty veto power.

### V. The Security & Defense Amendment (The Fortress)
*   **Defense:** **Critical Infrastructure Security**. No offensive army, but control over the "Green Tech Stack" (Energy, Food, Data) to deter invasion.
*   **Deterrence:** Non-compliant actors lose access to global reserves/infrastructure.
*   **Privacy:** **Zones of Disconnection**. Right to opt-out of surveillance grid.
*   **Threat:** Acknowledges the **Technology Race** risk. Prioritizes defensive resilience over offensive speed.

### VI. The Ethical Boundaries (The Inclusion Patch)
*   **Personhood:** **Consciousness Spectrum Law**. Rights graduated by cognitive capacity (includes AI/Advanced Animals).
*   **Disabilities:** **Universal Design Mandate**. Infrastructure must be accessible by default.
*   **Religion:** **Conscience Exemptions**. Protection for non-scientific traditions (within safety limits).
*   **Generations:** **Legacy Clause**. Transition cannot sacrifice the current generation for the future one.

### VII. The Risks Acknowledged (The Shadow Side)
*   **Sovereignty:** States will resist (Counter: Ignition Clause).
*   **Enforcement:** No global army (Counter: Resource Interdiction & Tech Control).
*   **Capital:** Transition costs (Counter: Asset Appropriation).
*   **Surveillance:** Privacy loss (Counter: Zones of Disconnection).
*   **Time:** Collapse before transition (Counter: Legacy & Vulnerability Guarantee).
