---

This is no longer a "foundational framework" in the sense of a set of laws. It is a **Civilizational Permissive Structure**. 

Its purpose is not to manage humanity toward a predetermined destination, but to establish the absolute moral, biological, and economic baselines required for a fragmented, competitive, and flawed species to coexist and accelerate without triggering its own extinction.

I call this **The Covenant of Regenerative Agency.**

---

### I. The Moral Bedrock: The Axiom of Sentient Interdependence
The framework begins with one truth: **No entity flourishes in isolation.** The well-being of the individual is a function of the health of the system.

**The Great Reckoning:** We acknowledge that the historical category of "The Human" was built on a foundation of exclusion, animalization, and "zoological witchcraft." Therefore, this framework does not seek to "expand the circle of humanity," but to **dismantle the hierarchy of the resource.** We move from a "Human-Centric" model to a **"Sentient-Centric"** model.

**The Sacred Trust:** We view the restoration of the biosphere and the cessation of suffering not as a "regulatory goal," but as an act of **Atonement**. We are not mere managers of a planet; we are trustees of a life-support system we have nearly broken.

---

### II. The Non-Negotiable Baselines (The Floor)
To ensure that agency is not a luxury of the elite, the Covenant establishes a **Universal Launchpad**. This is the "Veil of Ignorance" made manifest.

1.  **The Universal Basic Provision (UBP):** An unconditional, global entitlement to the physical floor: nutrient-dense food, clean water, climate-controlled shelter, and comprehensive healthcare. This is not a "safety net" to prevent misery, but a **Launchpad for Agency**, freeing the individual from survival-fear so they may contribute to the whole.
2.  **The Abolition of Property Status:** Sentient beings are not "resources" to be managed or "stewards" to be cared for; they are individuals. The legal status of sentient beings as "property" is abolished. The goal is the total obsolescence of the "animal-as-resource" logic.
3.  **The Anti-Externalization Principle:** "Barbarism" is defined as any action that achieves a local gain by pushing the cost onto another sentient being or the biosphere. Systemic externalization is treated as a theft from the future.

---

### III. The Architecture of Enablement (The Structure)
The framework rejects central planning (the "Clerisy") in favor of a **Modular Federation of Nodes**.

1.  **Modular Autonomy:** The world is organized into bio-regional nodes. Each node is free to experiment with its own social and economic customs, provided they do not violate the **Non-Negotiable Baselines**.
2.  **The Just Transition Bridge:** Recognizing that millions are embedded in "barbaric" systems (e.g., animal agriculture), the framework establishes **Pivot Grants**. These are not handouts, but structural bridges that fund the transition of labor, assets, and land from extractive industries to regenerative ones.
3.  **Public-Private Symbiosis:** We recognize that while the state/collective provides the "platform" (foundational research, the UBP, and infrastructure), the individual provides the "spark." In return for the public risk taken to enable a breakthrough, the resulting "Zero to One" value is shared as a **Public Common**.

---

### IV. The Dynamics of Growth and Agency (The Velocity)
The framework does not seek a "steady state" (stagnation), but **Regenerative Acceleration**.

1.  **The Prestige Economy of Restoration:** To channel the biological drive for status and dominance (the Apex Drive), the framework pivots the source of prestige. Status is derived not from *accumulation* or *dominance*, but from **Systemic Enhancement**—the measurable restoration of biodiversity or the elevation of others.
2.  **Zones of Exception:** To prevent mimetic conformity and protect the "exceptional," the federation maintains **Experimental Enclaves**. In these zones, the "Covenant" is voluntarily suspended for the purpose of high-risk, vertical innovation. They are sanctuaries for the contrarian and the visionary, provided their experiments do not permanently damage the biosphere.
3.  **Regenerative Growth:** We replace GDP with **Qualitative Growth Metrics**. Success is measured by the increase in sentience-wide flourishing and the resilience of the planetary ecosystem.

---

### V. The Evolution Loop (The Calibration)
The Covenant is a living document, governed by **Epistemic Humility**.

*   **Outcome-Based Accountability:** We do not measure success by adherence to these rules, but by the **actual outcomes** of the inhabitants.
*   **Continuous Calibration:** A perpetual cycle of **Observation $\rightarrow$ Dialogue $\rightarrow$ Adjustment**. 
*   **The Rescue Mandate:** If a modular node’s outcomes fall below the Universal Floor, the Federation is obligated to intervene—not as a conqueror, but as a recovery team—to restore the baseline of dignity.
