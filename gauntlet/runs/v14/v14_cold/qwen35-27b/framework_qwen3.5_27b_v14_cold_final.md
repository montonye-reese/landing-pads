### PREAMBLE

**WE, THE INHABITANTS OF THE PLANET EARTH,**

Recognizing that our existence is inextricably linked to the stability of the biosphere;  
Acknowledging that the current trajectory of consumption, conflict, and division threatens the continuity of complex life;  
Affirming that national borders are administrative artifacts that cannot supersede planetary physics;  
Understanding that survival requires the surrender of certain freedoms in exchange for security, yet demanding that survival does not mean suffering;  
**Acting from behind a Veil of Ignorance**, not knowing who we will be, what we will lose, or when we will live;  
**Recognizing that justice is not just about the future, but about the past, and that the people who built the world must be able to save it**;  

**DO HEREBY ESTABLISH** this Covenant as the foundational operating system for human civilization.

We acknowledge that this Framework is not a promise of a perfect world, but a protocol for a survivable one. It prioritizes continuity over variety, collective security over absolute individual liberty, and scientific consensus over cultural tradition where they conflict. It also ensures that the price of survival is not paid in human dignity.

We further acknowledge that this system must not become a new tyranny. Power must be decentralized. Truth must be open. And dissent must be protected.

### ARTICLE I: THE CENTRAL AXIOM (PHYSICS)

**1.1 Primacy of Life Support**  
The integrity of the planetary life-support system (atmosphere, hydrosphere, biosphere, geosphere) is the supreme law. No economic treaty, national security policy, or cultural tradition shall hold precedence over the maintenance of systems required for human and non-human survival. **This is non-negotiable.**

**1.2 The Closed-Loop Mandate**  
Earth is a finite system. All human activity must function within the regenerative capacities of the planet. There is no "away" place for waste. There is no "extra" resource.

**1.3 The Definition of Success**  
Civilizational success is redefined from "Growth of GDP" to "Quality of Continuity." A civilization is successful only if it can sustain itself indefinitely without degrading the capacity of its children to do the same.

**1.4 The Non-Enforcement Clause**  
While the Central Axiom is supreme, **compliance must not be enforced by a central authority**. The Covenant relies on **market incentives**, **local autonomy**, and **community accountability**, not global police forces. (Havel/Butler)

### ARTICLE II: ECONOMIC ARCHITECTURE (STRUCTURE)

**2.1 The Metric: Decoupled Growth & Integrity Index (GII)**  
*   **Standard:** GDP remains as a measure of activity, but **Taxation and Subsidy** are tied to the **Genuine Integrity Index (GII)**.
*   **Replacement:** GII measures Human Well-being + Ecosystem Health – Resource Depletion.
*   **Enforcement:** Entities that increase wealth while degrading the GII face **Carbon Pricing and Subsidy Withdrawal**, not bans. Entities that increase wealth *and* GII receive **Tax Incentives**. (Tyler Cowen/Jensen Huang)

**2.2 The Currency: Energy & Efficiency Backed**  
*   **Backing:** Terran Credits (TC) are backed by **verified renewable energy capacity** and **verified material throughput**.
*   **The Cap:** The money supply expands with **efficiency breakthroughs** (e.g., Fusion, Recycling). It does not cap absolute output if efficiency is high. (Jensen Huang)
*   **The Debt Limit:** Interest-based debt that necessitates perpetual extraction is prohibited.

**2.3 Ownership: Co-Operative Stewardship**  
*   **Concept:** Resources are **not owned** by individuals or corporations. They are **Stewed** by **Worker Co-Operatives**.
*   **Requirement:** 50% of all "Stewardship Trusts" must be owned and governed by the **workers** who manage them. (Dolores Huerta/Laura Reese)
*   **Distribution:** Profits are allocated: 40% Reinvestment, 40% Wages, 20% Planetary Restoration Fund.

**2.4 Production: Right to Durability & Innovation**  
*   **Ban:** Planned obsolescence is prohibited.
*   **Design:** All consumer goods must be designed for **50+ years of functionality**, fully repairable, and fully recyclable.
*   **Liability:** Manufacturers hold a **Material Lease License**. They own the atoms, selling the *service*, and are responsible for end-of-life recycling.
*   **Innovation:** **Iterative Sprints** (10-year renewable charters) replace Centennial Mandates to allow for rapid adaptation to new technology. (Jensen Huang)

**2.5 Distribution: Universal Basic Services & Resource Dividend**  
*   **The Floor:** Energy, water, food, housing, healthcare, and education are provided as public utilities (guaranteed to all).
*   **The Flourishing Standard:** Access to **art, play, leisure, and spiritual practice** is guaranteed to prevent a life of mere survival.
*   **Plant-Based Default:** The Life Floor provides **Plant-Based Food** as the standard. Animal products are excluded from public funding unless necessary for medical reasons. (Earthling Ed/Laura Reese)
*   **The Dividend:** Every human receives a **Carbon Dividend** and **Resource Dividend** derived from the capped resource budget.
*   **Incarcerated Inclusion:** The Life Floor explicitly includes **Incarcerated Persons** inside prison walls. Access to food, water, healthcare, and education must match the Life Floor outside. (Bryan Stevenson)

**2.6 Subsidy Reallocation (The Farmer's Path)**  
*   **Transition Fund:** 100% of current subsidies for animal agriculture and extraction must be redirected to **Regenerative Plant-Based Infrastructure** within 10 years.
*   **Grants:** Farmers transitioning from livestock to plant-based receive **Grant Funding** for retooling, not just taxes for leaving. (Laura Reese)
*   **Co-op Support:** 50% of grant funding must support **Worker-Owned Co-Operatives**, ensuring farmers own their transition. (Dolores Huerta)

### ARTICLE III: RIGHTS & DIGNITIES (THE PEOPLE)

**3.1 Ecological Standing & Sentience**  
*   **Standing:** Non-human entities possess legal standing. Violation of **Planetary Boundaries** is a crime against continuity.
*   **Individual Rights:** Explicit protection for **all sentient beings** (mammals, birds, fish, cephalopods, artificial intelligence). (Earthling Ed)
*   **No Cultural Loopholes:** "Tradition" is not a justification for causing **unnecessary suffering**. If a practice harms sentient beings, it must change. (Earthling Ed)

**3.2 Conscious Dignity & Labor**  
*   **Autonomy:** Every sentient being possesses the right to bodily autonomy and freedom from coercion.
*   **Right to Organize:** **Unionization, Collective Bargaining, and the Right to Strike** are explicitly protected. (Dolores Huerta)
*   **Worker Power:** Workers hold **20% of Seats** in all "Local Community" councils. No "Community" without the workers. (Dolores Huerta)

**3.3 Resource Equity**  
*   **The Floor:** Every inhabitant is guaranteed the **Life Floor** (Article II.5).
*   **Poverty is Not a Violation:** Individuals living in poverty are not penalized for exceeding **Sustainability Ceilings** if resources are unavailable. (Bryan Stevenson)
*   **The Ceiling:** No individual may consume more than the **Sustainability Ceiling**, but the system must provide alternatives to avoid penalizing the poor.

**3.4 Epistemic Integrity (Truth)**  
*   **Science:** Scientific data regarding existential threats is public domain. Obfuscation is prohibited.
*   **Watch Transparency:** "The Watch" (Article IV.2) is **Open Source Code**. No one owns the data. (Smedley Butler)
*   **Algorithmic Ban:** Algorithms cannot be used for **Criminal Enforcement**, **Hiring**, or **Parole**. (Bryan Stevenson)

**3.5 Intergenerational & Ancestral Stewardship**  
*   **Future:** Future generations are recognized as legal stakeholders.
*   **Past:** A **Reparative Fund** (10% of Planetary Restoration) addresses historical harm (Environmental Racism, Colonialism, Slavery). (Bryan Stevenson)
*   **Debt:** Financial, ecological, and social debt may not be transferred to the unborn.

**3.6 The Right to Dissent (The Exit Clause)**  
*   **Conscientious Objection:** Inhabitants may **refuse to participate** in the Covenant's mandates (e.g., "The Pledge") without penalty, provided they do not harm others. (Václav Havel)
*   **Exit:** The Covenant acknowledges the **Right to Exit**. One may leave a Covenant region and live outside its boundaries without penalty, even if it is harder.
*   **Watch Limitation:** The Watch is **Advisory Only**. It cannot enforce compliance or trigger "Red Line Protocols" unilaterally. (Václav Havel)

### ARTICLE IV: GOVERNANCE & ENFORCEMENT

**4.1 Polycentric & Nested Structure**  
*   **Local First:** Decisions are made at the most local level possible. Global bodies manage planetary boundaries.
*   **Regional Resilience:** Regions must be **self-sufficient** in food, energy, and critical goods to survive if others defect.

**4.2 The Watch (Advisory & Transparent)**  
*   **Function:** An independent, **Open Source** AI-driven monitoring system tracks the Five Pillars in real-time.
*   **Enforcement:** The Watch provides **Data**, not **Orders**. Compliance is driven by **Market Exclusion** (trading partners boycott non-compliant regions) and **Local Audit**. (Smedley Butler/Václav Havel)
*   **Public Oversight:** Any citizen can run the Watch's code to verify the data.

**4.3 Red Line Protocols (Market-Driven)**  
If Planetary Boundaries are breached:
*   **Phase 1:** **Automatic Carbon Taxes** increase on offending entities (Market Signal).
*   **Phase 2:** **Subsidy Withdrawal** for non-compliant sectors.
*   **Phase 3:** **Suspension of non-essential industrial activity** (Triggered by **Global Assembly**, not Watch).

**4.4 Strategic Sovereignty (Defense Clause)**  
*   **Necessity:** Covenant states may maintain **military parity** and **critical resource stockpiles** to defend against non-compliant "Side B" threats.
*   **War-Time Exception:** In the event of existential invasion, Article I.1 (Central Axiom) is temporarily suspended for **National Defense**, provided restoration cost is paid post-conflict.
*   **Limit:** **No Standing Armies** funded by private contractors. Defense is **Local Militia** based. (Smedley Butler)
*   **Defense Budget:** Must be audited by the **Global Assembly** (Article VI.2).

**4.5 Humanitarian Corridors**  
*   **Unconditional Lifelines:** No trade embargo or resource sanction may block access to **medicine, food, or emergency aid** for vulnerable populations (children, elderly, refugees, incarcerated) regardless of the state's political stance.
*   **Delivery:** Aid must be delivered by **Neutral Third Parties** to prevent regime exploitation.

### ARTICLE V: ACKNOWLEDGED CONSTRAINTS & TRADE-OFFS

**5.1 Cultural Particularity**  
*   **Local Self-Certification:** Communities may define "Low Impact" thresholds, provided they remain within the global budget. External audits are only triggered if global boundaries are breached.
*   **Sentience Limit:** "Low Impact" does not permit the **unnecessary suffering** of sentient beings. (Earthling Ed)

**5.2 Negative Liberty**  
*   **The Ceiling:** The right to consume beyond the Sustainability Ceiling is abolished.
*   **The Exit:** You are free to live, but only within the limits of the biosphere. If you wish to live outside those limits, you may leave, but you bear the cost. (Václav Havel)

**5.3 The Transition Pain**  
*   **20-Year Transition:** Extraction industries are grandfathered for 20 years with declining quotas.
*   **70% Tax on Extraction:** Wealth from these industries is taxed at 70% to fund the **Life Floor** for displaced workers. (Smedley Butler)
*   **Reparative Transition:** 10% of this fund goes specifically to communities historically damaged by extraction (Black, Brown, Indigenous). (Bryan Stevenson)

**5.4 Anti-Tyranny Safeguards**  
*   **Sunset & Recall:** All Covenant institutions have a **10-year sunset provision**. Any entity that exceeds power limits can be recalled by a global citizen assembly.
*   **Watch Override:** The Watch cannot override democratic decisions without supermajority approval (75%). (Václav Havel/Smedley Butler)

**5.5 Knowledge Boundaries**  
*   **Safety Buffer:** Planetary Boundaries are set at 80% of estimated safe limits to account for scientific uncertainty.
*   **Historical Data:** "Safe Limits" must include historical data on environmental racism and colonial harm. (Bryan Stevenson)

### ARTICLE VI: AMENDMENTS & EVOLUTION

**6.1 Regional Resilience**  
The Covenant can be adopted regionally. A region that adopts the Covenant must be **self-sufficient** in food, energy, and critical goods to survive if others defect.

**6.2 The Living Document**  
This Covenant is not static. It shall be reviewed every **10 years** by a randomly selected global assembly of citizens and subject matter experts.
*   **Representation:** **Farmers, Workers, Incarcerated Persons, and Indigenous Communities** must hold **30% of Seats** in this assembly. (Dolores Huerta/Bryan Stevenson)

**6.3 The Evolutionary Clause**  
New discoveries (interplanetary colonization, new sentient species) shall be integrated without violating the Central Axiom.

**6.4 Sunset Provision**  
If humanity achieves a sustainable equilibrium for 100 consecutive years, this document shall be reviewed for dissolution into organic cultural norms.
