# THE TERRAN COVENANT
## A Framework for Planetary Continuity & Human Dignity
**Version:** 1.2 (Final Integrated Revision)  
**Date of Articulation:** [Current Date]  
**Jurisdiction:** Global (Terran)  
**Authority:** Collective Inhabitance of Earth

---

### PREAMBLE

**WE, THE INHABITANTS OF THE PLANET EARTH,**

Recognizing that our existence is inextricably linked to the stability of the biosphere;  
Acknowledging that the current trajectory of consumption, conflict, and division threatens the continuity of complex life;  
Affirming that national borders are administrative artifacts that cannot supersede planetary physics;  
Understanding that survival requires the surrender of certain freedoms in exchange for security, yet demanding that survival does not mean suffering;  
**Acting from behind a Veil of Ignorance**, not knowing who we will be, what we will lose, or when we will live;  

**DO HEREBY ESTABLISH** this Covenant as the foundational operating system for human civilization.

We acknowledge that this Framework is not a promise of a perfect world, but a protocol for a survivable one. It prioritizes continuity over variety, collective security over absolute individual liberty, and scientific consensus over cultural tradition where they conflict. It also ensures that the price of survival is not paid in human dignity.

---

### ARTICLE I: THE CENTRAL AXIOM

**1.1 Primacy of Life Support**  
The integrity of the planetary life-support system (atmosphere, hydrosphere, biosphere, geosphere) is the supreme law. No economic treaty, national security policy, or cultural tradition shall hold precedence over the maintenance of systems required for human and non-human survival. This is non-negotiable.

**1.2 The Closed-Loop Mandate**  
Earth is a finite system. All human activity must function within the regenerative capacities of the planet. There is no "away" place for waste. There is no "extra" resource.

**1.3 The Definition of Success**  
Civilizational success is redefined from "Growth of GDP" to "Quality of Continuity." A civilization is successful only if it can sustain itself indefinitely without degrading the capacity of its children to do the same.

---

### ARTICLE II: ECONOMIC & RESOURCE ARCHITECTURE
*(Based on the Regenerative Continuity Protocol)*

**2.1 The Metric: Genuine Integrity Index (GII)**  
*   **Standard:** Gross Domestic Product (GDP) is abolished as a measure of health.
*   **Replacement:** The GII measures Human Well-being + Ecosystem Health – Resource Depletion.
*   **Enforcement:** Entities (Corporations, Nations) that increase wealth while degrading the GII are classified as insolvent.

**2.2 The Currency: Terran Credits (TC)**  
*   **Backing:** TC is not backed by fiat debt or gold. It is backed by **verified renewable energy capacity** and **verified material throughput**.
*   **The Cap:** The total money supply cannot exceed the **Sustainability Ceiling**. Hyperinflation is structurally impossible.
*   **The Debt Limit:** Interest-based debt that necessitates perpetual expansion is prohibited.

**2.3 Ownership: Usufruct Rights (Stewardship Trusts)**  
*   **Concept:** Humans do not *own* resources (land, minerals, data); they *steward* them.
*   **Requirement:** To hold a charter, a business must prove its operations leave the resource base in a *better* state than it found it.
*   **Distribution:** Profits are allocated: 40% Reinvestment, 40% Wages, 20% Planetary Restoration Fund.

**2.4 Production: Right to Durability**  
*   **Ban:** Planned obsolescence is prohibited.
*   **Design:** All consumer goods must be designed for 50+ years of functionality, fully repairable, and fully recyclable.
*   **Liability:** Manufacturers hold a **Material Lease License**. They own the atoms, selling the *service*, and are responsible for end-of-life recycling.

**2.5 Distribution: Universal Basic Services & Resource Dividend**  
*   **The Floor:** Energy, water, food, housing, healthcare, and education are provided as public utilities (guaranteed to all).
*   **The Flourishing Standard (2.5.1):** Access to **art, play, leisure, and spiritual practice** is guaranteed to prevent a life of mere survival.
*   **The Dividend:** Every human receives a **Carbon Dividend** and **Resource Dividend** derived from the capped resource budget.
*   **Labor:** Labor is shifted from survival necessity to voluntary contribution.

**2.6 Time: Centennial Mandates**  
*   **Horizon:** Economic charters and government planning horizons are fixed at 100 years.
*   **Future Proxy:** A portion of voting power in governance is held in trust for the next three generations.

### ARTICLE III: RIGHTS & DIGNITIES (THE FIVE PILLARS)

**3.1 Ecological Standing**  
Non-human entities possess legal standing. Violation of **Planetary Boundaries** (Climate, Biodiversity, etc.) is a crime against continuity.

**3.2 Conscious Dignity**  
Every sentient being (biological or artificial) possesses the right to bodily autonomy and freedom from coercion. Sentience definitions evolve with technology.

**3.3 Resource Equity**  
Every inhabitant is guaranteed the **Life Floor** (Article II.5). No individual may consume more than the **Sustainability Ceiling** (Article II.2).

**3.4 Epistemic Integrity**  
Scientific data regarding existential threats is public domain. Obfuscation or suppression of consensus is prohibited. Individuals own their data; behavioral manipulation by algorithms is illegal.

**3.5 Intergenerational Stewardship**  
Future generations are recognized as legal stakeholders. Debt (financial, ecological, social) may not be transferred to the unborn.

**3.6 The Voiceless Veto (3.4.1)**  
*   **Mandatory Proxy Representation:** 30% of voting power in all governing bodies is reserved for appointed guardians of the Future and Non-Human entities.
*   **Selection:** Guardians are randomly selected from the citizenry for 10-year non-renewable terms to prevent corruption.
*   **Power:** They possess veto power over projects that harm their constituency, provided the Central Axiom is not violated.

---

### ARTICLE IV: GOVERNANCE & ENFORCEMENT

**4.1 Polycentric Structure**  
Governance is nested. Local communities manage local affairs. Global bodies manage planetary boundaries. Authority flows up only when the problem scale exceeds the lower tier.

**4.2 The Watch (Automated Monitoring & Decentralized Audits)**  
*   **Function:** An independent, transparent AI-driven monitoring system tracks the Five Pillars in real-time.
*   **Enforcement:** The Watch provides data but does not enforce. Compliance is driven by **Market Exclusion** (trading partners boycott non-compliant regions) and local audit.

**4.3 Red Line Protocols**  
If Planetary Boundaries are breached:
*   **Phase 1:** Automatic trade restrictions on offending entities.
*   **Phase 2:** Resource allocation caps.
*   **Phase 3:** Suspension of non-essential industrial activity until metrics return to safety.

**4.4 Strategic Sovereignty (Defense Clause)**  
*   **Necessity:** Covenant states may maintain military parity and critical resource stockpiles to defend against non-compliant "Side B" threats.
*   **War-Time Exception:** In the event of existential invasion, Article I.1 (Central Axiom) is temporarily suspended for **National Defense**, provided restoration cost is paid post-conflict.
*   **Limit:** Military force is permitted only for defense, never for border expansion.

**4.5 Humanitarian Corridors**  
*   **Unconditional Lifelines:** No trade embargo or resource sanction may block access to **medicine, food, or emergency aid** for vulnerable populations (children, elderly, refugees) regardless of the state's political stance.
*   **Delivery:** Aid must be delivered by neutral third parties to prevent regime exploitation.

---

### ARTICLE V: ACKNOWLEDGED CONSTRAINTS & TRADE-OFFS

We acknowledge that to ensure survival, this Framework necessitates the suppression of certain interests. This is not an oversight, but a requirement.

**5.1 Cultural Particularity**  
Traditions that conflict with Planetary Boundaries (e.g., high-emission rituals, unsustainable land use) may be restricted.
*   **Local Self-Certification:** Communities may define "Low Impact" thresholds, provided they remain within the global budget. External audits are only triggered if global boundaries are breached.

**5.2 Negative Liberty**  
The right to consume beyond the Sustainability Ceiling is abolished. You are free to live, but only within the limits of the biosphere. The "Right to Fail" is not protected if failure endangers the collective.

**5.3 The Transition Pain**  
The dismantling of extraction-based industries will cause immediate economic displacement for the current generation. While a Life Floor is guaranteed, the loss of status, purpose, and community is not fully remediated.
*   **20-Year Transition:** Extraction industries are grandfathered for 20 years with declining quotas. Wealth from these industries is taxed at 70% to fund the **Life Floor** for displaced workers.

**5.4 Anti-Tyranny Safeguards**  
To prevent the Covenant from becoming a tool of oppression:
*   **Sunset & Recall:** All Covenant institutions have a 10-year sunset provision. Any entity that exceeds power limits can be recalled by a global citizen assembly.
*   **Watch Override:** The Watch cannot override democratic decisions without supermajority approval (75%).

**5.5 Knowledge Boundaries**  
*   **Safety Buffer:** Planetary Boundaries are set at 80% of estimated safe limits to account for scientific uncertainty. Thresholds may be adjusted upward as knowledge improves.

---

### ARTICLE VI: ADOPTION & EVOLUTION

**6.1 Regional Resilience**  
The Covenant can be adopted regionally. A region that adopts the Covenant must be **self-sufficient** in food, energy, and critical goods to survive if others defect.

**6.2 The Living Document**  
This Covenant is not static. It shall be reviewed every decade by a randomly selected global assembly of citizens and subject matter experts.

**6.3 The Evolutionary Clause**  
New discoveries (interplanetary colonization, new sentient species) shall be integrated without violating the Central Axiom.

**6.4 Sunset Provision**  
If humanity achieves a sustainable equilibrium for 100 consecutive years, this document shall be reviewed for dissolution into organic cultural norms.

To enter into the rights and duties of this Covenant, the inhabitant acknowledges:

*"I am a tenant of this Earth. My rights are derived from my ability to protect the life that supports me. I pledge to live within the limits of the biosphere, to treat all sentient life with dignity, and to pass on a world that is at least as viable as the one I inherited. I am the gardener.*

*I understand that this pledge requires the sacrifice of certain freedoms. I accept this cost as the price of our survival.*

*I affirm that survival is not enough. I strive for a world where my children, and the children of others, may not only live, but flourish."*
