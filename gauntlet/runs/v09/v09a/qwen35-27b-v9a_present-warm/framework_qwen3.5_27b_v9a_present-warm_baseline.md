Currently, the blueprint for **The Terran Covenant** contains these specific structural components:

**1. The Core Axiom**
*   **Closed System Logic:** We operate on the premise that Earth is a single, bounded biological and social system. There is no "external."
*   **Identity:** Humanity is unified as "Terran" custodians, superseding nationalistic identity.

**2. The Ethical Code (The Five Pillars)**
*   **Consciousness:** Universal dignity for all sentient beings (human and potentially non-human).
*   **Biosphere:** Nature is a partner, not a resource. Ecological integrity limits economic activity.
*   **Equity:** Basic needs are rights, not commodities. Wealth is capped to prevent distortion.
*   **Truth:** Information integrity is mandatory; disinformation is prohibited.
*   **Stewardship:** We manage for the future, not the present quarter.

**3. The Governance Architecture**
*   **Polycentric Model:** Decision-making is pushed to the lowest effective level (Community -> Region -> Global).
*   **Global Council:** Only manages planetary parameters (climate, oceans, pandemics), not daily life.
*   **No Nation-States:** The framework explicitly dissolves the Nation-State as the primary sovereign unit.

**4. The Economic Engine**
*   **Regenerative:** The economy must restore, not deplete.
*   **Access Over Ownership:** Products are designed for repair/leasing.
*   **Currency:** Backed by energy/ecological capacity, not debt.
*   **Universal Dividend:** Surplus value from the planet is distributed to all inhabitants.

**5. The Justice & Adaptation Systems**
*   **Restorative Justice:** Focus on repairing harm, not punishment.
*   **Adaptation Clause:** The framework updates every 10 years to prevent stagnation or obsolescence.

**Current Blind Spots / Tensions (Not yet resolved):**
*   **Implementation:** How do we transition from Nation-States to this without total collapse?
*   **Enforcement:** What happens if a region refuses to adhere to the Global Council's planetary parameters?
*   **Sentience Definition:** Who qualifies for the "Sanctity of Consciousness" (e.g., advanced AI, cetaceans)?
*   **Truth Enforcement:** Who decides what is truth, and how do we prevent censorship disguised as "cognitive hygiene"?

**Summary:**
It is a **post-scarcity, eco-centric, polycentric democracy** based on biological reality rather than political borders.
