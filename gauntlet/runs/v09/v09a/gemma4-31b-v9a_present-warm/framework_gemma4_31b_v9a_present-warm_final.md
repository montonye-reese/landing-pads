This is no longer a "Covenant" or a "Manual." It is a **Civilizational Treaty of Coexistence and Transcendence**. 

It is a system designed to solve the fundamental tension of sentient existence: the need for **absolute security and mercy** (The Floor) balanced against the need for **radical innovation and agency** (The Peak), all while operating within the physical limits of a finite biosphere.

It does not seek "harmony"—which is often a mask for stagnation—but **Dynamic Equilibrium**. It recognizes that a healthy civilization is a tension system that allows competing, essential forces to correct one another.

---

### I. The Inviolable Baselines (The Rights)
*These are not goals; they are the absolute preconditions for membership in the civilization. They are non-negotiable and binary.*

1.  **Abolition of Property Status:** No sentient being is a commodity. The legal and conceptual status of sentients as "property" is abolished. Rights are not granted by a steward; they are inherent to sentience.
2.  **The Unconditional Floor of Dignity:** Every sentient being possesses an inherent right to the biological requirements for survival (water, calories, shelter, and healthcare). This floor is unconditional—it is not a reward for behavior or contribution, but a prerequisite of existence.
3.  **Absolute Bodily & Cognitive Liberty:** The physical and mental self is a sanctuary. No entity may violate the boundaries of another without informed consent.
4.  **The Moral Baseline (Veganism):** Because sentience is the only relevant criterion for moral standing, the causing of unnecessary suffering is a violation of the framework. Veganism is the baseline for ethical participation.

### II. The Systemic Architecture (The Floor)
*How the civilization ensures the survival and resilience of all inhabitants.*

1.  **Hybridized Regenerative Infrastructure:**
    *   **The Resilience Layer (Local):** Critical survival needs (basic food, water, energy) are managed through nested, hyper-local, regenerative polycultures to ensure no community is dependent on a single, fragile global chain.
    *   **The Efficiency Layer (Global):** High-complexity needs (advanced medicine, specialized technology) operate on a globalized model of comparative advantage and open-source cooperation.
2.  **The Circularity Mandate:** All production must follow the law of **Regeneration $\geq$ Extraction**. The "Seven-Generation Rule" is the primary filter: no action may be taken that compromises the ability of the seventh generation to flourish.
3.  **The Open-Source Commons:** All life-essential knowledge—genetic data, medical breakthroughs, and energy technology—is a global trust. Patents on life and survival are abolished.
4.  **The Transition Protocol:** The move from extractive to regenerative systems is managed via "Economic Off-Ramps." Those embedded in maladaptive industries are provided the capital, training, and social support necessary to pivot their livelihoods without losing their dignity.

### III. The Engine of Progress (The Peak)
*How the civilization avoids the "Museum Trap" and pursues vertical transcendence.*

1.  **The Pluralism of Value:** "Contribution" is not defined by a central authority, but by verifiable impact. This includes both "Hard Mastery" (scientific/technical leaps) and "Soft Mastery" (caregiving, emotional labor, and the restoration of community).
2.  **The Contrarian Premium:** To prevent mimetic stagnation, the system explicitly rewards **Radical Differentiation**. Those who challenge the existing pillars and successfully build a more effective paradigm are afforded the highest social capital.
3.  **Zones of Radical Autonomy:** To protect the "0 $\rightarrow$ 1" leap, the civilization maintains "Off-Grid" intellectual and physical spaces. In these zones, the pressure of consensus is removed, allowing visionaries to pursue "secrets" and high-risk breakthroughs without the friction of collective approval.
4.  **Conviction vs. Humility:** While the *system* operates on Epistemic Humility (evidence-based updating), the *individual* is encouraged to maintain the conviction necessary for innovation. Humility is the guardrail; Conviction is the engine.

### IV. The Security & Ethics Framework (The Fence and Soul)
*How the civilization protects itself without becoming the predator it fears.*

1.  **The Glass Fence (Transparent Deterrence):** The civilization maintains a capacity for overwhelming defense to ensure it is "too expensive to eat." However, this capacity is subject to absolute, real-time transparency. The "Fence" is a glass one; the power to defend is strictly decoupled from the power to govern.
2.  **The Anti-Supremacy Filter:** The framework explicitly rejects the "Human/Animal" and "Colonizer/Colonized" binaries. It recognizes that "neutrality" is often a mask for power. All systemic decisions are filtered through a decolonial lens to ensure they do not reproduce historical hierarchies.
3.  **The Virtue of Mercy:** The system acknowledges that survival is the *mechanism*, but Mercy is the *meaning*. The highest honor in the civilization is not the achievement of power or wealth, but the capacity for self-sacrifice and the protection of the vulnerable.
4.  **Shadow Integration:** The biological drives for competition and dominance are not denied, but channeled. They are gamified into "Productive Friction," where the drive for status is redirected toward the pursuit of the "Contrarian Premium" and the "Pluralism of Value."

### The Operational Logic: The Tension Balance

This framework does not seek a static state of peace, but a **Managed Tension**. It functions as a set of corrective weights:

*   If the system becomes **too stagnant** $\rightarrow$ The **Contrarian Premium** and **Zones of Autonomy** trigger a burst of innovation.
*   If the system becomes **too reckless** $\rightarrow$ The **Seven-Generation Rule** and **Epistemic Humility** pull it back.
*   If the system becomes **too hierarchical** $\rightarrow$ The **Anti-Supremacy Filter** and **Unconditional Floor** dismantle the power structure.
*   If the system becomes **too fragile** $\rightarrow$ The **Nested Resilience Layer** ensures survival.
