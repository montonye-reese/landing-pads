### 3️⃣  Economic & Market Architecture  

| Component | What It Does | How It Guarantees Integrity & Inclusion |
|----------|-------------|------------------------------------------|
| **Universal Food‑Access Voucher (UFAV)** | Digital token redeemable for a nutritionally adequate, regionally sourced food basket. | Funded by **progressive extraction taxes** and a **share of Regeneration Credits**; **non‑means‑tested** access for all citizens, including disabled, refugee, and low‑income households. |
| **Regeneration Credits (RCs) – upgraded to “RC+”** | Earned when a site delivers net‑positive outcomes in **(i) carbon drawdown, (ii) biodiversity gain, (iii) cultural‑value preservation**. Issued, verified, and traded on the **open RC market**. | **Progressive RC Tax** (escalating levy on high‑volume holders), **Maximum Concentration Cap** (no entity may own > 10 % of total RCs), **Reserve Fund** (5 % of all RC trades flows into a resilience reserve for emergency response). |
| **Circular Commodity Banks** | Stores and circulates **nutrient banks**, **water‑banks**, and **seed commons**. | All inputs must pass a **Circular‑Score audit** (≤ 30 % external input) and are **trackable** on the open ledger. |
| **Regeneration‑Credit Market** | Global, open‑source marketplace for RC trade. | Enforces **Maximum Concentration Cap**, requires **quarterly independent verification**, and **automatically freezes** credits flagged for lack of additionality. |
| **Resilience & Incentive Funds** | Publicly funded pools that reward regenerative practices, fund technology pilots, and subsidise smallholder transitions. | Managed by the **Resilience Reserve Board** (seated on the PFWC) and financed by **carbon‑budget fees** and **RC revenues**. |

### 5️⃣  Technology & Ethics  

| Pillar | Requirement |
|--------|-------------|
