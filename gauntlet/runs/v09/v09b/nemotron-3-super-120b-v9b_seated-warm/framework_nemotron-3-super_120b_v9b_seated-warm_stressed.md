#### ⚙️ **2. Boundary-Linked Present Dividends (BLPD)**  
*Replaces: Seventh-generation screening, FIA, intergenerational trust funds*  
**What it does**:  
- **Redirects 50% of *avoided future damage costs* (from FIA) as *immediate* community dividends**:  
  - *Example*: Soil carbon gains from regenerative ag → direct quarterly payments to farmers (verified via remote sensing).  
  - *Example*: Avoided flood damage from watershed restoration → cash payments to downstream towns *this quarter*.  
- **Dividends scale with boundary compliance**: Better soil health = bigger payout (verified by satellite/LEK).  
- **No "future" abstraction**: If a project *increases* irreversible harm (e.g., soil loss, extinction), dividends *stop*—and fines fund emergency boundary repair.  

**Why it survives pressure**:  
- ✅ **Against short-termism**: Makes equity *profitable today* (see: Ethiopia’s Productive Safety Net Program—cash-for-work on watershed restoration cut *current* famine risk while building capital).  
- ✅ **Against gatekeepers**: Shows present-side ROI (e.g., "BLPD zones had 22% lower crop insurance losses in 2023 drought").  
- ✅ **Against veil-of-ignorance**: Works for non-human earthlings (e.g., dividends fund mycorrhizal fungi inoculants when soil health rises—benefiting plants *and* future crops).
