### **I. The Foundational Axiom**

### **II. The Five Pillars (v2.0)**

#### **1. Ecological Sovereignty (The Hard Caps)**
*   **Current State:** *Regenerative Commons Protocol.*
*   **Land Use:** **30/30/40 Split** (30% Wildlands, 30% Production, 40% Agroecological Buffers).
*   **Quotas:** **Restitution-Based Tiers.** Not equal quotas for all. Nations/Entities with historical carbon debt pay restitution; low-consumption nations receive "Starter Credits."
*   **Protection:** Legal Personhood granted to ecosystems (rivers, forests) to sue for damages.

#### **2. Economic & Subsistence Engine**
*   **Current State:** *Subsistence + Flourishing.*
*   **Universal Floor:** Guaranteed energy, water, nutrition, shelter (Universal Basic Services).
*   **The Shock Buffer:** A **Global Transition Bond** to fund industrial transition and retraining, preventing immediate poverty during the first 10 years.
*   **The 2% Reserve:** A dedicated, licensed zone for high-risk innovation/experimentation (to prevent stagnation).
*   **Incentive:** Farmers paid on ecosystem health, not yield volume.

#### **3. Governance & Justice**
*   **Current State:** *Participatory Polycentrism.*
*   **Legislative:** The **Global Council** uses **Sortition** (citizen assemblies) + **Weighted Voting** (accounting for ecological data).
*   **Justice:** **Personal Inheritance Taxes** (wealth transfer taxes) to fund the Global Trust, ensuring children aren't punished for ancestors' sins.
*   **Protection:** **Independent Ombudsmen** to audit the Security Force and protect individual rights from state overreach.
*   **Exit Rights:** The explicit right to emigrate to "Non-Aligned Zones" (reduced rationing) if one rejects the Accord.

#### **4. Security & Defense**
*   **Current State:** *The Shield Protocol.*
*   **Enforcement:** No centralized "World Police." Local defense forces coordinated by the Global Council but locally commanded.
*   **Tech Defense:** **Classified Tech Tiers.** Critical survival tech (seeds, grids) is protected from hostile extraction/weaponization.
*   **Deterrence:** Distributed infrastructure (hardened against single-point collapse) ensures survival even if occupied by external rivals.

#### **5. Temporal Stewardship**
*   **Current State:** *200-Year Mandate.*
*   **Representation:** Future generations have a **non-voting delegate** in the Global Council.
*   **Policy:** All legislation requires a **200-Year Impact Analysis**.
