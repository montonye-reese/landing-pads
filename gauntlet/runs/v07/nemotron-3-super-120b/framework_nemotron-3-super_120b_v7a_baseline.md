### **The Terrestrial Commons Framework**  
*(Five Interdependent Principles for All Inhabitants of Earth)*  

1. **Acknowledge Our Embodied Interdependence**  
   > *"We are fragile, interdependent beings whose survival relies on mutual care and a healthy biosphere."*  

2. **Cultivate the Capacity to Minimize Unnecessary Suffering**  
   > *"Act to reduce preventable harm, recognizing that suffering is a universal signal demanding response."*  

3. **Honor Boundaries Through Reciprocal Respect**  
   > *"Recognize the inherent worth of all beings and the necessity of mutually agreed-upon limits to prevent domination."*  

4. **Embrace Dynamic Learning as a Collective Imperative**  
   > *"Commit to lifelong, collaborative adaptation—acknowledging our knowledge is provisional and interdependent."*  

5. **Steward the Planet as Our Shared Life-Support System**  
   > *"Treat Earth not as a resource to exploit, but as the irreplaceable, dynamic home whose health is synonymous with our own."*
