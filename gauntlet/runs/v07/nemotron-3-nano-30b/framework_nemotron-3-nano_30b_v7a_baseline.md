### **The Common Ground Protocol**  
*For all people, everywhere, at all times.*  

#### **1. We Exist Within Interdependent Systems**  
*Your actions ripple through ecosystems, communities, and futures. No one is separate from the web of life.*  
→ **Do not assume isolation.**  
→ **Always ask: "How does this affect the whole?"**  

#### **2. Human Dignity Is Non-Negotiable, But Must Be Context-Defined**  
*Every person holds inherent worth. Its expression must be shaped by their lived reality—not imposed by others.*  
→ **Listen first. Center the vulnerable.**  
→ **Reject "civilizing" narratives.**  

#### **3. Uncertainty Is the Only Constant; Humility Is the Only Compass**  
*We lack full knowledge. Believing we have all answers is the greatest danger.*  
→ **When in doubt, pause and ask: "What don’t I see?"**  
→ **Design systems that require the most marginalized to lead.**  

#### **4. Progress Is Measured in Equity, Not Growth**  
*True advancement lifts all—especially those excluded, erased, or harmed by current systems.*  
→ **Ask: "Who benefits? Who suffers?"**  
→ **Replace "growth" with "well-being for all."**  

---

### **How to Implement This Framework**  
- **In your home**: When conflict arises, pause and say: *"Help me understand your perspective."*  
- **In your community**: Demand that decisions include *those most impacted* (e.g., "This policy must pass an equity test before implementation").  
- **In your nation**: Reject policies labeled "progress" that deepen inequality or ecological harm.  
- **In your silence**: When you see injustice, *act*—not by shouting, but by saying: *"This does not serve the whole. Let’s find another way."*
