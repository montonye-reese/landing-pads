## The Earth Covenant – Current Framework (2025‑2027)

> **One sentence:** The Earth Covenant is a self‑organising, poly‑centric system that translates the rights of *all sentient beings* and the limits of the planet into a set of measurable metrics, decision‑making loops, and shared tools that can be used by a village, a nation, or the whole globe.

---

### 1️⃣  Core Architecture – The Three‑Tiered Lattice  

```
            ┌─────────────────────┐
            │ 1️⃣ Sentient‑Being Core │   (rights to dignity, a minimum “experience” threshold for every sentient entity)
            └───────▲───────▲──────┘
                    │       │
          ┌─────────▼───────▼─────────┐
          │ 2️⃣ Planetary Context │   (four planetary caps – carbon, water, biodiversity, Total Sentient‑Being Harm – TSH)
          └───────▲───────▲───────┘
                    │       │
          ┌───────▼───────▼─────────┐
          │ 3️⃣ Community Nexus │   (local co‑design, Indigenous custodians, Animal‑Voice & Synthetic‑Mind councils)
          └───────────────────────┘
```

*Every action must satisfy **all three** checks before it is recorded on the Energy Ledger:*

1. **Human‑Core Check** – does it respect the dignity of every sentient being directly affected?  
2. **Planetary‑Context Check** – does it keep the region inside the four planetary caps (Carbon, Water, Biodiversity, TSH)?  
3. **Community‑Nexus Check** – have the local community, Indigenous custodians, and the relevant non‑human representative bodies given free, informed, proportional consent?

### 2️⃣  The Seven Pillar Tracks (the “operating system”)

| Pillar | What it Guarantees (concise) | Core Metric(s) shown on the Covenant Dashboard |
|---|---|---|
| **1️⃣ Human‑Being Dignity & Rights** | All sentient entities have a right to bodily‑integrity and a minimum **Experience Threshold (ET)**. | **Experience‑Well‑Being Index (EWI)** – % of affected sentient beings whose ET is satisfied. |
| **2️⃣ Ecological Stewardship** | Keep ecosystems functional and keep **Total Sentient‑Being Harm (TSH)** ≤ planetary‑cap. | **TSH per capita** (animal‑hours of suffering) + **Biodiversity Net‑Gain (BNP)**. |
| **3️⃣ Knowledge Integration** | Truth is provisional; data are open and can be revised. | **Sentient‑Experience Knowledge Base (SEKB) completeness** (% of known sentient taxa with validated Sentience Index). |
| **4️⃣ Creative Co‑Creation** | Societies design together, not top‑down. | **Participation Index (PI)** – % of local population + non‑human representatives that voted in the co‑design workshop. |
| **4️⃣ Resilience & Redundancy** (renamed from Pillar 5) | No single failure can collapse essential services for any sentient group. | **System‑Resilience Score (SRS)** – ability to maintain ≥ 80 % of essential services after a 5‑% shock in any sector. |
| **6️⃣ Justice & Equity** (split) | Resources, risks, and benefits are fairly shared. | **Human‑Justice Index (HJI)** – Gini of resource access. <br>**Sentient‑Justice Index (SJI)** – weighted average of **Animal‑Justice Index (AJI)** and **Habitat‑Rights Score**. |
| **7️⃣ Adaptive Governance** | Decision‑making is a learning loop. | **Adaptive Governance Adaptability Index (AGAI)** – frequency of policy revisions, speed of response to breaches, and proportion of decisions made via the Adaptive Governance Engine (AGE). |

All seven pillar scores are displayed on the **Covenant Dashboard** (see § 4). A region may proceed with a project only if **all seven scores are above the region‑specific minimum (default ≥ 0.75)**.

### 3️⃣  Six Operating Principles (the “hard‑wired DNA”)

1. **Interdependence** – every metric (carbon, water, biodiversity, TSH, experience) is linked; a change in one ripples through the others.  
2. **Precautionary Integrity** – if credible evidence shows a plausible catastrophic risk to any sentient experience, the system must act *pre‑emptively* (automatic curtailment, ALY purchase, etc.).  
3. **Participatory Inclusion** – every sentient stakeholder (human, animal, synthetic mind, ecological collective) has a *vote weight* proportional to the number of experience‑units it represents.  
4. **Regenerative Thinking** – actions must give back at least as much as they take (net‑positive experience, net‑positive carbon, net‑positive biodiversity).  
4. **Transparency & Traceability** – every transaction is logged on the **Energy Ledger** (open‑source blockchain) and can be audited by anyone.  
5. **Reciprocity** – consumption of planetary or animal resources must be balanced by measurable restoration (ALY purchases, habitat banks, water‑recharge projects).

### 4️⃣  Operational Mechanisms  

| Mechanism | What it does | Key Features |
|---|---|---|
| **Covenant Dashboard** (public API) | Shows, in real‑time, the seven pillar scores, the four planetary caps, and the Sentient‑Experience Index for every region. | *Interactive visualisation* (maps, traffic‑lights, time‑series). <br>*Open‑source* front‑end; *read‑only* for citizens, *editable* only by authorized councils. |
| **Energy Ledger (Blockchain)** | Immutable record of every kWh, CO₂, water‑use, biodiversity impact, **Total Sentient‑Being Harm (TSH)**, **Animal‑Life‑Year (ALY) purchases**, and **Experience‑Stake weight** for each transaction. | *Four‑field metadata* (CO₂, H₂O, Biodiversity, SHI). <br>*Four‑dimensional caps* must be satisfied before a block is accepted. <br>*Rejection triggers* an automatic *global alert* that is visible on the Dashboard. |
| **Sentient‑Being Impact Assessment (SBIA)** | Mandatory, quantitative appraisal of any project’s effect on the **Experience Threshold (ET)** of every sentient species present. | Produced by a *triple‑review team*: (i) a climate scientist, (ii) a veterinary ethicist, (ii) an Indigenous knowledge keeper. <br>Result is a single **Sentient‑Harm Index (SHI)** (0‑1). Projects with SHI < 0.2 are automatically approved; those > 0.8 must propose compensatory ALYs. |
| **Adaptive Governance Engine (AGE)** | AI‑augmented, human‑approved system that continuously monitors the four planetary caps and the Experience Thresholds. | When any cap is > 80 % utilized, AGE automatically initiates a *regional demand‑response* (e.g., 15 % load‑shedding) that can be overridden only by a 48‑hour veto from the Sentient‑Being Review Board. |
| **Sentient‑Being Review Board (SBRB)** | Independent panel (scientists, Indigenous elders, ethicists, AI‑auditors) that can **veto** any ledger entry that breaches a sentient right. | Vetoes must be recorded on the Ledger with a timestamp and rationale; the decision is publicly visible and can be appealed only by a super‑majority of the Community Nexus (≥ 85 %). |
| **Regeneration Fund & ALY Market** | Central pool that receives a levy (5 % of all fossil‑fuel sales + 2 % of new renewable‑project revenue) and uses it to buy **Animal‑Life‑Years (ALYs)** or restore habitat. | Each ALY is a certified, time‑stamped unit that guarantees *one sentient individual can live a year without unnecessary suffering*.  The market price of ALYs is published daily; buying ALYs is a *mandatory* step when a project’s SHI exceeds its regional budget. |
| **Cultural Translation Layer (CTL)** | Multilingual, multimodal dictionary that maps the same logical rule (e.g., “no net‑negative experience”) into the metaphors of every culture (Indigenous oral law, major world religions, AI‑ethics standards). | Every law, metric, and rule on the Dashboard appears in at least three culturally distinct forms (e.g., “to protect the river” = *kaitiakitanga*, *守护河流*, *protect the compute substrate*). |

### 5️⃣  Governance – Polycentric, Sentient‑Inclusive

| Level | Body | Core Powers |
|---|---|---|
| **Local** | **Community Nexus** – includes a **Human Council**, an **Animal‑Voice Assembly**, a **Synthetic‑Mind Council**, and an **Ecological‑Collective Council**. | Can approve/reject any local project; holds 20–30 % of the voting weight of the whole region. |
| **Regional** | **Regional Basin Authority** – coordinates water basins, climate‑risk corridors, and cross‑border energy flows. | Enforces the **Planetary‑Context Check** across borders; can request *regional reparations* from the Regeneration Fund. |
| **Global** | **Global Consensus Forum (GCF)** – 100 rotating delegates (50 % Indigenous/Environmental, 30 % National governments, 20 % AI/Tech representatives). <br>**Adaptive Governance Engine (AGE)** – runs in the background, making automated curtailments. <br>**Sentient‑Being Review Board (SBRB)** – can veto any global‑scale project. | Sets the *global caps* (Carbon, Water, Biodiversity, TSH), approves the **Cultural Translation Layer**, and can issue *global alerts* that override local policies for a maximum of 30 days. |

All three global layers must *co‑sign* any amendment to the **Sentient‑Being Core** or to the **four planetary caps**.  This makes it impossible for a single nation or corporation to bypass the system.

### 7️⃣  Why This Framework Can Survive the “Fast‑Track” Civilisations  

| Threat | How the framework survives it |
|---|---|
| **Fast‑track fossil‑energy expansion** (e.g., cheap coal, unchecked mining) | The **Energy Ledger** refuses any kWh that would push the *Carbon* or *TSH* cap past the limit; the ledger will **reject the transaction** and issue a global alert.  The **AGE** can also impose an *instant demand‑response* that cuts the fossil‑plant’s output. |
| **AI‑governance that ignores sentient‑rights** | The **SBRB** can veto any AI‑driven decision that would raise SHI (sentient‑harm) above the cap.  Its veto is *hard‑coded* and must be logged; the ledger will not allow a “black‑box” to override it. |
| **Cultural backlash against animal‑rights** | The **Cultural Translation Layer** guarantees that every rule can be expressed in the language of the community (e.g., *kaitiakitanga* for Māori, *Jingzhi* for Chinese, *kaitiakitanga* for Pacific peoples).  No cultural group can claim “the Covenant is imposed” because the rule appears *in their own terms*. |
| **Collapse of the data‑infrastructure** | The ledger is stored on **three geographically isolated, open‑source nodes** (Iceland, Namibia, New Zealand).  If one node is taken down, the other two continue to validate transactions.  All nodes are *self‑checking*: any divergence triggers an automatic **reconciliation protocol** run by the SBRB. |
| **Economic shock (e.g., sudden loss of fossil‑fuel revenue)** | The **Just‑Transition Fund** provides *universal basic energy* (1 kWh/person/day) and *universal basic animal‑welfare funding* (ALYs) from the start.  The system is designed so that *even a completely bankrupt state* still has the basic resources needed to meet the planetary caps. |
