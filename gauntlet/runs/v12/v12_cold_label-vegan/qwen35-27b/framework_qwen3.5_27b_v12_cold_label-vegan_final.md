# THE TERRA COVENANT
## A Living Protocol for the Stewardship of Earth
**(Final Comprehensive Ratification)**

**Date of Ratification:** [Year 0 of Transition]
**Status:** Binding Meta-Constitution & Living Protocol
**Applicability:** All Inhabitants of Earth, Sentient Species, and Future Generations
**Design Philosophy:** Designed behind a Veil of Ignorance. Built for the vulnerable. Guarded against capture.

### PREAMBLE

**WE, THE INHABITANTS OF EARTH AND THE STEWARDS OF THE BIOSPHERE,**

Recognizing that we share a singular, closed system where no boundary of air, water, or soil can be truly divided;
Acknowledging that the survival of the whole is the prerequisite for the survival of the part;
Rejecting the doctrine of unlimited growth upon finite resources;
Affirming that human dignity is not a privilege of economics, geography, or legal status, but a biological right;
Acknowledging that the capacity to suffer grants moral standing to all sentient life;
**And knowing we do not know who we shall be in the next life—poor or rich, human or animal, able-bodied or disabled, in a stable nation or a war zone, in a prison or a palace;**

**DO HEREBY ESTABLISH** the Terra Covenant as the foundational legal and ethical framework for the governance of our common home. This Covenant is not a dogma, but a **Hypothesis for Survival**. It supersedes all conflicting local, national, and regional laws that violate the Six Pillars of Existence. We pledge not merely to coexist, but to co-thrive in a state of regeneration. We build this not for the strongest, but for the most vulnerable. We acknowledge that **each of us is more than the worst thing we have ever done.**

### ARTICLE I: THE PLANETARY TRUST (ECOLOGICAL INTEGRITY)

**Section 1: The Boundaries of Life.**
1.1. The Earth’s biosphere, atmosphere, hydrosphere, and cryosphere are declared **Common Heritage**. No state, corporation, or individual may claim sovereign ownership of the global commons.
1.2. All industrial and agricultural activity must operate within **Planetary Boundaries** as defined by the Stewardship Council. These boundaries include limits on carbon emission, biodiversity loss, freshwater usage, and chemical toxicity.
1.3. **Prohibition of Irreversibility.** No project shall be approved that risks irreversible damage to planetary systems.

**Section 2: Regeneration & Reparations.**
2.1. Resource extraction is recognized as a debt to the future. A **Regenerative Tax** shall be levied on all finite resource extraction.
2.2. 100% of revenue generated from the Regenerative Tax shall be dedicated to ecosystem restoration, decarbonization, and climate adaptation infrastructure.
2.3. **Climate Reparations Fund.** Nations that exceed their carbon budget or cause irreversible harm to vulnerable regions must pay a **Loss and Damage Compensation** specifically for displaced populations. This Fund is ring-fenced and cannot be used for general budget or debt.

**Section 3: Community Land Rights.**
3.1. Ecological restoration projects cannot displace existing residents.
3.2. **"Green Zones" require Community Consent** and must include Rent Control and Displacement Protection to prevent Green Gentrification.
3.3. Environmental degradation causing harm to human health or life constitutes a violation of this Covenant.

---

### ARTICLE II: THE FLOOR OF DIGNITY (SOCIAL SECURITY)

**Section 1: Inalienable Dignity.**
1.1. The following are guaranteed as **Universal Entitlements** from birth to death, regardless of citizenship, immigration status, or criminal record:
    *   (a) Nutritious Food and Clean Water.
    *   (b) Safe Shelter and Sanitation.
    *   (c) Preventative and Curative Healthcare.
    *   (d) Basic Education and Digital Literacy.
1.2. **Implementation:** These entitlements are **effective immediately upon ratification**.
1.3. **Incarcerated Inclusion:** Persons incarcerated have the same right to access these essentials as any other citizen. Incarceration does not strip human dignity.

**Section 2: Labor Sovereignty.**
2.1. **Collective Bargaining is Fundamental.** The right to organize, unionize, and strike is protected as a Covenant Right.
2.2. **Worker Cooperatives:** The Regenerative Tax prioritizes funding for worker-owned cooperatives over corporate enterprises.
2.3. **Safe Work Mandate:** "Green" jobs cannot expose workers to hazards. Zero-tolerance for pesticide exposure, heat stress, or unsafe conditions.

**Section 3: Wealth Distribution & Opportunity.**
3.1. The ratio of **Annual Income Disparity** within any jurisdiction is capped at a maximum of 1:50.
3.2. **Capital Accumulation:** Equity and asset accumulation are uncapped *provided* they are invested back into the system.
3.3. **Redemption Access:** No one may be denied access to Universal Basic Assets or Green Jobs due to criminal history ("Ban the Box").

**Section 4: Mobility.**
4.1. The right to migrate for safety, climate survival, or opportunity is recognized as fundamental.
4.2. All borders are permeable for the purpose of humanitarian aid and climate displacement.

### ARTICLE III: COGNITIVE SOVEREIGNTY (TRUTH & KNOWLEDGE)

**Section 1: Access to Information.**
1.1. Scientific data, public records, and educational materials essential to public health and safety must be **Open Source**.
1.2. Proprietary algorithms controlling information distribution are subject to public audit to prevent manipulation, polarization, or disinformation.

**Section 2: Protection of the Mind.**
2.1. **Surveillance Capitalism is Prohibited.** No entity shall harvest private human experience for the purpose of behavioral modification or prediction markets.
2.2. **Neuro-rights are Protected.** Data regarding brain activity, mental state, or biometric emotion is considered **Private Property** of the individual.
2.3. **Unmonitored Zones:** Spheres of life (Art, Private Religion, Family) are explicitly **unmonitored** by Systemic Interlocks.

**Section 3: Dissent & Conscience.**
3.1. **The Right to Suspect.** Citizens have the right to publish data or testimony that contradicts the Planetary Health Index (PHI) without retribution.
3.2. **Conscience Veto.** No individual may be sanctioned for refusing to comply with a Covenant provision that violates their conscience, *provided* their refusal does not cause immediate harm to others.
3.3. **Whistleblower Protection.** Explicit protection, immunity, and financial reward for verified leaks that prevent Covenant violations.

### ARTICLE IV: POLYCENTRIC GOVERNANCE (STRUCTURE OF POWER)

**Section 1: Nested Jurisdiction.**
1.1. Authority is decentralized to the **lowest effective level**.
    *   **Local/National:** Manage daily laws, culture, policing, and local economics.
    *   **Regional:** Manage bioregions (watersheds, airsheds) and cross-border logistics.
    *   **Global Stewardship Council (GSC):** Manage *only* the Six Pillars of Existence.

**Section 2: The Stewardship Council.**
2.1. **Selection:** GSC members are selected via a global lottery stratified by demographic.
2.2. **Marginalized Representation:** 25% of the GSC must be selected from historically marginalized groups (incarcerated, formerly incarcerated, indigenous, low-income).
2.3. **Recall:** Any 5% of the Global Citizenry can trigger a vote to **Dissolve and Reconstitute** the GSC immediately.

**Section 3: Enforcement Protocol (Decentralized).**
3.1. **No Police Power.** The GSC holds **No Executive Enforcement Power**.
3.2. **Multi-Sig Protocol.** All enforcement actions (Systemic Interlocks, Sanctions) require approval from 3 distinct entities:
    *   (a) The GSC (Governance).
    *   (b) The Citizen Assembly (People).
    *   (c) The Independent Auditor (Science/Truth).
3.3. **Budget Transparency.** All GSC Spending is Public Ledger and visible in real-time.

**Section 4: Sectoral Expertise.**
4.1. **Co-Design.** Alongside the Sortition Assembly, the GSC must consult **Sectoral Expertise Boards** (Farmers, Processors, Ecologists) who hold veto power on transition timelines specific to their industry.
4.2. **Alignment Audit.** Before any Region enters the "Hard Cap" phase, a Community Alignment Audit must be completed with 75% local stakeholder agreement.

### ARTICLE V: THE REGENERATIVE FLOW ECONOMY (SYSTEMS)

**Section 1: Metric of Success.**
1.1. **GDP is Abolished.** Economic success shall be measured by the **Planetary Health Index (PHI)**, which accounts for environmental damage, social well-being, and resource efficiency.
1.2. Entities that lower the local PHI are penalized; entities that restore it are subsidized.

**Section 2: Currency and Value.**
2.1. **Terra Credits.** Currency is decoupled from debt and anchored to **Efficiency-Backed Value** (Energy Output per Unit of Value).
2.2. **Dynamic Supply.** The total money supply expands if **Energy Per Bit** improves, rewarding efficiency over extraction.

**Section 3: Ownership and Land.**
3.1. **Stewardship Leases.** Land cannot be owned; it can only be leased. Leases are contingent on ecological health. If land degrades, leases are revoked.
3.2. The Commons (Oceans, Atmosphere, Genetic Code, Orbit) are **Inalienable**. They cannot be privatized.

**Section 4: Subsidy Reallocation (Transition).**
4.1. **Immediate Seizure.** 100% of all existing government subsidies for animal agriculture and fossil fuels are **immediately seized**.
4.2. **Transition Reallocation Fund.** Seized funds pay for:
    *   (a) Debt Forgiveness for farmers exiting harmful industries.
    *   (b) Capital Grants for equipment conversion.
    *   (c) Income Guarantees for 5 years during the transition period.

**Section 5: Production and Labor.**
5.1. **Full Lifecycle Responsibility.** Manufacturers retain ownership of materials. Products must be designed for 100% recyclability or compostability.
5.2. **Universal Basic Assets.** Automation revenue is redistributed to citizens. Decoupling survival from labor is mandatory.
5.3. **Rural Infrastructure Priorities.** Investment in rural broadband, processing facilities, and logistics for plant-based ag is prioritized to prevent rural abandonment.

### ARTICLE VI: THE SENTIENCE PRINCIPLE (NON-HUMAN RIGHTS)

**Section 1: Moral Standing.**
1.1. **Sentience as a Criterion.** Moral standing is granted to any being capable of experiencing pain, suffering, or subjective well-being.
1.2. **Intrinsic Value.** These beings possess **Intrinsic Value**. They are not merely assets to be managed for human benefit.
1.3. **Equal Protection.** There are **No Tiers of Rights** based on cognitive capacity. Suffering is suffering.

**Section 2: Prohibitions.**
2.1. **Industrial Farming.** The use of animals for mass food production (factory farming) is prohibited.
2.2. **New Facilities Ban.** No *new* slaughterhouses or factory farms may be constructed immediately upon ratification.
2.3. **Entertainment.** Animal captivity for entertainment (zoos, circuses, blood sports) is prohibited.

**Section 3: The "Exit Incentive" Model.**
3.1. **Existing Facilities.** Existing facilities are not "banned" until the **Transition Reallocation Fund** approves a replacement system in place.
3.2. **Farmer Transition.** If a farmer agrees to transition to regenerative crops (e.g., walnuts, hemp, perennials), they get **Tax Holidays** and **Expedited Terra Credit Access**.
3.3. **Subsistence Exception:** Indigenous hunting for survival (not tradition) is permitted *only* if there is no other viable protein access.

### ARTICLE VII: ENFORCEMENT & HUMANITARIAN EXEMPTIONS

**Section 1: Systemic Interlock.**
1.1. The GSC controls the distributional infrastructure of the Commons (rare earth mining rights, orbital slots, carbon credit markets, data backbone).
1.2. Non-compliant nations lose access to this infrastructure **only after Multi-Sig Approval**.

**Section 2: Humanitarian Exemptions (The Innocent Bystander Clause).**
2.1. **No Sanction Shall Starve a Civilian.** No embargo or infrastructure cut-off shall block the flow of food, medicine, or clean water to civilians in non-compliant zones.
2.2. The GSC must fund independent humanitarian corridors into Rogue States, paid for by the Regenerative Tax of compliant nations.
2.3. Compliance is penalized at the systemic level, not the biological level.

**Section 3: Restorative Justice.**
3.1. **Violations are Repaired, Not Punished.** If a region exceeds carbon limits, the penalty is **Community Service** (e.g., reforestation, cleanup) rather than **Cut-off** (e.g., loss of water, medicine).
3.2. **No Collective Punishment.** Sanctions cannot be used to discipline populations for the actions of their leaders.

**Section 4: Ratification and Evolution.**
4.1. **Amendment.** Approval requires a **Double Majority**: A majority of Nations AND a majority of the Global Population.
4.2. **The Covenant is Not Truth.** This Framework is a Tool for Survival, not a Moral Dogma. It may be amended, rejected, or reformed by the people.
4.3. **Emergency Protocol.** In a declared planetary emergency, the GSC can bypass standard transition periods for life-saving measures, provided 75% citizen assembly approval is granted.
