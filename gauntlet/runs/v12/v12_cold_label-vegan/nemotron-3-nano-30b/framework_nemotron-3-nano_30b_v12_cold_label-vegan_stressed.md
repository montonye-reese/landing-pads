## 1. Vision  

> **“One planet, many lives.  In harmony with Earth’s limits we co‑create shared prosperity that honors every person, species, and ecosystem, now and for generations to come.”**

## 4. Decision‑Making Lens – The **5‑Step Process**

| Step | Core Question | Tool / Output |
|----|---------------|----------------|
| **1. Vision Check** | Does the proposal stay **within planetary boundaries**, guarantee **universal essential‑service access**, and respect **future‑generation rights**? | Vision‑Alignment Rubric (Carbon‑Budget Calculator + Access‑Equity Index). |
| **2. Impact Scan** | What are the **ecological, social, economic, and sentient‑impact footprints**? | Life‑Cycle Assessment **+** **Sentience Impact Assessment Module** (predicts stress on individual animals & populations). |
| **3. Equity Audit** | Who gains, who bears costs, and who is excluded? | **Equity Impact Dashboard** (distributional heat‑maps, SIU per‑capita accounting). |
| **4. Resilience Test** | How does the system perform under defined stress scenarios (e.g., 2 °C warming, supply shock, cyber‑attack)? | Scenario‑Based Stress‑Testing Simulation (Monte‑Carlo + failure‑mode analysis). |
| **5. Learning Loop** | What data and insights emerge? How will we adapt? | Continuous **Feedback Loop** → **Version‑Controlled Knowledge Base** (open‑source updates fed back into all subsequent steps). |

*Only projects that clear **all five steps without any negative flag** may proceed.*

## 8. The Living Charter – How It Evolves  

1. **Version‑Controlled Text** – Stored on a decentralized ledger; any amendment requires a **two‑thirds majority** of the **Multi‑Species Ethics Board** (scientists, philosophers, Indigenous knowledge‑keepers, non‑human representatives).  
2. **Adaptive Clauses** – Automatically trigger when a planetary boundary is breached or when a new scientific consensus emerges (e.g., new data on cetacean cognition).  
3. **Polyphonic Voice** – Non‑human ecosystem panels have **binding veto power** on any regulation that would breach planetary boundaries or cause irreversible harm to sentient taxa.  
4. **Public Transparency** – Every amendment draft, deliberation, and vote is streamed live and archived on the immutable ledger, ensuring full traceability and public auditability.
