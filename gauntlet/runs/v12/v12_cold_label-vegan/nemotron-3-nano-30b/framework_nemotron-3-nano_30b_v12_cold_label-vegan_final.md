## 5.  Institutional Architecture  

### 1. **Micro‑Level (Household / Community)**  
*Micro‑Micro‑Grid & Smart‑Metering* – each household gets a **modular solar + storage kit**, a **privacy‑by‑design peer‑to‑peer energy market**, and a **community‑budget contribution** that feeds the **Universal Energy Fund**.  

### 2. **Meso – Community / District Council**  
*Community Action Panels* (elected citizen juries) decide local land‑use, energy mixes, and **Community‑Benefit Funds** (schools, co‑ops, broadband).  

### 3. **Macro‑Level – Federation of Energy Authorities (FEA)**  
*Sets national renewable‑energy targets, allocates RECs, runs carbon‑price floor, collects revenue, distributes **Universal Energy Dividend**.*  

### 3.1 **National Oversight Body** (FEA)  
- **Mandate:** enforce planetary‑boundary caps, allocate **Human Capital Investment Funds**, **audit** compliance, and issue **Ecosystem‑Voice Certificates**.  
- **Mandate**: FEA must adopt **planetary‑boundary budgeting** (e.g., total SIU budget ≤ 5 Mt CO₂e/yr for the nation).  

### 3.3 **Meta‑Macro – Planetary Governance**  
- **Earth Energy Charter 2.0** (legally binding across signatories)  
- **Inter‑Planetary Council of Stewards (IPEC)** – rotating panels of ecosystem scientists, Indigenous knowledge keepers, youth delegates, and **non‑human ecosystem delegates** (e.g., forest, ocean, wetland, soil).  
- **Inter‑Planetary Council of Stewards (IPEC)** has **veto power** over any project that would breach planetary boundaries, **override** a national decision only with a 2/3 super‑majority of the full Council.

### 5.  The **Dynamic Energy‑Simulation Engine (DESE)**  

*An open‑source, continuously trained simulation that runs in real‑time on a distributed ledger.*  
- Takes inputs: **SIU budget**, **biodiversity intactness**, **climate scenario forecasts**, **solar/wind/solar‑hydrogen generation data**, **human‑capital stock**.  
- Outputs **optimisation solutions** that maximise **Resilience Resilience Score** (≥ 99.9 % uptime) while **minimising** SIU impact.  
- All model updates are stored on a **public, version‑controlled ledger**; anyone can download, verify, and propose amendments.

## 6.  Full Technical‑Operational Description (Condensed)

1. **Registration & Identity** – Every citizen registers on the **Open‑Source Ledger** using a verified identity token (mobile ID).  
2. **Identity Verification & Access Control** – Enables *role‑based access* to dashboard, decision‑making panels, and voting modules.  
3. **Equity‑Impact Calculation** – Periodic **Equity Impact Audit** that calculates **Equity Impact Units (EUI)** per household/region; triggers *financial redistribution* when thresholds are exceeded.  
3. **Resilience Testing** – The system runs **scenario simulations** (e.g., 2 °C warming, 1 m sea‑level rise, supply chain shock) and produces **Resilience Stress‑Test reports**. All projects must meet **≥ 95 % resilience for 100‑year horizon**.  
3. **Decision‑Making** – At the *Meso* level, proposals are taken through the **5‑Step Lens**, receiving automated **red‑flag** if any of the five steps is flagged.  
6. **Learning Loop** – Raw data, model outputs, community feedback, and audit logs feed into **Version‑Control Ledger**; all updates are timestamped and publicly viewable.
