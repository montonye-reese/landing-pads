# The Living‑Earth Charter (Version 2028)

> **“We, the species that can speak, imagine, and build, agree to be co‑authors of the story of the Earth, not its sole writers.”**  
> The charter is a *living* document – it is interpreted, applied, and revised every year by the people who inhabit the planet, no matter whether they are born as a human, a sentient animal, a synthetic intelligence, or a living ecosystem.

## 4. Global Response Cycle – Real‑Time Reaction Loop  

1. **Data Ingestion (continuous)** – All climate, biodiversity, health, energy, and socio‑economic data flow into the **Open‑Science Platform**.  
2. **Threshold Detection (Thresholds‑Team)** – Automated algorithms flag when any **planetary‑boundary component** or **Sentient‑Being Impact (SBI) score** crosses its *danger zone*.  
3. **Emergency Review (ECC – Emergency Branch)** – The **Rapid‑Response Sub‑Council (RRSC)** (100 members: 50 randomly drawn citizens + 2 AI‑auditors) meets within **48 hours**; it can (i) issue an *override directive* that temporarily suspends the offending activity, (ii) release **remediation bonds** from the **Planetary Budget Reserve (PBR)**, and (iii) order a post‑mortem audit.  
4. **Policy Cascade (Adaptive Societal Levers)** – The GCC, together with national governments, activates pre‑written **contingency statutes** (e.g., “Carbon‑Safeguard Protocol” – a global carbon tax that automatically scales when the 1.5 °C ceiling is breached).  
5. **Adaptive Learning (Living Learning Loop)** – Outcomes are fed back; AI‑driven simulations test new policy scenarios; the next year’s **Earth State Report** is updated.  

*All steps are logged on the immutable ledger; any citizen can view the full chain of events.*

## 5. Sentient‑Being Impact (SBI) – The Core Metric that Protects All Life

1. **Components**  
   * **Number of sentient individuals directly used** (e.g., livestock slaughtered, lab animals, wildlife culled).  
   * **Suffering intensity** – validated pain‑scale (0 = no pain, 1 = maximum suffering).  
   * **Availability of humane alternatives** – quantified as a percentage of the task that can be done without harming sentient life.  

2. **Scale** – 0 (complete exploitation) → 1 (fully humane, alternatives available).  

3. **Thresholds** – Any new project must achieve **SBI ≥ 0.70** before it can receive a licence. Projects that fall below are automatically blocked by the **Integrated Impact Score (IIS)** (see §5.2).  

4. **Enforcement** – The **Animal‑Trust (AT)** (see §5.5) can *veto* any project with DAI ≥ 0.3 (Direct Animal‑Impact) regardless of other scores. The AT’s 5 % equity stake can fund *remediation bonds* if a violation occurs.

## 5. Integrated Impact Score (IIS) – The Hard Gate

The **IIS** is a **vector of three sub‑scores**, each weighted by the *SAM* (Sentient‑Being Capacity) of the class most affected:

* **I₁ – Carbon‑Budget Score** (0‑1).  
* **I₂ – Biodiversity / Ecosystem Score** (0‑1).  
* **I₃ – Sentient‑Being Impact Score (SBI)** (0‑1).  

A **project passes** only if **all three components exceed the thresholds tied to the SAM of the most‑affected class**.  

Example:  
* A mining operation that would destroy a high‑SAM river (SAM ≈ 0.7) must have I₂ ≥ 0.7, even if I₁ (carbon) and I₃ (SBI) are both high.  

The **IIS** is computed by the **Open‑Science Platform** and is immutable for the life of the project.

* Finance **wildlife rescue** and **re‑wilding** after a high‑damage incident.  
* Provide **compensation** to communities whose SBI score falls because of a corporation’s activity.

## 5.7  Universal Dividend & Data‑Use Levy  

*Every person (human, AI, or legal guardian of an ecosystem) receives a **monthly digital dividend** directly from the PBR.*  
The **Data‑Use Levy** taxes any AI system that *directly influences planetary systems* (e.g., climate‑model controllers, orbital‑debris‑removal AI). The revenue flows straight into the **Planetary Budget Reserve**.

## 6. Decision‑Making Flow (Live‑Loop)

1. **Data → Integrated Impact Score** – The Open‑Science Platform continuously updates the **IIS**.  
2. **Threshold breach?** – If any component falls below its SAM‑dependent threshold, the system automatically **flags an Emergency**.  
2. **Rapid‑Response Sub‑Council** – Issues an **Override Directive** (48 h).  
3. **Remediation** – The **Eco‑Trust** or **Sentient‑Being Fund** releases the necessary bonds.  
4. **Post‑mortem** – Full audit on the blockchain; any party can comment; a *global citizen assembly* may vote to change the thresholds for future cycles.  

All steps are **transparent**, **auditable**, and **subject to the meta‑layer principles**.

| Threat | How the charter counters it (in one sentence) |

| **Political capture of the ECC** | The **GCC** requires *2/3 continental representation* *and* *2/3 expert trustee* approval for any rule change; the **Animal‑Trust** and **Future‑Generations Trust** each hold an *irrevocable* 5 % of the vote, making capture costly and visible. |

## 7.  The Charter as a Living Document  

* **Annual Global Summit** – 1 million citizens (randomly selected, demographically balanced) vote on the next 5‑year *Focus Areas*.  
* **Science‑Policy Review Board** – All new scientific findings (climate, AI, biodiversity) must be reviewed before they can influence the next set of thresholds.  
* **Open Amendment Process** – Anyone can submit an amendment (e.g., “Add a new Living‑Entity”). It passes through (a) **Transparency Office check**, (b) a **demographically balanced citizen assembly**, (c) a **global vote** (≥ 2/3 in favour).  

The charter therefore evolves, but *its meta‑layer never changes*.

### **In short:**  
The **Living‑Earth Charter** is a *self‑protecting, self‑learning* constitution. It treats **every possible sentient subject** (human, animal, AI, ecosystem) as a stakeholder, gives each a *guardian* and a *rights‑level* proportional to its capacity, and builds **hard, algorithmic limits** that no nation, corporation, or rogue technology can bypass without immediate, irreversible loss of the planetary budget.  

If you wake up tomorrow as a child, a cow, a quantum‑AI, or a coral reef, the charter will still protect you – because it is **designed for the unknown earthling you will become**.
