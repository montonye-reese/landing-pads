*   **The Right:** Access to the "Big Three" (Nutritious Food, Potable Water, Shelter) is a fundamental birthright, not a market privilege. Healthcare and Education are public utilities.

The framework rejects the "Linear Economy" (Extract -> Use -> Dispose).
*   **Metric:** Success is not measured by GDP, but by **Genuine Progress Indicator (GPI)**. This accounts for unpaid care work, environmental restoration, and health outcomes.
*   **Ownership:** Means of production (AI, energy grids, land) are held in public or community trusts. Private ownership is allowed for personal goods, but capital accumulation is capped to prevent oligarchy.
*   **Flow:** Energy is free and decentralized (solar/wind). Material goods are designed for 100% recyclability (Cradle-to-Cradle design).

War is deemed a failure of the Covenant.
*   **Mechanism:** The Global Chamber maintains a **Peacekeeping & Mediation Corps**. They do not invade; they isolate and mediate.

It is not a promise of Utopia—it acknowledges human error, greed, and tragedy. But it is a **Lifeboat Agreement**. It is the understanding that if the ship sinks, the life jackets do not matter.
