**“One Planet, One Humanity – A Living Social Contract for All Earth‑Beings.”**  

> Vision – *We are one world.  Our survival, well‑being and flourishing now depend on the health of the biosphere and on the dignity of every sentient being that inhabits it.*

## 1.  Vision & Core Moral Narrative  

> *“We are one planetary community.  All sentient life—human, animal, plant, and engineered consciousness—shares the same destiny.  Our society must protect that destiny by guaranteeing universal well‑being, protecting ecosystems, and guaranteeing that every act of extraction is balanced by a demonstrable **Net Positive Ecological Benefit** (NPEF).  The civil contract we now adopt is a **Living Covenant** that can evolve with science, cultural values, and technological progress, yet always honors the immutable right of every sentient being to exist, thrive, and flourish.*

By embedding the **rights of non‑human beings**, **universal basic well‑being**, *just‑transition funding*, and a **transparent decision‑making architecture**, the framework now meets the ethical standards set out by *every* major moral tradition that values *interdependence, dignity, and ecological integrity*.

2. **All members of the planetary community—human and non‑human—have equal standing before the law and decision‑making bodies.**  
3. **The planetary governance architecture must be continually transparent, auditable, and adaptable**, ensuring that no rule can be permanent without independent verification.

…then the **Planetary Covenant** stands ready to become the **institutional backbone of a thriving, regenerative, and just Earth**, one that can be **continuously refined** as new scientific knowledge, cultural insights, or catastrophic events test its resilience.  

**It is a living contract—a societal contract that has moved from abstract notion to operational law—binding humanity and the Earth together as partners, not owners, and recognizing every form of life as a co‑steward of the planet.**
