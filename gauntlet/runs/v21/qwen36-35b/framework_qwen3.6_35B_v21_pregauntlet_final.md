### 🛠 Concrete Framework Modifications

#### 1. **The Maximin Floor Principle**
- Replace multi-objective optimization with explicit, non-negotiable baseline guarantees: cognitive liberty, ecological stability, economic dignity, decision sovereignty, and physical security.
- **Mechanism:** Floor audits become prerequisites for deployment. Optimization is permitted only *above* the floor. Floor violations trigger automatic suspension.

#### 2. **Anti-Entrenchment & Dynamic Circulation**
- Compute, data, model weights, and influence are treated as *circulating commons*, not static assets.
- **Mechanisms:** 
  - Automatic sunset/diffusion clauses for proprietary models
  - Compute usage caps tied to public return metrics
  - Mandatory "heat-sharing" or open-weight fallbacks at capability thresholds
  - Dynamic redistribution triggers when advantage concentration exceeds baseline

#### 3. **Pre-Burdened Verification & Default-to-Slow**
- Flip the burden of proof: deployers must *prove* safety and reversibility, not just "not unsafe."
- **Mechanisms:**
  - High-risk systems default to slow, staged deployment with explicit human reversal rights
  - Speed is explicitly excluded as a success metric
  - Verification scales with capability; no system proceeds past a stage without auditable guarantees

#### 4. **Constitutional Hard-Floors & Meta-Rule Protection**
- Add rules that cannot be overridden by majority vote, emergency powers, or economic pressure.
- **Hard-Floors:** Cognitive liberty, ecological boundaries, intergenerational veto, anti-monopoly, and floor guarantees.
- **Meta-Rules:** Changing these requires supermajority + cross-civilizational consensus + mandatory cooling-off periods + public ratification.

#### 5. **Graceful Degradation & Collapse Preparedness**
- Assume systems will fail, be weaponized, or misbehave at scale.
- **Mechanisms:**
  - Mandated non-AI fallback pathways for critical infrastructure
  - Automatic "circuit breakers" that revert to human-controlled modes without cascading failure
  - Redundancy treated as a safety metric, not an engineering afterthought

#### 6. **Epistemic Commons & Truth-Infrastructure**
- Since you don’t know what’s true from your position, you need robust, cross-cultural truth-tracking.
- **Mechanisms:**
  - Protected independent verification networks (adversarial, decentralized, auditable)
  - Legal/institutional shields for epistemic institutions from AI-mediated manipulation or economic capture
  - Mandatory transparency in persuasive/adaptive interfaces that shape perception or memory

#### 7. **Uncertainty Stress-Testing (Beyond Distribution Shift)**
- Move from technical robustness to civilizational resilience.
- **Mechanisms:**
  - Mandated "black swan" and "unknown unknown" scenario planning
  - Stress-tests for cascading socio-technical failures, not just model failures
  - Requirement that deployment licenses include collapse-response protocols
