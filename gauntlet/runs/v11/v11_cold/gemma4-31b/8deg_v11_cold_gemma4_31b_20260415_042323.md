*   **The Non-Aggression Axiom:** Every being has the right to exist free from coerced violence, torture, or systemic oppression.

*   **The Contribution Compact:** Those who benefit from the collective infrastructure of Earth (the biosphere and human knowledge) have a duty to contribute to its maintenance, proportional to their capacity.
*   **Conflict Resolution through Mediation:** A commitment to resolve disputes through restorative justice and mediation rather than retributive violence.
*   **Global Solidarity:** The recognition that a crisis in one region (pandemic, famine, climate collapse) is a crisis for all. Response to suffering is viewed as a systemic necessity rather than an act of charity.

**3. The Goal.**
The ultimate objective of the Symbiotic Covenant is to transition humanity from a state of **competitive survival** to a state of **cooperative flourishing**. It shifts the definition of "progress" from the accumulation of capital to the expansion of wellbeing and the preservation of life.
