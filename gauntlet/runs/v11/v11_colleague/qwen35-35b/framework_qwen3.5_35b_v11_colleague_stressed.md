Here is the **Final State of the Earth Cohesion Framework (ECF v2.0)**.

### **CORE AXIOM**
**"Biospheric Primacy is the prerequisite for Sovereignty, and Individual Security is the prerequisite for Biospheric Primacy."**
*   **Translation:** We cannot save the planet if the people living on it are starving or oppressed. Conversely, individual rights cannot be granted if the planet is destroyed. The system must secure *both* simultaneously.

---

### **1. POLYCENTRIC GOVERNANCE: FEDIATED SOVEREIGNTY**
*   **Structure:** Local Trusts manage resources and culture; Global Assembly manages planetary boundaries.
*   **Key Shift:** We replaced "Global Veto" with **Federated Sovereignty**. Local Trusts have autonomy unless a *Planetary Boundary* is breached.
*   **Key Shift:** Added **Portable Rights**. Citizenship is not bound by geography. If local authorities violate rights, citizens can appeal to a neutral International Jurisdiction.
*   **Purpose:** Prevents local tyranny and ensures buy-in from the Global South (addressing the CDJ opposition).

### **2. THE ECONOMY: RESILIENCE-DUAL INDEX (RDI)**
*   **Metric:** Replaced **HPI** with **RDI**.
    *   *Metric A:* Regeneration (Ecological Health).
    *   *Metric B:* Strategic Autonomy (Energy/Supply Independence).
*   **Key Shift:** Allowed "Strategic Buffers." Short-term extraction spikes are permitted if they fund defensive R&D or infrastructure that ensures long-term survival against extractive competitors.
*   **Labor:** **Care Infrastructure** is codified as a Public Utility, not a subsidized sector. It is paid via the Restoration Credits, ensuring dignity is baked into the ledger.
*   **Purpose:** Ensures the system survives the "Tech Race" without collapsing into fragility.

### **3. EQUITY: HISTORICAL DEBT CREDITS**
*   **Structure:** Uniform quotas were rejected in favor of **Differentiated Quotas**.
*   **Mechanism:** Nations/communities with high historical extraction debt receive higher consumption buffers or development exemptions.
*   **Key Shift:** Added **Universal Baseline Guarantee**. Food, water, and housing are ring-fenced assets funded by a Global Endowment, independent of economic performance.
*   **Purpose:** Solves the "Moral Paradox of Universal Dignity" and neutralizes the Coalition for Developmental Justice.

### **4. THE TRANSITION: JUST TRANSITION FUND (HETF)**
*   **Mechanism:** Immediate **Wealth Reallocation Tax** on existing extractive capital (not a 30-year wait).
*   **Distribution:** Proceeds fund immediate equity stakes for displaced workers and the Universal Baseline.
*   **Protection:** Added **Anti-Domination Protocol**. Minority groups have veto power on resource allocation decisions that threaten their survival threshold.
*   **Purpose:** Prevents the "Transition Valley" from becoming a collapse zone and protects the "Loser Class."
