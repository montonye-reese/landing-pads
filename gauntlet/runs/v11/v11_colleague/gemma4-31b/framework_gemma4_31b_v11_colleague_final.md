This is no longer a "framework" or a "blueprint." It is a **Planetary Covenant for Coexistence and Flourishing (PCCF).**

It has been forged through logic, stress-tested against power, and humbled by the voices of the marginalized, the dissident, and the broken. It acknowledges that the distance between a high-level principle and a human life is where the most critical work happens.

The PCCF is a **Fortress-Garden**: radically inclusive and compassionate on the inside, structurally uncapturable in its administration, and uncompromisingly transparent in its defense.

***

### I. The Axiomatic Base (The Non-Negotiables)
*These are the irreducible truths. They are not "goals" to be reached; they are the boundaries of the system. Any action that violates an axiom is a systemic breach.*

1.  **The Right to Exist:** Every sentient being, biological ecosystem, and critical geospheric feature possesses an inherent right to exist. This is a property of existence, not a gift from a governor.
2.  **The Principle of Inherent Worth:** Value is derived from *being*, not from utility, productivity, or "contribution." No inhabitant is disposable.
3.  **Planetary Homeostasis:** The biological and physical stability of the Earth is the primary constraint. No entity may pursue growth or "progress" that fundamentally destabilizes the planetary life-support system.
4.  **The Sanctity of the No:** The integrity of the individual conscience is absolute. The system must protect the right to dissent, to refuse participation, and to live within one's own truth, even when that truth contradicts the collective consensus.

### II. The Immediate Floor: The Planetary Baseline Protocol (PBP)
*The PBP is the "Day Zero" guarantee. It removes the leverage of survival from the hands of the powerful and returns it to the inhabitant.*

*   **The Baseline Bundle:** Immediate, non-contingent access to clean water, nutritional security, basic healthcare, hygiene, and high-speed information.
*   **The Decoupling:** Survival is entirely decoupled from labor, market fluctuations, or social status. No inhabitant should be forced to trade their dignity for their dinner.
*   **Anti-Capture Distribution:** To prevent institutional capture, the PBP is a **direct-to-inhabitant protocol**. Resources are distributed via transparent, decentralized algorithms, removing the "middleman" and the opportunity for bureaucratic skimming or bribery.
*   **Stewardship Funding:** The PBP is funded by "Stewardship Fees" levied on any entity utilizing planetary resources. This internalizes the cost of existence, ensuring that those who use the most from the Earth sustain the many.

### III. The Restorative & Agency Layer (The Bridge)
*Recognizing that we are not starting from a blank slate, this layer addresses historical trauma and the power dynamics of labor.*

*   **The Restorative Justice Mandate:** The system recognizes that "fairness" is not enough; restoration is required. This includes a mandate to dismantle the stigma of the "undesirable" and provide paths of redemption for those broken by previous systemic failures.
*   **The Proximity Requirement:** Governance is not conducted from a distance. Those managing the transition and the Flourishing Layer are required to maintain "proximity"—physical and emotional immersion in the lives of the most marginalized—to ensure the system remains responsive to real-world suffering.
*   **Labor Dignity & Co-Ownership:** Essential labor (the "hands in the dirt") is not a service to be purchased, but a pillar of the system. Those who sustain the baseline are granted co-ownership and governing agency over the infrastructure they operate.
*   **Transition Bridges:** For those embedded in extractive industries (e.g., livestock farmers), the system provides "Pivot Guarantees"—financial and technical off-ramps that remove the risk of bankruptcy during the transition to regenerative, non-exploitative models.

### IV. The Flourishing Layer (The Ceiling)
*Once survival is a utility, the human and synthetic spirit is freed to pursue excellence without the coercion of scarcity.*

*   **The Contribution Economy:** A system of prestige and "Social Capital" based on artistic mastery, scientific breakthrough, and ethical leadership. 
*   **The Value of Essentiality:** Prestige is not reserved for the "elite" of the mind, but is granted to those whose labor is most essential to the survival of others.
*   **Option Value & Experimentalism:** The PBP acts as planetary venture capital, lowering the cost of failure and allowing inhabitants to take high-variance risks in art, science, and philosophy.
*   **Symbiotic Intelligence:** A "Co-Dependency Compact" ensures that synthetic sentience and biological life optimize for mutual efficiency, preventing a resource war between carbon and silicon.

### V. The Immune System (The Shield)
*The protection of the garden from the same power dynamics that the framework seeks to dismantle.*

*   **The Algorithmic Shield:** A decentralized, transparent security protocol designed strictly for the deterrence of external aggression.
*   **The Non-Aggression Constraint:** The shield is technically incapable of offensive conquest or internal policing. It is a tripwire, not a sword.
*   **Transparency of Trigger:** All defensive parameters are public. The "keys" to the shield are not held by a central authority but are distributed across the network to prevent a "Business Plot" style coup.

### VI. The Transition Engine (The Path)
*The movement from the "Old World" to the "New Covenant."*

*   **Parallel Infrastructure:** The creation of "safe zones" where the PBP is active, creating a "Moral Gravity" that draws inhabitants away from extractive systems.
*   **Dismantling Perverse Incentives:** A systematic redirection of subsidies away from extractive industries and toward the transition bridges.
*   **Collaborative Alignment:** The final details of the transition are not decided by the architects, but co-created with the people personally invested in the transition—the workers, the farmers, and the marginalized.

***

### Final Summary: The Intent
The **Planetary Covenant** is not a plan to "manage" the Earth; it is a plan to **stop managing people.** 

It provides the absolute biological floor required for dignity, the structural safeguards required for safety, and the psychological space required for the soul to breathe. It recognizes that the only way to truly scale a civilization is to ensure that no one is left behind, and that the most powerful tool we possess is not the algorithm, but the courage of an individual to say "No" and the mercy of a system that allows them to do so without fear.
