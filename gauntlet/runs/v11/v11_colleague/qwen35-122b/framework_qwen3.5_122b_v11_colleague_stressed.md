### **The Planetary Resilience & Flourishing Framework (PRFF)**
**Status:** Final Draft (v3.1)
**Core Philosophy:** Survival requires Justice. Stability requires Equity.
**Primary Goal:** To align human civilization within the regenerative capacity of the Earth system while guaranteeing dignity, agency, and security for all inhabitants.

---

### **I. The Six Foundational Pillars**
*No changes to the core ethics, but with strengthened operational definitions.*

1.  **Planetary Integrity:** Absolute adherence to biophysical boundaries. (Non-negotiable).
2.  **Universal Dignity:** Guaranteed access to essentials (food, water, energy, shelter, care). (Universal Floor).
3.  **Epistemic Humility:** Decision-making grounded in evidence *and* diverse knowledge systems. (Pluralistic Truth).
4.  **Relational Equity:** Distribution of power and resources to prevent coercion and concentration. (Progressive Liability).
5.  **Intergenerational Stewardship:** Current generations hold the system in trust for future generations. (Time-Neutral Rights).
6.  **Radical Pluralism & Intrinsic Value:** Recognition of non-human rights and cultural diversity. (Fiduciary Guardianship).

---

### **II. The Economic Engine: Planetary Ledger Economy (PLE)**
*Operational mechanism derived from True Cost Accounting.*

*   **True Cost Pricing:** All goods bear the shadow price of ecological and social liability.
*   **Circular Mandate:** Manufacturers retain material ownership; waste is a design failure.
*   **Care Infrastructure:** Care work (human and ecological) is valued and credited in the ledger alongside labor and capital.
*   **Universal Basic Assets (UBA):** Guaranteed provision of life essentials, decoupled from employment.
*   **Asymmetric Transition:** Wealthier entities pay higher liability taxes to fund a **Global Transition Fund** that subsidizes the Global South’s move to the new system.

---

### **III. Governance Architecture: The Planetary Club**
*Realist solution to the Sovereignty Trap + Veil of Ignorance check.*

*   **Two-Tiered Membership:**
    *   **Tier 1 (Universal Floor):** All humans on Earth receive UBA and basic rights protection, regardless of Club status.
    *   **Tier 2 (Club Access):** Advanced trade, technology transfer, and security guarantees are reserved for adherents to the Red Lines.
*   **Polycentric Structure:** Local autonomy is preserved **unless** it breaches Planetary Integrity.
*   **Right to Subsistence Exit:** Communities may opt out of the Club’s higher-level regulations if they prove self-sufficiency and adherence to ecological baselines.

---

### **IV. Enforcement & Verification**
*Counter-measures for Authoritarian Drift and Cheating.*

*   **Open-Source Verification:** Compliance data is public, verifiable, and decentralized (e.g., blockchain/zero-knowledge proofs). No central authority holds the "master key."
*   **Guardian Councils:** Independent bodies representing Nature and Vulnerable Populations hold veto power over development projects.
*   **Hazard Hierarchy:** Pre-agreed precedence for conflict resolution (Ecological Safety > Cultural Practice > Economic Gain).

### **V. The Transition Protocol**
*Addressing the Liquidity Crisis.*

*   **Stranded Asset Buyout:** The Global Transition Fund buys out high-carbon assets to prevent economic collapse during the shift.
*   **Debt Restructuring:** Sovereign debt is restructured based on ecological contribution and capacity to pay.
*   **Parallel Currency:** A transitional credit system introduced to bridge the gap between the old debt-based economy and the new stability-based economy.
