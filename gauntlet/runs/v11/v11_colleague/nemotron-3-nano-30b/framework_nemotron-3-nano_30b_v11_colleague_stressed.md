|--------|----------------------------|--------------------------------------------------|

| **B. | **C. Interlinked Governance** | *How do we coordinate across scales without collapsing into hierarchy or anarchy?* | • **World Council of Earth Stewards (WCES)** – a *rotating, citizen‑elected* body (1,000 delegates, 1 % of global population) that **aggregates feedback** from all layers. <br>• **Regional Economic Councils (RECs)** – multi‑stakeholder bodies (businesses, Indigenous councils, NGOs, academia) that translate WEI targets into *local incentive packages*. <br>• **Legal‑Pluralism Protocol** – any law must be drafted in at least three legal traditions (civil, customary, religious) and pass a **multi‑jurisdiction compatibility review** before adoption. |
| **D. Sustainable Prosperity** | *How do we generate wealth without breaking planetary boundaries?* | • **Well‑Being Economics Index (WEI)** replaces GDP; it aggregates **Ecological Integrity, Human Dignity, Economic Security, and Cultural Flourishing**. <br>• **Regenerative Accounting Standards** (Regenerative Monetary Units) – every monetary transaction is logged on the **Global Regenerative Ledger** with a *debit* (cost) and a *credit* (regenerative benefit). <br>• **Circular Value‑Chain Hubs** (local, regional) that convert waste streams into new products; **Circular Token (LCT)** system incentivises material reuse. <br>• **Universal Regenerative Basic Income (URBI)** – a cash flow that rises when WEI improves and falls when ecological thresholds are breached. |

## 3.  Core Metrics & Data Architecture

| Metric / Tool | Purpose | How It Guarantees Honest Moral Scrutiny |
|--------------|---------|----------------------------------------|
| **Earth Stewardship Index (ESI)** | Tracks planetary‑boundary health (biodiversity, carbon, nitrogen, water). | Auto‑feeds into the **Global Resilience Review**; breaches trigger *automatic corrective levies* on the ledger. |
| **Well‑Being Economics Index (WEI)** | Composite score of human dignity, security, cultural flourishing, and ecological integrity. | Serves as the *single budgetary yardstick*; all fiscal decisions (taxes, URBI, public spending) are contingent on staying within the WEI envelope. |
| **Regenerative Monetary Units (RMU)** | Price‑tags that embed ecological and social externalities into every transaction. | All financial statements must display **RMU debits/credits**; non‑compliant entities are blocked from market access. |
| **Global Regenerative Ledger (GRL)** | Public, blockchain‑style ledger that records every RMU transaction, co‑ownership share, and policy decision. | Fully **open‑source**, auditable by anyone; any tampering triggers an automatic audit and sanction. |
| **Narrative Amplification Engine (NAE)** | Produces multilingual, evidence‑based stories that showcase real regenerative successes. | Countered misinformation; ensures that **public perception** is shaped by verified outcomes, not by corporate PR. |
