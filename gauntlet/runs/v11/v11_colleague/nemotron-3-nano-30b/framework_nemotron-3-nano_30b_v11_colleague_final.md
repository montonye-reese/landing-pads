## 2.  Layers & Governance Architecture  

1. **Local (Layer 1 – Community Commons)**  
   - *Community Regeneration Councils (CRCs)* – elected, gender‑balanced, include Indigenous custodians, neuro‑diverse and cultural‑minority delegates.  
   - **Local Currency (TOKEN)** – redeemable only for goods/services produced or regenerated within the community.  
   - **Community Capital Fund** (micro‑grant pool) financed by token sales and matched by the Macro‑Financial Stabilisation Fund (MFSTF).  

2. **Regional (Layer 2)**  
   - **Regional Regeneration Councils (RCCs)** – multi‑stakeholder bodies (municipalities, Indigenous councils, farmer cooperatives, SMEs).  
   - **Regional Economic Resilience Funds** (financed by Renewable‑Energy Investment Bonds) channel capital to circular‑value‑chain hubs and renewable‑energy upgrades.  

3. **Regional Networks & National‑Level Governance** (Layer 3)  
   - **Regional Economic Council (RCE)** – multi‑stakeholder body that translates WEI outcomes into **policy incentives** (tax rebates, low‑interest green bonds, preferential market access).  
   - **Regional Financial Reserve (RF)** holds emergency reserves, renewable‑energy assets, and seed funding for circular‑innovation pilots.  

4. **Global Layer (Layer 4 – Emergent Planetary Layer)**  
   - **World Council of Earth Stewards (WCES‑EC)** – 300 rotating citizen‑elected delegates (1 per 100 000 residents) plus 12 **non‑human rights delegates** (indigenous, disabled, future‑generation).  
   - **Macro‑Financial Stabilisation Fund (MFSTF)** – funded by a **0.05 % levy on all speculative financial transactions** (derivatives, derivatives of derivatives). Revenues fund: (a) the **Global Resilience Reserve**, (b) **Emergency Green‑Bond purchases**, (b) the **Macro‑Financial Stabilisation Tax** on speculative transactions.  
   - **Global Regenerative Ledger (GRL)** – public, immutable, open‑source ledger that records **all RMUs, token transactions, tax receipts, and policy actions**. All entries are immutable, auditable, and visible to any citizen.  

5. **Emergent Planetary Layer** (the “Planetary Council”)  
   - **World Council of Earth Stewards – Economic Sub‑Council (WCES‑EC)** – decides on planetary‑budget caps, planetary‑boundary thresholds, and planetary‑wide policy.  
   - **Planetary‑Boundary Threshold Monitors** (N‑balance, carbon, ocean health, etc.) feed real‑time data into the **Global Resilience Review**.  
   - **Emergency Crisis Response Board** can temporarily suspend non‑essential permits, impose temporary *Carbon‑Budget Caps*, and automatically **release resilience assets** from the Resilience Reserve.
