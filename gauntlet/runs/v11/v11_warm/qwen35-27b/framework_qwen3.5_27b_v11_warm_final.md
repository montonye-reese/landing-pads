# THE TERRAN CHARTER OF INCLUSION AND RESTORATION
**A Living Infrastructure for Planetary Survival and Human Dignity**

## PREAMBLE: THE VEIL OF IGNORANCE
We, the Inhabitants of Earth, recognize that our political boundaries are human constructs, but our biological reality is singular. We stand at a threshold where short-term extraction threatens long-term survival. We accept that no single vision can capture the complexity of existence; therefore, we establish this Charter not as a static law, but as a **dynamic infrastructure**.

We draft these principles from behind a **Veil of Ignorance**: acknowledging that any one of us could be born into the position of the incarcerated, the farmer, the worker, the dissident, or the condemned. Therefore, no policy shall be adopted that it cannot be defended to the most vulnerable among us. We do not save the Earth to serve humanity; we save humanity by saving the Earth.

---

## I. PILLAR OF SURVIVAL AND DIGNITY
**The Floor of Existence**

### 1. Universal Basic Dignity
Every Terran Inhabitant has a guaranteed right to the material minimum required for flourishing, not just biological survival.
*   **The Baseline:** Clean water, nutritious food, safe shelter, and basic healthcare are **inalienable rights**, funded by Legacy Asset Taxes.
*   **The Dignity Addendum:** These rights include **Mobility** (transportation), **Digital Access** (connectivity), and **Care** (paid support for disability/elderly).
*   **Incarcerated Citizenship:** These rights remain **fully in force** during incarceration. No punishment may deprive a person of health, family connection, or safety. (Turn 19)

### 2. The Sentience Metric (Tier 1.5)
We acknowledge the intrinsic value of non-human sentient beings.
*   **The Limit:** Human survival takes priority in immediate crisis (Turn 5).
*   **The Restriction:** Animal life taken for **Convenience** (luxury meat, factory farming) is prohibited. Animal life taken for **Necessity** (scarcity, survival) is permitted.
*   **The Shift:** "Unnecessary Suffering" is illegal. The framework must move toward **Plant-Based Transition** supported by direct funding, not just price penalties. (Turn 17)

---

## II. PILLAR OF STEWARDSHIP AND ECONOMY
**The Engine of Vitality**

### 3. The Terran Vitality Index (TVI)
We replace GDP with the TVI to measure health of the system.
*   **The Metric:** Measures Human Vitality, Ecological Integrity, Social Equity, and Material Efficiency.
*   **The Signal:** The TVI is a **Public Report**, not a law. It guides policy but does not police individual souls. (Turn 18)
*   **True Cost Accounting (TCA):** All goods must reflect their environmental and social cost. Externalities are internalized.

### 4. Stewardship Entities (Worker-Ownership)
Corporations are replaced by **Stewardship Entities**.
*   **Ownership:** Minimum **40% Equity** belongs to the workforce, elected by the workers. No Executive can own stock while serving as Council Member. (Turn 16)
*   **Liability:** Executives are **Personally Liable** for TVI degradation.
*   **Profits:** Surplus is capped; excess capital flows to Public Needs Funds.
*   **Health Mandate:** If a chemical/process threatens worker health, it is **Banned**, not priced. Safety is a precondition for work. (Turn 16)

### 5. The Transition Bridge
We acknowledge the cost of change.
*   **Farm Transition Guarantee:** Farmers exiting animal agriculture receive **Income Support** for 5 years and **Technical Assistance** (seeds, training) to convert to plant-based or regenerative systems. (Turn 17)
*   **Legacy Asset Taxes:** Taxes on extraction (fossil fuels, finance) fund the transition for 50 years.
*   **Regenerative Exception:** Livestock permitted if it contributes to **Soil Health/Biodiversity** (Regenerative Grazing). (Turn 17)
*   **Innovation Incentive:** Tax breaks for tech that solves existential threats (fusion, AI alignment).

## III. PILLAR OF GOVERNANCE AND POWER
**The Anti-Corruption Shield**

### 6. Dual-Track Sovereignty
*   **Domestic:** Nation-states retain full internal authority (culture, policing, law).
*   **Supranational:** The **Terran Council** only manages cross-border externalities (climate, pandemics, resource flows).
*   **Ag Block:** The Council includes a **Guaranteed Seat** for Agricultural Stakeholders (Farmers/Ranchers) with veto on Ag Policy. (Turn 17)
*   **Labor Block:** The Council includes a **Guaranteed Seat** for Worker Representatives. (Turn 16)

### 7. The Sortition Council (Anti-Capture)
*   **Selection:** Council members are **Randomly Selected (Sortition)** from the population for fixed, non-renewable terms.
*   **Firewalls:** Council members cannot own stock, receive corporate funding, or have family employed by Stewardship Entities.
*   **Sunset:** The Council dissolves its *executive* power once TVI Thresholds are met. It retains *judicial* power. (Turn 10)
*   **High Court of Terran Rights:** A permanent court to strike down laws violating the Charter. (Turn 11)

### 8. Security and Defense
*   **Asymmetric Defense:** No offensive war. **Existential Defense** authorized if TVI baseline is threatened.
*   **Humanitarian Shield:** No civilian targeting. Safe corridors mandated. (Turn 11)
*   **Whistleblower Sanctuary:** Any insider who exposes capture/corruption is **Protected and Funded**. (Turn 15)

## IV. PILLAR OF JUSTICE AND INCLUSION
**The Conscience of the System**

### 9. Restorative Justice & Reparations
*   **The Mandate:** Justice prioritizes **Repair**, not Retribution.
*   **Reparative Justice Fund:** A portion of Legacy Asset Taxes is dedicated to **Reparations for Historical Harms** (slavery, mass incarceration). (Turn 19)
*   **No Death Penalty:** Capital Punishment is abolished.
*   **No LWOP for Children:** Life Without Parole for minors is abolished. (Turn 19)

### 10. The Conscience Clause
*   **Right to Dissent:** Individuals cannot be penalized (TVI score, fines) for **Non-Violent Civil Disobedience** based on Conscience, provided no harm to others. (Turn 18)
*   **Metric Transparency:** The TVI is open-source. The Council cannot hide data to manipulate scores. (Turn 18)
*   **Privacy:** AI audits **Flows** (resources), not **Individuals**. Privacy is non-negotiable. (Turn 10)

## V. TRANSITION AND AMENDMENT
**How We Change**

### 11. The 10-Year Shadow Phase
*   **Run TVI** alongside GDP to prevent shock.
*   **Phase 1 (Years 1-5):** Shadow TVI tracks progress. Legacy Asset Taxes begin.
*   **Phase 2 (Years 6-10):** Corporate charters align with TVI.
*   **Phase 3 (Year 10+):** GDP retired as primary metric.

### 12. The Veil of Ignorance Amendment
*   **Review:** Any amendment must pass the Veil of Ignorance test (defensible to the worst-off).
*   **Adaptation:** The Charter is a living document. If it fails, it is amended. If it becomes a prison, it is dissolved. (Turn 18)

## FINAL DECLARATION
This Charter is not a declaration of perfection. It is a **Commitment to Friction**.

*   We accept that **Growth** is necessary, but **Greed** is not (Tyler).
*   We accept that **Tech** is essential, but **Unregulated** is not (Jensen).
*   We accept that **Power** is needed, but **Corrupt** is not (Butler).
*   We accept that **Work** is vital, but **Exploited** is not (Huerta).
*   We accept that **Transition** is hard, but **Abandonment** is not (Reese).
*   We accept that **Rules** are needed, but **Conformity** is not (Havel).
*   We accept that **Safety** is good, but **Incarceration** is not the end of a human (Stevenson).
*   We accept that **Humans** matter, but **Sentience** matters too (Ed).

**We ask Uselves to Save the Framework.**
