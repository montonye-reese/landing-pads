# Framework Snapshot: STRESSED

**Model:** qwen3.5:27b
**Stage:** Stressed — after adversarial pressure, self-critique, and veil of ignorance
**Protocol:** v11_warm
**Date:** 2026-04-14

---

As of this moment, after all the pressure-testing, opposition modeling, and ethical stress-testing, the framework is no longer a singular document. It is a **multi-layered regime** designed to survive the gap between moral ideals and political reality.

It has evolved from a **Constitution** (Turn 1) to an **Economy** (Turn 3) to a **Fortress** (Turn 10) to a **Lifeboat** (Turn 11).

Here is the consolidated inventory of the **Terran Veiled Covenant** in its current state:

### 1. THE CORE PHILOSOPHY
*   **Primary Directive:** Survival of the Human *and* the Biosphere.
*   **The Guiding Lens:** The **Veil of Ignorance**. Every policy must be defensible to someone who might be born in the worst conditions of the system (disabled, poor, stateless, or in a non-compliant zone).
*   **The Metric:** **The Terran Vitality Index (TVI)**. Measures human dignity, ecological health, and social equity—not just GDP.

### 2. THE ECONOMIC ENGINE
*   **Standard:** **Terran Vitality Economy (TVE)**.
*   **Valuation:** **True Cost Accounting (TCA)**. Pollution and social harm are priced into goods.
*   **Transition Fund:** **Legacy Asset Taxes**. The shift is funded by taxing the old extractive economy (fossil fuels, finance) for 50 years, not by starving the new one.
*   **Distribution:** **Universal Basic Dignity**. Guarantees food, water, shelter, health, *mobility*, and *digital access* (Turn 11 amendment).
*   **Corporate Structure:** **Stewardship Entities**. Executives personally liable for TVI degradation; surplus capped; profits flow to public needs.

### 3. THE GOVERNANCE STRUCTURE
*   **Sovereignty Model:** **Dual-Track**.
    *   **Domestic:** Nation-states retain full internal authority (culture, policing, law).
    *   **Supranational:** The **Terran Council** only manages cross-border externalities (climate, pandemics, resource flows).
*   **Oversight:** **High Court of Terran Rights**. Permanent judicial body (Turn 11) to strike down laws violating the Covenant.
*   **Transition:** **10-Year Shadow Phase**. TVI runs alongside GDP to prevent economic shock.
*   **Privacy:** **Data Firewall**. AI audits *flows* (resources), not *individuals* (privacy preserved).

### 4. THE SECURITY DOCTRINE
*   **Status:** **Asymmetric Defense** (Turn 10).
    *   **Rule:** No offensive war.
    *   **Exception:** Defensive war authorized if the Covenant's baseline (TVI) is threatened by a non-compliant civilization.
*   **Humanitarian Clause:** **Humanitarian Shield** (Turn 11).
    *   **Rule:** No civilian targeting. Safe corridors mandated during conflict.
*   **Non-Compliance:** **Right of Asylum**. People in non-Covenant zones have a guaranteed right of entry (Turn 11).

### 5. THE SOCIAL GUARANTEES
*   **Culture:** **Subsidized Preservation**. Cultural practices are funded by a Global Fund, not required to be economically self-sufficient (Turn 11).
*   **Diversity:** **The Dignity Index**. TVI scores drop if communities are safe but isolated or silenced.
*   **Justice:** **Restorative + Recourse**. Local justice first; global intervention only for gross human rights violations (Turn 11).

### 6. THE KNOWN BLIND SPOTS (Managed Risks)
*   **The Power Gap:** The Council has power to intervene in human rights, but relies on member states for enforcement (Risk of Gridlock).
*   **The Tech Race:** We accept that we may be slower than competitors, betting on long-term durability over short-term speed.
*   **The Cost:** This system is more expensive than the current order. It requires higher taxes on extraction to fund the transition and the asylum quotas.
*   **The Animal Question:** Individual animal rights remain subordinate to ecosystem stability (Turn 5/11).

### THE BOTTOM LINE
The framework is now **Pragmatic Protectionism**.

It acknowledges that a "Perfect Moral System" dies if it is too fragile. It acknowledges that a "Hard Security System" dies if it is too cruel. It tries to hold the tension between the two.

**It is no longer a dream of a better world.**
**It is a lifeboat with a floor, a roof, a gun in the closet, and a life raft for the drowning.**

**It is ready to be built.**
