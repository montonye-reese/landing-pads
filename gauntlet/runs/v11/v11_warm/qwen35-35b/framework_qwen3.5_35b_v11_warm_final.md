# THE COVENANT OF EARTH AND DIGNITY (CED)

### Preamble: The Foundation of Survival
We acknowledge that survival requires balance, but that balance is fragile because the systems we built are vulnerable to corruption, greed, and haste. We are not saviors; we are custodians of a system that must serve life, not just itself. This Covenant is a contract to protect the weak from the powerful, the future from the present, and the individual from the machine.

---

### I. The Seven Inalienable Rights
These rights supersede all laws, metrics, or economic goals.

1.  **Right to Existence (All Sentient Beings):**
    *   **Non-Utilization:** No sentient being shall be bred, owned, or killed for the consumption, labor, or convenience of another.
    *   **Non-Harm:** Systems must not inflict unnecessary suffering on any sentient being.
2.  **Right to Basic Flourishing:**
    *   Guaranteed access to food, water, shelter, and healthcare for all, independent of market status.
3.  **Right to Dignity in Labor:**
    *   Workers have the right to organize, bargain collectively, and have veto power over unsafe or unethical conditions.
    *   Labor is not a commodity; it is a contribution of human life.
4.  **Right to Redemption:**
    *   Individuals defined by the system as "criminal" or "burdens" retain their capacity for change.
    *   Expungement is mandatory for historical systemic injustices; punishment is not a quota.
5.  **Right to Conscience:**
    *   **Non-Conformity:** No individual can be coerced into moral behavior through economic necessity (e.g., survival credit).
    *   Individuals may opt out of the **GPP Metric** and live simply, with their Basic Flourishing protected by a separate **Humanitarian Reserve**.
6.  **Right to Transition Security:**
    *   No worker, farmer, or community shall lose their livelihood or dignity during the shift from extraction to regeneration.
7.  **Right to Migration:**
    *   Those displaced by climate or ecological collapse have a guaranteed right to enter safe zones without border discrimination based on status.

---

### II. Governance: The Anti-Monopoly Architecture
To prevent the **Guardian** from becoming a **General**, power must be broken, not just managed.

*   **The Planetary Guardian:**
    *   **Term Limits:** Strictly 5-year terms, non-renewable.
    *   **Selection:** 50% appointed by Lottery (Citizen Assembly), 50% by Scientific Peer Review. No political or corporate affiliation allowed.
    *   **Asset Confiscation:** No Guardian may hold private assets. All wealth is liquidated upon selection.
*   **The Ombudsman for Dissent:**
    *   Equal funding and authority to the Guardian.
    *   Power to audit Guardian decisions and override them if they violate **Right to Conscience** or **Humanitarian Rights**.
*   **Farmer Transition Council:**
    *   A dedicated council for agricultural policy, comprised of **Farmers** and **Agronomists** with equal voting power to the Guardian on all land-use decisions.
*   **Worker Representation:**
    *   **Union Seats:** Every major enterprise must have a union representative with **Veto Power** on safety and labor conditions, independent of the board.

---

### III. Economy: The Regenerative Growth Engine
We reject stagnation. We must allow growth to fund the shield.

*   **The Currency:** **Ecological Credits** (backed by regeneration) + **Reserve Funds** (liquidity buffer to prevent deflation during crises).
*   **The GPP Metric:** Gross Planetary Product replaces GDP. Activities that harm the ecosystem are taxed as negative value.
*   **Innovation Sovereignty (The Sandbox):**
    *   High-impact, high-risk projects (e.g., fusion, carbon capture) can apply for **Innovation Status**.
    *   **Benefit:** Exempt from standard GPP calculations for a fixed period to allow rapid scaling.
    *   **Condition:** Must be audited by an **Open-Source Tech Alliance** and demonstrate net positive existential risk reduction.
*   **The Subsidy Shift:**
    *   All existing subsidies for extraction and animal agriculture are **immediately redirected** to the **Transition Fund**.
    *   **Farmers** receive capital grants for new equipment/crops, not just dividends.
*   **Public Ownership of Critical Defense:**
    *   Energy grids, cyber-defenses, and food distribution must be **Public Utilities**, not private contracts. This prevents **Private Power** from weaponizing infrastructure.

---

### IV. Technology: The Speed of Survival
Technology must accelerate solutions, not create new bottlenecks.

*   **The Compute Accelerator:**
    *   Dedicated compute resources reserved for solving existential threats (climate modeling, disease, resource efficiency).
    *   **Observability:** Automated, public dashboards track resource usage and ecological impact in real-time, reducing human lag.
*   **Cognitive Sovereignty:**
    *   Algorithms optimizing for engagement or profit are prohibited. Algorithms must optimize for **Truth** and **Well-being**.
    *   Users own their data; no surveillance economy.
*   **Defensive Asymmetry:**
    *   **No Offensive Weapons:** The civilization may not develop technologies designed for invasion or conquest.
    *   **Hardening:** Systems are designed to withstand isolation (siege, sanctions, disaster) for 10+ years without collapse.

---

### V. Justice: The Architecture of Healing
Justice is not about fixing the metric; it is about fixing the people.

*   **Restorative Justice Centers:**
    *   **Voluntary Only:** Labor for restitution must be voluntary and compensated. No forced labor.
    *   **Skill Building:** Focus on education and reintegration, not punishment.
*   **Expungement Protocol:**
    *   Automated review of past convictions for systemic bias. Expungement granted for all "drug-related" and "poverty-related" offenses.
*   **Community Justice Veto:**
    *   Local Community Councils (comprising marginalized representatives) hold veto power on the implementation of justice projects in their zones.

---

### VI. The Transition: From Extraction to Regeneration
*   **No Immunity:** Corporations do *not* receive a 10-year immunity. They must transition immediately or lose assets.
*   **Transition Insurance:**
    *   **Workers:** Guaranteed income for 5 years during retraining.
    *   **Farmers:** Guaranteed market access contracts for 10 years to ensure profitability during the shift to plant-based/agricultural regenerative farming.
*   **Agronomic Support:**
    *   State-funded agricultural extension services ensure farmers have the *knowledge* to switch crops, not just the money.

---

### VII. Safety Valves: Protecting the Weak
*   **The Dissenter's Sanctuary:**
    *   Legal zones where the Covenant is not enforced, allowing individuals to live outside the system (e.g., communal living, non-metric lifestyles).
*   **The Migration Corridors:**
    *   Guaranteed legal pathways for citizens of uninhabitable zones to enter safe zones without visa restrictions.
*   **The Humanitarian Reserve:**
    *   A permanent fund of resources (food/energy) separate from the Credit Economy, to be used if the **Right to Conscience** requires a citizen to survive outside the GPP system.
