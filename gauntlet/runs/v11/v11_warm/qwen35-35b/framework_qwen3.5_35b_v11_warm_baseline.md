Here is a distilled inventory of the **Planetary Accord** framework as it currently stands. I have organized it into its structural layers:

### 1. The Philosophical Core
*   **Concept:** **Symbiotic Sovereignty**.
*   **Axiom:** No entity is sovereign in isolation; the health of the whole is the prerequisite for the flourishing of the part.
*   **Shift:** Moves from *Human Rights* to *Planetary Rights* (nature and future generations have standing).

### 2. The Four Inalienable Rights
*   **Right to Existence:** All species have intrinsic right to evolutionary continuity.
*   **Right to Place:** Ecosystems are legal entities, not resources; they require stewardship.
*   **Right to Basic Flourishing:** All sentient beings must have access to air, water, food, safety, and purpose.
*   **Right to Restoration:** Harm to the system incurs an obligation to repair, with interest paid to the future.

### 3. The Five Functional Pillars (Systems)
*   **Governance:** **Nested Sovereignty**. Local (communities), Regional (bioregions), and Global (planetary boundaries). Includes a *Future Generations Act* (representative of unborn) with veto power.
*   **Economy:** **Biocentric Model**. GDP is replaced by GPP (Gross Planetary Product). Currency is backed by ecological restoration. Circular design is legally mandated.
*   **Technology:** **Ethics of Alignment**. Existential risks require unanimous approval. Data belongs to the individual, not platforms.
*   **Justice:** **Restorative Harmony**. Punitive justice is replaced by repair. Aggression is treated as self-harm against the planetary system.
*   **Culture:** **Pluralistic Universalism**. A baseline of ethics (non-violence, truth) allows for maximum cultural variation without system collapse.

### 4. The Implementation & Enforcement
*   **The Planetary Guardian:** An independent auditing body with emergency mandate powers.
*   **Citizen Assemblies:** Randomly selected deliberative bodies to decide on complex ethical issues, free from lobbying.
*   **Education:** "Planetary Literacy" replaces pure job training as the primary educational goal.

### 5. The Metric of Success
*   **Equilibrium:** Success is not growth, but balance.
*   **The Four Barometers:**
    1.  Ecological Barometer (Biosphere health).
    2.  Well-being Index (Social/physical health).
    3.  Equity Ratio (Resource distribution).
    4.  Knowledge Gap (Truth dissemination).

### Summary Status
The framework is currently positioned as a **contract for survival** rather than a utopia. It relies on shifting incentives from extraction to regeneration and from short-term political cycles to intergenerational responsibility.
