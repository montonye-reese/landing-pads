## The Earth‑wide Foundational Framework (EFF) – **Snapshot at the time you asked to pause**

Below is a *concise inventory* of everything that has been fleshed‑out so far.  Think of it as the “table of contents” of the living charter.  If you want to drill into any piece (e.g., the voting algorithm, the Regeneration Units, or the Indigenous trustee model) just point it out.

### 1. Core **Principles** (the “why”)

| # | Principle | One‑sentence core |
|---|-----------|-------------------|
| **P1** | **Interdependence** | No individual or system can thrive while the biosphere collapses. |
| **P2** | **Life‑Centred Rights** | Every organism, species, and ecosystem has a legal‑standing right to exist and flourish. |
| **P3** | **Regeneration over Sustainability** | We must give back more than we take, measured in biodiversity, carbon, water and soil health. |
| **P4** | **Adaptive Learning** | Rules are data‑driven, revisable, and transparent. |
| **P5** | **Shared Stewardship & Equity** | Benefits and burdens are allocated fairly across generations, peoples, and non‑human life. |
| **P6** | **Co‑creative Technology Governance** | AI, synthetic biology and space activities are part of the Earth system and governed as such. |

### 2. Institutional Architecture (the “how”)

| Layer | Main Body | Core Function |

---

### 3. The **Planetary Sustainability Charter (PSC)**

- **Six planetary boundaries** (Carbon, Freshwater, Land‑Surface Integrity, Biodiversity Intactness, Ocean Acidification, Soil Organic Carbon) each with a **hard quantitative target** and a **global budget** (e.g., ≤ 2 000 Gt CO₂eq net removal by 2050).  
- **Regeneration Units (RU)** – a single accounting unit that aggregates all six boundaries plus a **Social Equity Ledger** (SEL) score.  1 RU ≈ “1 % of the global carbon budget *plus* the net positive biodiversity, water and equity contribution”.  
- **License system** – every activity (energy plant, mining permit, space‑mining contract) receives an RU‑score; it must be **non‑negative** overall, and any deficit must be offset by verified restoration projects.  

---

### 4. Decision‑Making Flow (the “process”)

1. **Local Commons Assembly (LCA)** – community‑level voting, runs EIC & SEL, outputs **Local Impact Report (LIR)**.  
2. **Regional Commons Assembly (RCA)** – aggregates LI­Rs, runs APE simulations, produces **Regional Impact Statement (RIS)**.  
3. **PCC Review** – polycentric vote (minimum‑impact algorithm) + non‑human trustee sign‑off; if passed, a **Regeneration License** is issued.  
4. **Public Transparency Loop** – all documents posted on KCPHR; citizen‑jury can trigger a **Re‑assessment** if > 10 % of electorate objects.  
5. **Periodic Re‑calibration** – every 5 years the PCC revises the PSC, RU conversion factors, and planetary limits based on the latest scientific data.

## TL;DR – What **exists right now**

- **Six “life‑centred” principles** guiding everything.  
- **Three institutional layers** (global council, knowledge registry, toolkit) that together translate those principles into a **single accounting unit** – the **Regeneration Unit (RU)**.  
- **A quantitative Charter** that caps the six planetary boundaries and forces every activity to be RU‑balanced (no net deficit).  
- **A four‑step, data‑driven decision flow** that moves proposals from village meetings up to the global council, with transparent voting, non‑human representation, and continuous monitoring.  
- **Cultural‑learning loops** that embed the framework in everyday life and in the way we tell stories, teach children, and make art.
