---

### **The Planetary Covenant (Revised Edition)**
**Status:** Dynamic Transition Treaty (50-Year Horizon)

#### **Preamble: The Contract for Continuity**
*   **Survival First:** We acknowledge that the transition to sustainability requires sacrifice, discomfort, and the redistribution of power.
*   **Systemic Integrity:** We are part of the biosphere, not owners of it. Economic systems must serve ecological limits, not vice versa.
*   **Justice as Defense:** Rights are protected not just by law, but by redundancy and decentralization.

---

### **I. The Five Pillars (Updated with Mechanisms)**

**1. Basic Dignity (The Floor)**
*   **Universal Guarantee:** Clean air, water, food, energy, and housing are non-negotiable rights.
*   **Transition Safety Net:** Guaranteed income, retraining, and community reinvestment for workers displaced by Covenant compliance (e.g., fossil fuel miners).
*   **No Emergency Exception:** Civil liberties (dignity, safety) cannot be suspended even during ecological crises.

**2. Planetary Integrity (The Boundary)**
*   **Extraction ≤ Regeneration:** Hard limits on carbon, water, and material flows.
*   **Bio-Sensor Representation:** Ecosystems (rivers, forests) are represented by **autonomous data networks** (not just human guardians) that trigger legal action based on real-time biological health.
*   **Biodiversity as Intrinsic Value:** All living systems have rights to exist independent of human utility.

**3. Equitable Agency (The Voice)**
*   **Differentiated Responsibilities:** Historical polluters (Global North) fund and lead the transition; developing nations (Global South) have extended timelines and support.
*   **Future Veto Power:** A randomly selected **Steward Council** representing future generations holds a binding veto on irreversible risks (e.g., carbon lock-in).
*   **Indigenous Sovereignty:** Indigenous Nations are distinct signatories with treaty rights, not sub-units of local governance.
*   **Right to Opacity:** Vulnerable individuals (dissidents, refugees) have guaranteed anonymity and encryption to protect against persecution.

**4. Ethical Innovation (The Horizon)**
*   **Cognitive Commons:**
    *   **Data Sovereignty:** Personal data resides in individual vaults, not corporate servers.
    *   **Attention Ledger:** Value generated from attention/data flows back to the subject.
    *   **Algorithmic Audit:** Algorithms must prioritize well-being over engagement; "infinite scroll" is prohibited.
*   **Cultural Pluralism:** Universal *conditions* (safety, ecology) do not mandate universal *lifestyles*.
*   **Precautionary Principle:** Safety and reversibility precede deployment.

**5. Restorative & Transitional Justice (The Repair)**
*   **Repair over Punishment:** Focus on restoring harm to victims and communities.
*   **Ecological Liability:** Strict liability for environmental damage; polluters pay for cleanup.
*   **Physical Verification:** Compliance is verified by ground-level citizen observers and bio-sensors, not just corporate self-reporting (countering Goodhart’s Law).

---

### **II. Implementation & Transition Mechanisms**

*   **50-Year Transition Window:**
    *   **Phase 1 (0–10 Years):** Decoupling growth from extraction; building infrastructure.
    *   **Phase 2 (10–30 Years):** Full implementation of planetary boundaries.
    *   **Phase 3 (30–50 Years):** Steady-state restoration.
*   **Resilience Security Pact:**
    *   Collective defense against non-compliant states threatening the biosphere (e.g., resource embargoes).
    *   Priority on **Decentralized Redundancy** (local energy, supply chains) to prevent systemic collapse.
*   **Sacrifice Clause:**
    *   Explicit acknowledgment that convenience and consumption must be reduced to meet planetary limits.

---

### **III. Governance & Enforcement**

*   **Citizen Oversight (Sortition):** Randomly selected assemblies have veto power over systemic risks and audit institutions.
*   **Planetary Impact Rating:** Transparent rating for nations and goods, tied to trade and credit access.
*   **Whistleblower Immunity:** Enhanced legal and financial protection for those exposing internal Covenant violations.
*   **Decentralized Compliance:** Local self-governance is permitted provided it meets the Floor (Dignity) and Boundary (Integrity) standards.

---

### **IV. The Ethical Core (The Soul)**

*   **Veil of Ignorance Test:** The framework is designed to protect the person most at risk (the displaced worker, the future child, the river, the dissident).
*   **Resilience Over Dominance:** We prioritize the ability to survive shock over the ability to conquer rivals.
*   **Humility:** We admit we do not have perfect solutions. The framework is adaptive, subject to revision by the Citizen Assemblies and Future Councils.
