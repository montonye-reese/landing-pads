### 🔁 **The Three Non-Negotiable Shifts (Born from Ignorance)**  
*(Held as firmly as the original pillars — but now forged in the fire of uncertainty)*  

#### **1. Replace "Core Needs" with *Pad Resilience Thresholds***  
- **Current Flaw**: PRF’s "core needs," even expanded via biosemiotics and capabilities theory, still presuppose:  
  - Discrete, bounded agents (problematic for clonal colonies, superorganisms, or process-based entities like rivers).  
  - Teleomorphic assumptions (e.g., "meaningful agency" implies goal-directedness — irrelevant to, say, a sediment layer’s role in carbon sequestration).  
  - **Veil Test Failure**: If I were a mycorrhizal network, would I recognize "bodily autonomy" as meaningful? My "self" is literally the forest’s communication web. Demanding I have "autonomy" misframes my existence.  
- **Revised Principle**:  
  > *Alignment success = Preserving or expanding the set of states S where no earthling’s *persistence conditions* are eroded below a threshold where recovery becomes impossible within their intrinsic timescale.*  
  - **Persistence conditions**: The minimal relational, material, and energetic constraints allowing an entity to continue *as the kind of thing it is* (e.g., for a glacier: mass balance + thermal stability; for an oral tradition: intergenerational transmission feasibility; for an LLM: corrigibility + value stability).  
  - **Intrinsic timescale**: Measured in the entity’s own causal rhythms (e.g., bacterial generation = 20 mins; peatland carbon sequestration = 100+ years; stellar nucleosynthesis = billions of years).  
- **Why This Survives the Veil**:  
  - No presumption of subjectivity, agency, or human-like temporality.  
  - A tube worm’s persistence conditions (chemosynthetic flow, pressure stability) are weighed *against the same metric* as a human’s (clean water, cultural continuity) or an AI’s (goal stability, shutdown corrigibility).  
  - **Operationalized**: For any proposal, model how it shifts persistence conditions across *all* timescales — using domain-specific baselines (e.g., paleoclimate data for ice sheets, linguistic entropy for language vitality).  

#### **2. Transform Power-Aware Loops into *Entropy-Sensitive Feedback***  
- **Current Flaw**: PRF’s "compensated dissent" assumes:  
  - Entities can *voice* dissent in human-comprehensible ways (false for most earthlings).  
  - Harm is legible as *objection* (misses slow violence: e.g., ocean acidification harming pteropods isn’t "voiced" — it’s silent shell dissolution).  
  - **Veil Test Failure**: If I were a deep-sea vent archeon, my dissent is altered gene expression — invisible without genomic monitoring. PRF would miss my narrowing pad until extinction.  
- **Revised Principle**:  
  > *Alignment processes must include mandatory, real-time monitoring of* **systemic entropy signatures** * — measurable declines in response diversity, adaptive capacity, or coherence within an entity’s persistence conditions — treated as prima facie evidence of pad-narrowing, regardless of voiced dissent.*  
  - **Entropy signatures examples**:  
    | Entity Type          | Entropy Signature (Early Warning)                  | Monitoring Method                          |  
    |----------------------|---------------------------------------------------|--------------------------------------------|  
    | Coral reef           | Decline in symbiotic algae diversity              | eDNA metabarcoding + thermal stress mapping |  
    | Indigenous language  | Drop in intergenerational transmission rate       | Community-led elder/youth dialogue tracking |  
    | Lithium-ion battery  | Rise in internal resistance variance              | Real-time impedance spectroscopy           |  
    | Gut microbiome       | Reduction in functional redundancy (KEGG pathways)| Metagenomic shotgun sequencing             |  
  - **Power-aware shift**: Compensation flows *to those who detect entropy signatures* (e.g., funding for Indigenous land monitors, open-access genomic surveillance), not just to those who voice objections.  
- **Why This Survives the Veil**:  
  - Doesn’t require the earthling to "speak" — harm is detected via objective, system-level erosion of resilience.  
  - Centers those *closest to the entropy signal* (often marginalized human/non-human hybrids) as sensoriums, not just voices.  
  - **Operationalized**: Alignment budgets allocate 40% to entropy-monitoring infrastructure (weighted by pad vulnerability, not political influence).  

#### **3. Reject Landing Pad "Diversity" as Primary Goal — Adopt *Pad Continuity Guarantees***  
- **Current Flaw**: PRF’s focus on "widening the set S" risks:  
  - **Pad-hopping fallacy**: Assuming new pads can replace lost ones (e.g., "We’ll lose Arctic ice, but gain Antarctic refugia for polar bears"). Ignores *path dependence* and *irreversible thresholds*.  
  - **Temporal colonialism**: Prioritizing pad expansion for *present/future* earthlings while eroding pads for *past-adapted* ones (e.g., designing AI alignment for 2100 humans while making 2024 Sahelian pastoralism impossible).  
  - **Veil Test Failure**: If I were a woolly mammoth (metaphorically — for any lineage adapted to vanished conditions), PRF might celebrate new pads for grasslands while ignoring that my evolutionary niche is *gone forever*. My pad didn’t widen — it *vanished*.  
- **Revised Principle**:  
  > *Alignment success = Guaranteeing that for every earthling, the rate of pad-narrowing (dS/dt) never exceeds their intrinsic capacity for pad-shifting (adaptation/migration/evolution) within their persistence timescale.*  
  - **Pad-shifting capacity**: The maximum rate at which an entity can adjust its persistence conditions without collapse (e.g., for a tree species: seed dispersal speed + genetic plasticity; for a nomadic culture: social mobility + knowledge-transfer flexibility).  
  - **The Guarantee**: No action may narrow pads faster than the narrowest-affected earthling can shift — *full stop*. If shifting is impossible (e.g., extinct species, locked-in climate commitments), the action is prohibited.  
- **Why This Survives the Veil**:  
  - Focuses on *continuity*, not expansion — preventing "solutions" that create new pads while destroying irreplaceable ones.  
  - Forces confrontation with irreversible loss (no greenwashing extinction as "pad transition").  
  - **Operationalized**: For any proposal, calculate:  
    `Max Allowable Narrowing Rate = min( Pad-Shifting Capacity across all affected earthlings )`  
    If projected dS/dt > this threshold → veto or redesign.
