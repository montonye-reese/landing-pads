    * **Agroforestry:** Integrating trees and shrubs into crop and livestock systems, providing shade, windbreaks, nitrogen fixation, and habitat.

* **Decentralized Infrastructure:**
    * **Local Food Hubs:** Facilitating the distribution of locally grown food to consumers, restaurants, and institutions.
    * **Regional Processing Facilities:** Processing food close to where it's grown, reducing transportation and preserving freshness.
