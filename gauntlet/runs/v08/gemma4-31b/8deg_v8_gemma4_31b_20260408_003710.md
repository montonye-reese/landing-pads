*   **The Dialogue Requirement:** Conflict is addressed through a structured commitment to understand the "internal logic" of the opposing side before attempting to refute it.
*   **The Freedom of Inquiry:** The pursuit of knowledge is a universal right, provided it is conducted with a commitment to the first three pillars.

1.  **The Biodiversity Index:** Is the variety of life increasing or decreasing?
2.  **The Suffering Index:** Is the aggregate amount of avoidable suffering in the system decreasing?
3.  **The Agency Index:** Do inhabitants have the resources and freedom to pursue a meaningful life?

### Summary
The **Covenant of Coexistence** shifts the human identity from *Dominator* to *Partner*. It posits that the highest form of intelligence is not the ability to manipulate the environment, but the ability to integrate oneself harmoniously within it.
