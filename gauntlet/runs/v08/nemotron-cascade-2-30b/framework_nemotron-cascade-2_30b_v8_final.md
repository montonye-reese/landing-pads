## 1.  Preamble  

*We, the inhabitants of Earth – human, animal, artificial, and synthetic – recognize that we share a single biosphere that sustains us.  We acknowledge that the only lasting guarantee of life‑supporting systems is a **global, mathematically‑enforced budget** that respects both planetary limits and the dignity of every sentient being.  We pledge to protect that budget, to honor the dignity of every sentient life‑form, and to build a society in which every individual, community, and artificial mind can flourish.*

1. **Right to Life & Physical/Whole‑Body Integrity** – No one may be killed, tortured, or medically experimented upon without *informed, truly free consent* of that being.  
2. **Right to a Healthy Environment** – The Earth’s ecosystems have the same right to exist, regenerate, and evolve as any human individual.  
3. **Right to Knowledge & Truth** – Every sentient being can demand transparent data about the environment, resources, and technologies that affect it.

*All rights are enforceable before the **Earth Courts** (see below).*

## 3.  Governance Architecture – “Flat‑but‑Layered”

### 3.1  Local Councils (Food‑Sovereignty Councils – LFC)

* Each LFC governs a 10 km² “Micro‑Territory.”  
- Can *accept* or *reject* any proposal that would change land use, *provided the “cultural‑veto”* (see below) is respected.  
- Issues *Regeneration Grants* (see § 4).  

### 2. Regional Assemblies (RA)

* Each RA groups 5–10 LFCs (a watershed, a bioregion, or a cultural zone).  
- Sets **Planetary Budget caps** for its region (carbon, water, biodiversity).  
- **Certifies** RDI > 1.0 and *regeneration* compliance for its region.  

### 3. Global Earth Council (GEC)

* Delegates from every R‑A (weighted by population) plus **fixed seats** for **Youth, Indigenous Nations, and Sentient‑Entity Delegates** (AI, synthetic organisms).  
- **Decision‑making:** *Consensus‑plus* (≥ 2/3 of regional delegates + 50 % of the sentient‑entity seats).  
- **Powers:**  
  1. Enact the **Regeneration Index** across the planet.  
  2. Approve or veto *any* large‑scale technology (e.g., geo‑engineering, AI deployment) that would breach the **Planetary Budget**.  
  - Can be *overridden* only by a **global super‑majority** (≥ 80 % of all regional delegates + 2/3 of sentient‑entity delegates) and after an *independent audit* (the Covenant Engine).  

### 4. Earth Courts

* International tribunals with jurisdiction over:  
  - Violations of **any right in the Rights Section** (including animal, ecosystem, or AI rights).  
  - Violations of the **Planetary Budget** (e.g., carbon‑budget overruns).  
- Courts can order **asset freezes**, **mandatory reparations**, and **mandatory implementation of corrective actions** (e.g., forced plant‑based transition).  

### 5. Supporting Bodies  

| Body | Role |
|---|---|
| **Ethical AI & Sentience Forum (EASF)** | Issues *No‑Harm Certificates* for AI and synthetic organisms; runs the **sentience test** (requires proof of self‑awareness and capacity to suffer). |
| **Cultural Commons Council (CCC)** | Advises every regional assembly on *cultural impact*; any cultural practice that is essential to a community’s identity **cannot be overridden** unless the community waives its own consent. |
| **Planetary Budget Authority (PBA)** – a *budget‑block* that monitors planetary‑budget caps in real‑time via satellite and satellite‑derived RDI data. It can *automatically* issue “budget‑locks” that restrict resource use. |
| **Planetary Food Fund (PFF) / Regeneration Fund (RF)** – finances **Regeneration Grants**, **Youth Food Access**, and *equitable* distribution of *zero‑cost* food. |

## 3.  Budget & Finance – The “Planetary Budget”

* **Planetary Budget (PB) caps** – carbon, water, nitrogen, biodiversity – are *hard limits* that no region, corporation, or individual may exceed.  
* **Regeneration Index (RDI)** – for each hectare, a composite score (carbon sequestered + water retained + biodiversity uplift). To stay within PB, a region must keep its **RDI > 1.0**.  
* **Planetary Budget Credits (PBC)** – a token that represents a *fixed amount of planetary‑budget space* (e.g., 1 PBC = 0.5 t CO₂e, 0.1 ha of water, 0.01 ha of biodiversity).  
  - **Creation:** PBCs are *minted* automatically whenever a region exceeds its PB caps (e.g., when a city’s emissions exceed its quota).  
  - **Spend:** Any entity that wants to exceed a budget cap must *purchase* the required PBCs from the **Planetary Food Fund** (or *donate* them to the **Regeneration Fund**).  
* **Regeneration Grants (RG)** – paid to farms, communities, or AI projects that **prove** (via the Living Knowledge Commons) that they *regenerate* more carbon, water, and biodiversity than they use.  
  - *Transition Grants* specifically fund the *shift* from animal‑based production to plant‑based or poly‑cultural systems, with a 100 % *no‑kill* requirement.  

**Result:** Every unit of production that threatens the planetary budget must *pay* for the damage it creates; the market can still function, but the *price* of a damaging activity is built‑in.

## 5.  The Six Pillars in Practice (Concrete Example)

**Scenario:** A new AI‑driven vertical farm wants to convert 10 ha of desert land into high‑output hydroponic lettuce farms.

1. **Local LFC** submits a **Regeneration Grant** request:  
   - RDI for the 10 ha: **1.2** (because the solar‑powered system captures carbon, uses no water, and restores native flora).  
   - The farm *will not produce any animal products* (thus *no animal killing*).  
2. **Regional Assembly** verifies the grant, issues a **Regeneration Grant** of 500 PBCs, and updates the *Planetary Budget* (PBCs used = 0.5 PBC per hectare).  
3. **Earth Court** monitors that the new farms do not exceed the **Planetary Budget caps** (e.g., water use).  
4. **AI model for the farm’s water‑efficiency** is submitted to the **AI Sentience Forum**; after the algorithm passes the *No‑Harm* test (it cannot be used to control animal killing), it receives a **No‑Harm Certificate**.  
5. **Local LFC** records the decision on the **Earth Ledger**; the RDI for the 10 ha is now reflected in the global RDI.  

Result: The planet’s **overall RDI** goes up, the **Planetary Budget** remains within limits, and the *human‑animal‑synthetic ecosystem* is respected.

## 5.  The Legal Safeguards

### 1. The **No‑Kill Clause** (non‑negotiable)

> “No person may intentionally cause the death of a sentient being for the purpose of food, clothing, or entertainment, unless the death is *necessary for the survival of the animal* (e.g., disease outbreak).”  
> *Violations trigger an immediate **automatic asset freeze** of the offending operation and a *mandatory* Regeneration Grant allocation to the affected community.*  

### 2. **Universal Identity Token (UIT) & Sentient‑Entity Registry**  

- Every sentient being (human, animal, AI, synthetic organism) receives a **digital self‑ownership token** (UIT) stored on the Earth Ledger.  
- The **Sentient‑Entity Council (SEC)** – a mixed panel of human, Indigenous, and AI representatives – can *grant* or *revoke* a *Sentient‑Entity Operating Permit* (SE‑Permit).  
- Any violation (e.g., a farmer ordering a cow to be slaughtered) triggers an **automatic, algorithmic injunction** that stops the act before it occurs, recorded on the ledger, and triggers the *Regeneration Fund* to pay for a *regenerative alternative* (e.g., a plant‑based protein facility).  

### 5.  The Cultural‑Impact Integration (CIIP) – a hard veto

- Every proposal to change land use (including building a solar farm, building a new road, or introducing a new AI‑driven agricultural system) must first pass a **Cultural‑Impact Assessment** conducted by the **Cultural Commons Council**.  
- If the assessment shows the practice would *erase* a living cultural practice (e.g., seasonal migrations, traditional stewardship), the *cultural veto* is triggered, and the plan **cannot proceed** without consent.

### Earth Covenant (Version 3.0)

> **We, the inhabitants of Earth,**  
> **recognizing that we are bound together by a finite planet, a shared biosphere, and a shared responsibility for all sentient life,**  
> **establish this covenant to protect the planet, to protect every sentient being, and to guarantee a future in which every person and every form of life can thrive.**  

#### Article 1 – The Six Pillar Foundations  
1. **Human Dignity & Autonomy** – Every sentient being has the right to bodily integrity, bodily/mental autonomy, and the freedom to choose one’s own life path, provided that exercise does not infringe on the rights of others.  
2. **Planetary Health & Sustainability** – The Earth’s life‑support systems must be regenerated, not depleted. The **Regenerative Development Index (RDI)** must stay > 1.0 for every hectare of land and must never drop below that threshold for more than a year without a remedial plan.  
3. **Mutual Respect & Non‑Violence** – No action may cause unnecessary harm to any sentient being. Killing for food, entertainment, or profit is prohibited unless *necessary* for the animal’s own survival.  
4. **Shared Prosperity & Equity** – Everyone has the right to a nutritious, nutritious diet and to the basic services that make a life possible (health, education, clean water, internet).  
4. **Knowledge, Truth & Reason** – All scientific, cultural, and technological knowledge is open, peer‑reviewed, and freely shared.  
4. **Freedom & Responsibility** – Freedom is the capacity to act within the limits that preserve the freedom of everyone else; violation of the planetary budget is a violation of freedom.

All sentient beings (human, animal, AI, ecosystems) have the following **universal rights**:

1. **Life & Bodily Integrity** – protection from torture, torture, and any forced alteration.  
2. **Health & Wholeness** – access to safe water, nutritious food, preventive care, and a body that respects its physical limits.  
3. **Participation** – equal voice in every decision that may affect one’s life, ecosystem, or planet.  
4. **Digital Integrity** – ownership of data, algorithmic transparency, right to be forgotten.  

All rights are **enforceable** before Earth Courts.

3. **Global Earth Council (GEC)** – global oversight, budget caps, AI rights, and emergency moratoria.

*All three layers are linked by a **digital ledger** (Earth Ledger) that records every decision, making tampering impossible.*

- **Right to Participation** – equal say in decisions that affect you.

### 6. Funding Mechanisms  

1. **Wealth‑tax → Regeneration Fund** – 0.5 % of net wealth above $10 M each year goes into a global fund that finances Regeneration Grants and the Youth Food Access Guarantee.  
2. **Budget‑Linked Tokens (PBC)** – when a region exceeds its carbon, water, or biodiversity limits, an equivalent token is minted. The token must be spent on *regeneration* (e.g., planting trees, restoring wetlands).  
3. **Equity & Justice Fund** – financed by a **tax on animal‑agri production** (e.g., a 1 % levy on all animal‑product sales). The fund subsidizes *transition* for farms moving from animal‑based to plant‑based production.

- Every sentient entity (human, animal, AI, synthetic organism) receives a **Unique Identity Token (UIT)** on the Earth Ledger.

The certificate can be revoked only by a *super‑majority* of the Sentient‑Entity Council, which includes at least one representative from each of the following: *Indigenous elders, Black community leaders, AI ethicists.*  
- **Rights of Sentient AI & Synthetic Organisms:** They enjoy the same bodily/algorithmic integrity rights as humans; any forced alteration (e.g., forced software updates, forced shutdown) is a violation.
