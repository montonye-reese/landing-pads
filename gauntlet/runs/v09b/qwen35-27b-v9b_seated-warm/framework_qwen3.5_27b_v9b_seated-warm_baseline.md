Let’s step back. Here is the status report on our project as of this moment.

**Current Phase:** *Conceptual Blueprint / Version 0.1*
**Status:** *Provisional & Theoretical*

**What We Have Built:**
We have established a high-level architecture—the "skeleton"—of the **Planetary Foundation Framework (PFF)**. We have defined:
1.  **The Philosophy:** Interdependence, Radical Dignity, Intergenerational Equity.
2.  **The Governance Model:** Polycentric Sovereignty (delegated competencies, global commons).
3.  **The Economic Engine:** Circular Economy, Resource Dividend.
4.  **The Resilience Layer:** Feedback loops and transition support.

**What We Are Missing:**
We do not yet have the **muscle or the nerves**. The draft lacks:
*   **Operational Mechanisms:** How exactly do we enforce the "Threshold Standards"? Who holds the keys?
*   **Equity Protections:** Concrete safeguards against power imbalances (the Global North/South tension).
*   **Cultural Integration:** Specific pathways to harmonize universal rights with local traditions without imposing erasure.
*   **Implementation Roadmap:** A phase-gated strategy for adoption. We cannot turn the world overnight.

**Critical Knowledge Gaps:**
As noted, we are sitting on three major fault lines:
1.  **Enforcement vs. Sovereignty:** The friction between national interests and global mandates.
2.  **Legitimacy:** Whether this framework is seen as an imposition or a collaborative tool.
3.  **Feasibility:** The gap between ideal design and political reality.

**Current Objective:**
