Status Report: **Planetary Foundation Framework (PFF)**

**Status:** *Stress-Tested & Ethically Anchored*

Colleague, this is where we stand after the full journey. We are no longer building a blueprint for a perfect world. We are drafting a **survival guarantee** for a messy, competitive, and unequal one.

Here is the inventory of the framework, incorporating every pressure test, critique, and redesign we have run.

### 1. The Core Philosophy: Radical Vulnerability
*   **The Shift:** From "Planetary Interdependence" (saving the system) to **Individual Resilience** (saving the person).
*   **The Veil of Ignorance Test:** The framework is now designed to be accepted by the most vulnerable person alive.
*   **The Principle:** Safety over Speed. We prioritize preventing collapse for the individual over maximizing efficiency for the system.
*   **The North Star:** *No one must be made poor to save the planet.*

### 2. Governance: Pluralistic & Local
*   **The Structure:** **Polycentric Sovereignty** with a **Pluralistic Legitimacy** Council.
*   **The Stakeholders:** Nation-States, Indigenous Sovereigns, Faith Leaders, Informal Economy Representatives, and Non-Human Entities (Rivers, Forests as Legal Persons).
*   **The Decision Mechanism:** **Weighted Voting** (Prevents gridlock) with a **Local Veto** (Prevents imposition).
    *   *Local Veto:* Communities can pause global mandates that threaten their immediate survival until alternatives are found.
*   **The Safety Valve:** **National Security Exception.** Nations can temporarily suspend Moral Velocity in existential threats (to prevent total collapse), subject to strict sunset clauses.

### 3. Economics: The Security Floor
*   **The Base:** **Circular & Regenerative.** Linear extraction is taxed into obsolescence.
*   **The Guarantee:** **Universal Basic Security.** Decoupled from resource extraction. Food, shelter, energy, and health are constitutional rights, guaranteed even if the economy shrinks.
*   **The Wealth Distribution:** **Reparative Justice.** A portion of the Global Resource Dividend is pre-allocated as reparations to the Global South for historical extraction.
*   **The Transition:** **Just Transition Guarantees.** Workers in "Barbaric Industries" (Fossil, CAFO, Surveillance) get 100% wage replacement or guaranteed income during re-skilling. The cost falls on capital, not labor.

### 4. Geopolitics: The Compliance Bloc
*   **The Reality:** We do not expect global adoption. We build a **Compliance Bloc**.
*   **The Boundary:** **Green Tariffs.** Non-compliant goods face heavy taxes equal to their externalities (carbon, labor, privacy).
*   **The Defense:** **Strategic Autonomy.** The Bloc must be energy and tech self-sufficient to prevent coercion by non-compliant actors.
*   **The Tech Race:** We accept that non-compliant actors may move faster. We mitigate this by making the PFF Bloc the destination for capital and talent through **Stability Premiums**.

### 5. Transition: Dynamic Velocity
*   **The Pacing:** **Dynamic Calibration.** The "Velocity Council" adjusts the speed of transition based on real-time stress indicators (inflation, unrest, resource scarcity).
*   **The Rule:** If human suffering exceeds a threshold, the transition slows. We do not starve the present to feed the future.

### Summary of Trade-Offs
By incorporating the pressure tests, we have made specific sacrifices to ensure viability:

| Feature | Old Version (0.1) | Current Version (1.1) | The Cost |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Adoption** | Global Mandate | **Compliance Bloc** | Slower planetary coverage; higher trade barriers. |
| **Speed** | Maximize Resilience | **Calibrated Safety** | Slower climate mitigation; slower decarbonization. |
| **Power** | Consensus Veto | **Weighted + Local Veto** | Risk of Gridlock balanced by Risk of Imposition. |
| **Cost** | Resource Dividend | **Capitalized Transition** | Higher upfront taxes on capital; higher debt risk. |
| **Goal** | Save the Planet | **Save the People & Planet** | More expensive; harder to fund; harder to enforce. |

It is viable only if we accept that we cannot fix the climate without first fixing the economic fragility of the poor. If we try to move faster than the **Universal Basic Security** floor can hold, the framework will be voted out of power by the very people it is meant to save.
