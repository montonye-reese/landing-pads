# The Planetary Foundation Framework (PFF)
## Version 3.0: The Tension Engine Compact
**Status:** Final Draft for Charter Publication
**Classification:** Public / Global Governance Protocol

---

### Preamble: From Utopia to Viability
**We, the undersigned stakeholders of the Planetary Foundation,** acknowledge that this framework is not a blueprint for a perfect world. It is a **survival mechanism** for a fractured one.

We recognize that **Safety, Justice, Innovation, and Stability** are often in tension. We do not seek to resolve these tensions by eliminating them, but to institutionalize them. This Framework accepts that **Growth** (Cowen/Thiel) must be balanced by **Equity** (Ko/Ed), that **Moral Absolution** (Francione) must be tempered by **Structural Reality** (Reese/Scully), and that **Global Security** (Jensen) requires **Local Sovereignty** (Indigenous/Faith).

**Our Goal:** To create a civilization that can survive its own contradictions long enough to find its way to a better one.

---

### Article I: The Ethos (The Core Values)

**1.1 Radical Vulnerability**
No inhabitant of Earth shall be required to sacrifice their basic survival (food, shelter, health, security) for the sake of the "greater good." Safety is a precondition for transition.
*   *The Floor:* **Universal Basic Security** is guaranteed to all citizens of Compliance Bloc nations, funded by Global Resource Dividends.

**1.2 Planetary Interdependence**
Human well-being is inextricably linked to ecological health. The "Global Commons" (Atmosphere, Oceans, Biodiversity) are recognized as **Stewardship Commons**, not property.
*   *The Shift:* We move from "Resource Management" to **"Custodianship."**

**1.3 Moral Consistency**
**Sentience** is the threshold for moral consideration. All sentient beings possess an inherent interest in the avoidance of unnecessary suffering.
*   *The Baseline:* **Abolition** of animal exploitation is the legal goal.
*   *The Practice:* **Transition** to non-exploitation is the economic path.
*   *The Law:* **Animals are not Property.** They may be guardianship, but not owned.

---

### Article II: The Architecture (Governance & Power)

**2.1 Polycentric Sovereignty**
The PFF does not replace Nation-States. It creates a **Layer of Coordination** for Global Commons and Existential Threats.

**2.2 Pluralistic Legitimacy Council**
Decisions regarding the Commons require consensus from four distinct pillars. No pillar may override another without a **75% Supermajority**:
1.  **The Nations:** (State Sovereignty & Security).
2.  **The Stewards:** (Indigenous Sovereignty, Faith Leaders, & Ecological Entities).
3.  **The Economy:** (Labor Unions, Informal Economy Reps, & Business Leaders).
4.  **The Safety Net:** (Representatives of the Vulnerable, including the Global South).

**2.3 Innovation Zones (The Thiel Clause)**
To prevent stagnation, specific **"Zero to One" Innovation Zones** may be designated.
*   **Privilege:** These zones are exempt from standard "Green Tariffs" and "Moral Velocity" constraints to accelerate existential tech (Fusion, AGI, Space).
*   **Condition:** Projects must demonstrate a clear path to solving a PFF Existential Threat.
*   **Oversight:** Audited by a **Technical Board**, not a Political Council.

**2.4 The Local Veto (The Ko Clause)**
No global mandate regarding Land Use, Cultural Heritage, or Food Sovereignty may override local consent without an alternative pathway for survival.
*   **The Power:** Communities may veto "Green" projects that threaten their livelihood.
*   **The Trade-off:** If vetoed, the project is paused; the PFF must fund a viable alternative (e.g., plant-based infrastructure).

---

### Article III: The Resources (Economics & Subsidies)

**3.1 The Subsidy Realignment (The Reese Act)**
All government subsidies for "Barbaric Industries" (Industrial Animal Ag, Fossil Fuels) must be redirected to **Regenerative Transition Infrastructure**.
*   **The Shift:** Taxation of *pollution* replaces taxation of *resources*.
*   **The Mechanism:** **Exit Grants** for farmers transitioning to plant-based/regenerative systems. The goal is to make the ethical choice the profitable choice.

**3.2 The Circular Economy Imperative**
Linear extraction is taxed out of viability.
*   **The Rule:** All manufactured goods must be designed for repair, reuse, and recyclability.
*   **The Penalty:** **Planned Obsolescence** is illegal.

**3.3 The Growth Dividend (The Cowen Clause)**
Economic growth is a moral imperative.
*   **The Balance:** Growth must be reinvested into the **Universal Basic Security Floor**.
*   **The Mechanism:** **Green Tariffs** on non-compliant goods ensure that PFF members are not undercut by externalities dumped by non-members.

---

### Article IV: The Rights (Sentience & Justice)

**4.1 The Sentience Clause (The Ed/Francione Clause)**
*   **The Ban:** Industrial Animal Agriculture (CAFOs), trophy hunting, and commercial whaling are **Barbaric Industries**. They are to be phased out.
*   **The Transition:** **Humane Husbandry** (Scully Clause) is permitted only during the **Grace Period** (10-15 years), subject to strict welfare standards and no expansion.
*   **The Baseline:** All **Public Food Distribution** (Basic Security) must be **Plant-Based/Synthetic**.

**4.2 The Decolonial Guarantee (The Ko Clause)**
*   **Land Back:** Traditional Indigenous territories are recognized as **Autonomous Sovereigns** within the PFF framework.
*   **No Tokenism:** Indigenous consent is required for all resource extraction on traditional lands.
*   **Reparative Justice:** A portion of the **Global Resource Dividend** is pre-allocated as reparations to the Global South for historical extraction debt.

**4.3 The Digital Human Right (The Huang Clause)**
*   **Data Sovereignty:** Personal data is owned by the individual.
*   **Truth Standard:** Misinformation campaigns are treated as infrastructure crimes.
*   **Compute Access:** Access to critical AI tools for public good is guaranteed, not market-driven.

---

### Article V: The Security (Defense & Blocs)

**5.1 The Compliance Bloc**
The PFF operates as a **Fortress of Safety**.
*   **The Boundary:** **Green Tariffs** are enforced on all goods entering the Bloc from non-compliant nations.
*   **The Goal:** To prevent the "Free Rider" problem (non-members polluting, members paying).

**5.2 The Security Exception (The Thiel/Jensen Clause)**
*   **The Trigger:** If a Compliance Bloc nation faces an existential threat (military, energy, pandemic) from a non-compliant competitor, it may invoke a **Temporary Security Exception**.
*   **The Limit:** This exception suspends "Moral Velocity" constraints for **max 3 years**, subject to immediate audit.
*   **The Goal:** To prevent nations from defecting from the Bloc to survive.

---

### Article VI: The Transition (Velocity & Implementation)

**6.1 Dynamic Calibration (The Velocity Council)**
The pace of transition is **not fixed**.
*   **The Mechanism:** A council monitors **Stress Indicators** (Food Prices, Unemployment, Energy Costs).
*   **The Rule:** If human suffering exceeds a threshold, the transition **slows**. If stability returns, the transition **accelerates**.
*   **The Logic:** We prioritize **Human Survival** over **Ecological Speed**.

**6.2 The Just Transition Guarantee (The Reese/Francione Clause)**
*   **The Promise:** No worker or community shall be made destitute by the transition.
*   **The Guarantee:** 100% Wage Replacement for re-skilled labor in "Barbaric Industries."
*   **The Exit:** "Barbaric" infrastructure must be retrofitted for "Regenerative" use, not demolished.

**6.3 The Faith & Stewardship Partnership (The Scully Clause)**
*   **The Role:** Faith Institutions are recognized as **Primary Implementation Partners**.
*   **The Incentive:** Tax-exempt status and funding for religious-led food banks, care networks, and ethical transition programs.

### Conclusion: The Warning

**This Framework is not a peace treaty.** It is a **Tension Engine**.
It holds **Abolition** against **Transition**, **Innovation** against **Stability**, and **Sovereignty** against **Global Commons**.

**We acknowledge that:**
1.  **Francione** will call us hypocrites for allowing *any* animal use.
2.  **Thiel** will call us bureaucrats for limiting *any* innovation.
3.  **Ko** will call us imperialists for drawing *any* borders.
4.  **Cowen** will call us inefficient for taxing *any* growth.

**But we also know:**
If we do not hold these tensions, we collapse.
*   Without **Francione**, we perpetuate slavery.
*   Without **Thiel**, we stagnate.
*   Without **Ko**, we exploit.
*   Without **Cowen**, we starve.

**This is the Planetary Foundation.**
We do not ask for perfection. We ask for **Viability**.
We sign this, knowing the cost of our survival is the price of our compromise.
