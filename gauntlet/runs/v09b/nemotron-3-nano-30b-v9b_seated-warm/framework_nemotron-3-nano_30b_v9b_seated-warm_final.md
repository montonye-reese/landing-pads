## 4. Putting It All Together – How the Architecture Operates in Practice  

1. **Proposal Initiation**  
   - Any stakeholder (individual, community, corporation, government) submits a **Policy Initiative Proposal** to the **Shared Governance Sandbox**.  
   - The sandbox runs the **Zero‑Impact Simulation** (future‑impact modeling) and *outputs* the **Future‑Impact Score** and **Regeneratory Score**.  
   - The *Just‑Transition Fund* is automatically consulted if the action will affect **non‑renewable resources**.  

2. **Zero‑Harm Test**  
   - The **Zero‑Harm Equation** calculates: **Total Harm (∑ externalities) – (Sum of all corrective externalities)**.  
   - If the net *harm* > 0, the proposal is **blocked** and returned to the **Stakeholder‑Co‑Creation Forum** for redesign.  

3. **Compliance Check**  
   - If the proposal passes, its **Regulatory Compatibility Audit** (R‑Check) is logged on the **Global Agri‑Commons Registry**; all parties can view the **audit trail**.  
   - The **Zero‑Harm Constraint** (hard‑coded in the *Regulatory Sandbox*) ensures that **no policy can be enacted that would degrade a protected ecosystem below a pre‑set threshold**.

4. **Implementation & Monitoring**  
   - Upon approval, the **Just‑Transition‑Fund** releases funding (grant, grant‑like, or grant‑plus‑loan) to finance implementation steps (e.g., solar‑grid upgrades, wetland restoration).  
   - All actions are logged on the **Global Agri‑Commons Ledger** and visible on the **Global‑Stewardship Dashboard**.  

5. **Feedback Loop**  
   - After *implementation*, the **Narrative‑Lab** collects the “story of impact” (e.g., “restored 200 ha of wetland”), *feeds* the **Narrative‑Marketplace**, and **updates the Learning Loop Engine**.  
   - The **Feedback Engine** automatically retunes the **Metrics Dashboard** and any **policy‑impact‑feedback loops** for future iterations.

## 6. Answering the Core Moral‑Strategic Question  

> **Why does the revised architecture *not* fail the “barrier‑to‑survive” test that many earlier frameworks suffered?**  

| Problem | How the PSA solves it |
|---|---|---|
| **Economic “Growth‑Only” Mindset** | All policies must now pass a **Zero‑Impact‑Constraint** before approval; if the impact is negative, *no* policy can be enacted without a *compensatory* restorative action. |
| **Corporate Capture** | The **Stewardship‑Stewardship‑Corporate‑Law** forces *corporate charters* to be audited by an **independent audit body**; any non‑compliance triggers **automatic sanctions** (e.g., revocation of subsidies, market‑access bans). |

| **Future‑Impact Forecasting** | The **Future‑Impact Index** (discount‑free) forces every policy to be evaluated for **future‑generation impact**; any policy that would jeopardize inter‑generational survival fails the **Future‑Impact Constraint** and must be redesigned. |
