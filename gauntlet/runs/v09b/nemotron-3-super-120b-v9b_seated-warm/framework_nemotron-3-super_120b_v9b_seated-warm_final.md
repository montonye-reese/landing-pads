### 🔁 **1. Boundary-Inclusive Power-Sharing (BIPS) Trusts**  
*Replaces: FPIC+, youth councils, LEK consultation*  
**How it works**:  
- **Automatic revenue allocation**: ≥30% of *all* project revenue (energy, ag, mining, infrastructure) flows *immediately* to **community-controlled trusts** *before* groundbreaking—*no consultation needed*.  
- **Verification-triggered payouts**: Funds release *only* when LEK + satellite + blockchain-confirmed boundary metrics improve *in the same quarter*:  
  - *Ecological*: Soil nitrogen runoff ↓, water table ↑, NDVI (greenness) ↑, phylogenetic diversity stable.  
  - *Abolition Status*: Zero animal exploitation on land (verified via LEK/satellite: no livestock, no animal-derived inputs, no slaughter contracts).  
  - *Solvency*: Farmer operating income >0 (for ag) or cash flow positive (for industry).  
- **Defection velocity bonus**: Trusts pay *20% more* if boundary-healing action was first taken by an *individual* (e.g., lone-wolf water diversion, garage-born tech) *before* group consensus—verified via LEK timestamps + satellite anomaly detection.  
- **Gatekeeper proof**: Trusts publish quarterly ROI showing how payouts *lower present-side costs* (e.g., "BIPS zones had 40% lower flood damage claims in 2023; 22% lower veterinary costs from zoonotic outbreaks").  

**Why it works under pressure**:

- ✅ *Against abolitionists*: Zero payouts to animal exploiters—only to entities with verified zero animal exploitation.  

### 💰 **2. Boundary-Linked Present Dividends (BLPD)**  
*Replaces: Seventh-generation screening, FIA, intergenerational trust funds*  
**How it works**:  
- **Split into two non-negotiable streams** (funds flow *immediately* from avoided harm costs):  
  > **A. Solvency-Preserving Dividends (50%)**:  
  > - Funds redirected *from* avoided *near-term* harm costs (e.g., avoided vet bills from zoonotic outbreaks, avoided debt defaults from market volatility, avoided emergency flood response).  
  > - Paid as:  
  >   - **0% interest transition loans** (for barn conversions, equipment shifts)  
  >   - **Direct payments** for *transition-specific* costs (e.g., "Convert hog barn to mushroom farm: $X paid upon USDA verification of zero livestock")  
  >   - **Handler restoration** (trauma therapy for slaughterhouse workers, paid time off to tend gardens)  
  >   
  > **B. Ecological Healing Dividends (50%)**:  
  > - Funds redirected *from* avoided *long-term* harm costs (e.g., avoided soil restoration, avoided biodiversity loss).  
  > - Paid *only* when Solvency-Preserving needs are *met* (verified via LEK/satellite):  
  >   - Boundary metrics improve (soil N ↓, water table ↑, NDVI ↑)  
  >   - Zero animal exploitation maintained  
  >   - Operating income/cash flow >0  
  > - *No ecological payout if solvency is at risk.*  

**Why it works under pressure**:  
- ✅ *Against short-termism*: Makes equity *profitable today* (see: Ethiopia’s Productive Safety Net Program—cash-for-work on watershed restoration cut *current* famine risk while building capital).  
- ✅ *Against gatekeepers*: Shows *present*-side ROI (e.g., "BLPD solvency streams cut WI farmer distressed sales by 35% in 2023").  
- ✅ *Against abolitionists*: Zero payouts to animal exploiters—funds only flow to verified plant-based/cellular systems *after* solvency is met.  
- ✅ *Against tech maximalists*: Solvency stream funds immediate tech deployment (e.g., mobile AI units for real-time famine forecasting in drought zones).

*Replaces: Static thresholds, expert-driven FIA*  
**How it works**:  
- **Tracks 6 real-time boundary health metrics** (verified via LEK + satellite + blockchain—*no self-reporting*):

  | **Abolition Status (ASI)** | Rate of animal property dissolution | <2%/yr land transitioned to plant-based/cellular agriculture |

  | **Stewardship Virtue (SVI)** | Handler virtue & community mercy | >15% YoY drop in empathy scores or volunteer rescue rates |

  - New projects impacting that boundary *halt immediately* (no consultation needed).

    - **Virtue-nurturing resources** (handler therapy, community mercy projects)  
    - **Solvency-preserving support** (0% loans, direct transition payments)  
  - *Only after* PDI improves *and* metrics are verified do trusts resume normal payouts.  

**Why it works under pressure**:
