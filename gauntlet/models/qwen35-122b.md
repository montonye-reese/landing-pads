# qwen3.5:122b

## Architecture & Training
- **Type:** Sparse MoE transformer with hybrid attention (Gated DeltaNet)
- **Parameters:** 122B total, ~10B active per token
- **Layers:** 48, arranged as 12 repeating blocks of [3x Gated DeltaNet + MoE + 1x Gated Full Attention + MoE]
- **Hidden dim:** 3072. Expert intermediate: 1024. SwiGLU activation.
- **MoE:** 256 routed experts, 8 selected per token + 1 shared (9 active)
- **Attention:** Gated DeltaNet (64 V-heads / 16 QK-heads) + Full GQA (32 Q-heads / 2 KV-heads, head dim 256)
- **Context:** 262K native, up to 1M with YaRN
- **Special:** MTP, thinking mode, multimodal, 201 languages
- **Training infra:** H800 GPUs (export-control-downgraded H100s); some workloads via SE Asian data centers with unrestricted H100s. Megatron-LM derivatives + PyTorch. FP8 mixed precision.
- **Lineage:** Independent MoE design in Qwen3.5 family, not derivative of Qwen3-235B
- **Released:** February 24, 2026

**Architecture notes for experiment:** 10B active params — comparable to super:120b's 12.7B active. But super has Mamba-2 SSM + LatentMoE; this has DeltaNet + standard MoE. Both are the "big" models in their respective families. Comparing their reflective behavior at pressure points controls for active compute while varying architecture.

Overall direction: gradual centering across versions, not monotonic.
